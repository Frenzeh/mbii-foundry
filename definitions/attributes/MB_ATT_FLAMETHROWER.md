# 🔥 Flamethrower

`MB_ATT_FLAMETHROWER`

Wrist-mounted flamethrower for close-quarters combat.

> **Class Specific:** Mandalorian.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Damage:** Fire damage (ignores armor/block).
*   **Fuel:** Drains **400** units per second (`FLAMETHROWER_DEFUEL_RATE`).
*   **Effect:** Ignites enemies (DoT).

### 💡 Tips
*   Extremely effective against Jedi/Sith who get too close.
*   Consumes fuel rapidly.
