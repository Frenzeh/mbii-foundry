# ⚔️ Saber Max Chain

`MB_ATT_SABER_MAXCHAIN`

Sets the maximum number of swings in a lightsaber combo.

> **Default:** Varies by saber style (usually 3-5).

### 📊 Levels
*   **Level 1**: Max Chain **3**
*   **Level 2**: Max Chain **5**
*   **Level 3**: Max Chain **7**
*   **Level X**: Up to **10** (max in UI).

### 💡 Tips
*   Higher values allow for longer, more sustained offensive combos.
*   Styles like **Fast** (`SS_FAST`) benefit greatly from higher chain limits.
