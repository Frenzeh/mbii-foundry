# Dark Rage

`FP_RAGE`

> Fuel the dark side — faster swings, faster movement, more damage taken in return.

## What it does

Activates a self-buff that increases attack speed, movement speed and damage resistance for a duration. Drains FP continuously while active and leaves the user exhausted when it ends (reduced HP/stamina regen during the cooldown).

## Per level

- **Level 1** — short duration, high FP cost · *cost 80*
- **Level 2** — medium duration, moderate cost · *cost 70*
- **Level 3** — long duration, most efficient, immune to knockdown while raging · *cost 60*

## Notes

- Sith-only. Wookiee Fury is the non-Force equivalent.
- Rage ends with a debuff — you will be weak and slow for several seconds after.
- Standard opener for a rush/brawler Sith build.
