# Soldier

`MB_CLASS_SOLDIER`

> Imperial frontline grunt — cheap, fragile, numerous.

## Role

The Imperial team's basic rifle infantry. Low base health (50) and stock armor pool (5/15/25/35) make Soldiers expendable, but the point budget is wide enough to specialize into SMG skirmishers, scoped marksmen, or Close-Combat Training melee specialists. The cheap bucket of the roster — most Imperial comps run at least one.

## Default loadout

- **Base health** — 50
- **Base speed** — 187.5 (baseline infantry)
- **Armor pool** — `AP_5_15_25_35_M35` (5/15/25/35 ranks, 35 max)
- **Melee moves** — Punch only (`MM_PUNCH`)
- **Resource** — none
- **Abilities (CS1-4)** — none by default; FA specs commonly assign `EAS_HI_FURY`, `EAS_HI_QUICKTHROW`, or a grenade special
- **Forcecfg** — `forcecfg/blue/soldier`
- **Model** — `stormie`

## Signature mechanics

- `MB_ATT_CCTRAINING` (Close-Combat Training) is the Soldier-identity attribute: Rank 1 = 1.2x melee speed, Rank 2 = 1.2x melee + 1.1x gun speed + improved getup, Rank 3 = 1.2x all-around movement.
- Low HP pool rewards investing in armor ranks over raw health.

## Counters

- Melee classes — no innate defensive flags; 50 HP loses any straight trade.
- Jedi — saberthrow and Force powers chew through the thin armor pool.
- Wookiees with Strength 3 ignore knockback and shove through Soldier lines.
