# Wrist Flamethrower

`MB_ATT_WPFLAMETHROWER`

> Mandalorian wrist-mounted flamethrower. Short-range stream weapon for close encounters.

## What it does

Adds the Mandalorian wrist flamethrower attachment — a short-range continuous flame stream fired from the gauntlet, useful for breaking up Jedi rushes and clearing tight choke routes. Drains fuel from the Mandalorian fuel pool.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Basic flame stream, short range.
- **Level 2** — Longer range, slightly more damage per tick.
- **Level 3** — Maximum range and tick damage; widest cone.

## Notes

- Pairs conceptually with `WP_THROWER` (the broader thrower family).
- Shares fuel with jetpack on Mandalorian classes — manage usage.
- Excellent vs Jedi and Sith who close to melee.
