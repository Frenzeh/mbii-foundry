# Big Bacta Charges

`MB_ATT_BACTA_BIG`

> Heavy bacta-injection inventory.

## What it does

Sets the count and effectiveness of `HI_MEDPAC_BIG` charges. Larger heal per use than `MB_ATT_BACTA`, designed for clutch survival situations.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — 1 charge, baseline heal.
- **Level 2** — 2 charges or stronger heal.
- **Level 3** — full inventory, fastest application.

## Notes

- Sibling of `MB_ATT_BACTA` (standard medpac).
- Animation lockout is the same as standard bacta — plan around the commit time.
- Sith Drain can interrupt mid-heal — beware.
