# Jetpack

`MB_ATT_JETPACK`

Grants the ability to fly. Requires **Fuel** (`MB_ATT_FUEL`) to operate.

> **Default:** Disabled.

### Levels

* **Level 1**: **Enabled**.
 * **Double-Jump** to activate.
 * **Hold Jump** to ascend.
 * **Crouch** to descend (while flying) or hover.
 * Consumes Fuel while active.

### Tips
* **Flamethrower** and **Wrist Rocket** can be used while flying.
* **Knockdown effects** (Rocket explosions, Force Push) can disable the jetpack temporarily ("Jetpack Down").
* Fuel regenerates slowly when on the ground (unless disabled by class flags).
