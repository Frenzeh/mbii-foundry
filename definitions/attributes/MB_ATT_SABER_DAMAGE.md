# ⚔️ Saber Damage

`MB_ATT_SABER_DAMAGE`

Increases the base damage of lightsaber swings.

> **Default:** 0 (Uses .sab file values).

### 📊 Levels
*   **Level 1**: Adds **+5** damage per swing.
*   **Level 2**: Adds **+10** damage per swing.
*   **Level 3**: Adds **+15** damage per swing.

### 💡 Tips
*   This attribute is directly added to the base damage defined in the `.sab` file.
*   Often used by Sith "Juggernaut" builds to ensure high damage output across all saber styles.
