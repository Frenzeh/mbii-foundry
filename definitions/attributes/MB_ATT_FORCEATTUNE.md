# Force Attunement

`MB_ATT_FORCEATTUNE`

> Reduces Force Point cost of all Force powers.

## What it does

Discount on Force usage — multiplies the FP cost of every active power downward. Lets saberists rotate through powers more aggressively without bottoming out.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small discount.
- **Level 2** — medium discount.
- **Level 3** — large discount; sustainable continuous casting.

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_FORCEFOCUS` (cast speed) for full caster builds.
- Doesn't affect non-Force resources (Energy, Stamina, Battery).
