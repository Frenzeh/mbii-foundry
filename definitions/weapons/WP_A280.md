# A280 Rifle

`WP_A280`

> Rebel Alliance heavy blaster rifle. Hard-hitting, high velocity, slower cadence than an E-11.

## What it does

The A280 is the Rebellion's go-to long-arm: the per-bolt damage and projectile velocity are both higher than the standard E-11, but the primary fire rate is slower (285ms refire). Mode-cycle (`swapMode`) lets the operator switch between rapid primary fire and a long burst/precision secondary unlocked at MBF level 1. Useful in mid-to-long lanes where every shot matters more than spray volume.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 35
- **Velocity** — 8075 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 285ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Burst / scoped marksman shot (unlocked at level 1)
- **Velocity** — 1250ms cycle, 20 energy
- **Notes** — `ZOOM_PROJ` scope; level 3 unlocks the full sniper-style alt

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_A280`.
- Higher velocity than E-11 (8075 vs 6325) makes it forgiving on moving targets.
- Held by Rebel Soldier loadouts; well-suited to Soldier and Hero kits.
