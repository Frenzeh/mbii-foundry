# Stun Baton

`MB_ATT_STUN_BATON`

> Electrified melee club. Short reach, mini-stun on hit.

## What it does

Grants `WP_STUN_BATON` — a 25-damage electric melee weapon that applies a brief stun on hit. Primary swings at 400ms; heavy secondary at 1000ms. At level 3+ it adds a shock-pulse effect on hit (`swapEnable: 3`).

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Basic baton; 25 damage, no shock pulse.
- **Level 2** — Improved swing speed.
- **Level 3** — Shock pulse on hit (electric AoE on contact).

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_STUN_BATON`.
- Short reach — close-quarters only.
- Counts as melee (`MBF_MELEE`) for block/dodge interactions.
- Common on Prison Guard, Imperial Officer, Riot Trooper kits.
