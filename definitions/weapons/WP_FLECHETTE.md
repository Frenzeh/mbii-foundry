# Golan Arms Flechette Launcher

`WP_FLECHETTE`

> Shotgun-style scattergun primary; bouncing explosive mine secondary.

## What it does

The Flechette is a hybrid shotgun/mine-layer. Primary fires a tight cone of pellets (`isShotgun: qtrue`) that shreds at close range; secondary launches a pair of bouncing explosive mines that detonate on proximity or after a short fuse — useful for blocking corridors or fishing for kills around corners.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Pellet shotgun spread
- **Ammo cost** — 5
- **Fire rate** — 700ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Bouncing explosive mines
- **Fire rate** — 850ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_FLECHETTE`.
- Crosshair displays the shotgun reticle (`isShotgun` flag).
- Mercenary, Mandalorian, and Bounty Hunter loadouts.
