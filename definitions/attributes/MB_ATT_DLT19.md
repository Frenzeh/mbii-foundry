# 🔫 DLT-19 Heavy Blaster Rifle

`MB_ATT_DLT19`

A heavy repeating blaster rifle with a high rate of fire.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Primary Damage:** **26**
*   **Secondary Damage:** **35** (Explosive Shot)
*   **Primary Velocity:** 4500
*   **Secondary Velocity:** 9500
*   **Primary Ammo Cost:** 4
*   **Secondary Ammo Cost:** 10
*   **Clip Size:** 60.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Standard**. Rapid fire primary.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: Explosive shot.
*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Accuracy**: Improved.
*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Fire Rate**: Faster.

### 💡 Tips
*   Excellent for suppressing large groups of enemies.
*   The secondary fire can clear out entrenched positions.
