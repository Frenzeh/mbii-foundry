# 🔥 Fire Grenade

`MB_ATT_FIRE_GRENADES`

Incendiary grenade that creates a burning area of effect.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Direct Hit Damage:** **15**
*   **Splash Damage:** **15**
*   **Splash Radius:** **200** units
*   **Burn Time:** ~13 seconds (Primary) / 8 seconds (Secondary)
*   **Burn Damage:** ~2.22 per tick (every 30ms).

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Single Grenade**. Creates a small fire zone.
*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Two Grenades**.
*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Three Grenades**.

### 💡 Tips
*   Good for area denial and flushing out enemies.
*   Deals damage over time, bypassing some armor.
