# EE-3 Carbine

`WP_EE3`

> Boba Fett's signature carbine. Three-round burst primary; rapid secondary.

## What it does

The EE-3 fires a tight three-round burst per primary trigger pull (1100ms between bursts at level 1, scaling down to 800ms at level 4). Secondary is a rapid single-fire mode at 275ms cadence, useful for closer engagements where you want sustained pressure rather than burst spikes.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 33 per bolt
- **Velocity** — 6750 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 8 (per burst)
- **Fire rate** — 1100ms (level 1) → 800ms (level 4)

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Rapid single fire
- **Damage** — 33
- **Velocity** — 7000 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 275ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_EE3`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 10/9 primary, 13/8 secondary; blocking 10/7 / 12/6.
- `ZOOM_ADS` aim-down-sights on both modes; mode-cycle (swap at level ≥1).
- Iconic Bounty Hunter / Mandalorian carbine.
