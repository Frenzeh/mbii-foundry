# 🔮 Force Focus (Attunement)

`MB_ATT_FORCEFOCUS`

Increases the maximum size of the Force Pool.

> **Default:** 100 FP.

### 📊 Levels
*   **Rank 1:** **90 FP** Total. (Wait, rank 1 is less than default? Check `FP_ATTUNEMENT_POOL_1`).
*   *Correction:* `FP_ATTUNEMENT_POOL_0` is 80.
*   **Rank 1:** **90 FP**.
*   **Rank 2:** **100 FP**.
*   **Rank 3:** **120 FP**.

### 💡 Tips
*   Allows for more ability usage before exhaustion.
*   Vital for Force-heavy builds (Consular/Sorcerer).
