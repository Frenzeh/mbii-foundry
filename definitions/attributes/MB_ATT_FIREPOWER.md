# SBD Arm Blaster

`MB_ATT_FIREPOWER`

The integrated wrist blaster of the Super Battle Droid.

> **Class Specific:** SBD.

### Stats
* **Damage:** **34**
* **Velocity:** 4600
* **Ammo:** Drains Battery.

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Fire Rate:** Standard.
 * **Accuracy:** Moderate.

* **Level 2**:
 * **Fire Rate:** Increased.
 * **Battery:** More efficient usage.

* **Level 3**:
 * **Fire Rate:** **Rapid Fire**.
 * **Damage:** Consistent high DPS.
 * **Battery:** Highly efficient.

### Tips
* Your life depends on managing your Battery. Don't spray and pray unless you have Level 3.
