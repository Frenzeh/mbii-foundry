# Amban Phase-Pulse Rifle

`WP_AMBAN`

> Mandalorian phase-pulse rifle. Charge-up disintegrator with a pulse-spam alt-fire.

## What it does

The Amban is a long charge-time precision rifle with two clearly different attacks. Primary is a low-damage rapid pulse used to chip and harass; secondary is a heavy charged shot that disintegrates kills (gibs the corpse) at full charge. Charges scale with rank, hits hard at range, but consumes a long reload window after a fully charged shot.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 20 (per pulse)
- **Velocity** — 9825 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 1 (pulse mode)
- **Fire rate** — 500ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Charged disintegrator shot (`CHGE_AMBAN`)
- **Damage** — ~80 to 143+ depending on charge level
- **Effect** — Disintegrates target on lethal hit; `ZOOM_ADJUST` scope with charge zoom

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_AMBAN`.
- Reload speed 1500ms — committing to a charged shot leaves you exposed.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 40/30 base; blocking 30/24 base.
- Iconic Mandalorian Bounty Hunter weapon.
