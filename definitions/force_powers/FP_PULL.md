# Force Pull

`FP_PULL`

> Yank enemies toward you, strip their weapons, or drag them off a ledge.

## What it does

Fires a kinetic cone forward like Push, but reverses the vector. Pulls enemies toward the user, drags their weapons out of their hands, and at high ranks can knock them prone on arrival. Can also pull dropped items off the ground.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — short-range single-target pull · *cost 20*
- **Level 2** — wider cone, reliable disarm · *cost 20*
- **Level 3** — widest cone, strongest yank, best disarm chance · *cost 20*

## Notes

- Countered by Absorb, just like Push.
- Classic pair with saber throw or melee — yank, then swing.
- Pulls enemies off ledges into pits; use it on bridge maps.
