# Flashes

`HELD_FLASH`

> Flashbang-style blind on hit.

## What it does

Hits briefly blind the target (white-out / vision occlusion), simulating a flashbang. The target's screen goes white for a short window.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Distinct from `HELD_SONIC` (audio stun) — this is a vision blind.
- Useful for "concussion" weapon concepts.
