# Miraluka Vision

`MB_ATT_FP_MIRALUKA`

> Passive replacement for normal sight — permanent Force Sense flavour.

## What it does

A niche Miraluka-specific attribute. Provides a passive version of Force Sense's radar/see-through-walls effect without needing to activate `FP_SEE`. Requires `FP_SEE` to be granted on the class to take effect.

## Notes

- Character-flavour attribute — Miraluka are canonically blind and "see" via the Force.
- Class-restricted; not part of the standard duel/FA force kit.
