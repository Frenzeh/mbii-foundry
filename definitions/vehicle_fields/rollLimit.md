# Roll Limit

`rollLimit`

> Maximum angle the vehicle can roll (tilt sideways).

## What it does

Maximum angle the vehicle can roll (tilt sideways).

**Default:** ~45 degrees.

## Valid values

- **Value:** Degrees.
- **Effect:** Limits how much the vehicle leans when turning.

## Notes

- Set to **0** for stable platforms (Skiffs, Tanks) that shouldn't lean.
