# Dual Style

`SS_DUAL`

Dual-wielding lightsaber style (two sabers).

### Characteristics
* **Speed**: Fast.
* **Offense**: High damage output (two blades hitting).
* **Defense**: Smaller block box than Staff, but good against blasters.
* **Special**: Can throw one saber while keeping the other.

### Use Cases
* Overwhelming offense and versatility.
