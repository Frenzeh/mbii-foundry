# Block Points Drop

`MB_ATT_DROP_BLOCK`

> Drops a one-shot BP pickup on the ground.

## What it does

Spawns a Block Points pickup at the user's feet for ally saberists to grab mid-combat.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small BP per pickup.
- **Level 2** — medium BP per pickup.
- **Level 3** — large BP per pickup.

## Notes

- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DISP_BLOCK` and `MB_ATT_STIM_BLOCK`.
- Charge count tied to `MB_ATT_SUPPLYDROP`.
- Niche — only saberists benefit.
