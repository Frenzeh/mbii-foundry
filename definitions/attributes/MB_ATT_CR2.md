# 🔫 CR-2 Repeating Pistol

`MB_ATT_CR2`

Rapid-fire repeating blaster pistol.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Damage:** **25** (Primary & Secondary)
*   **Velocity:** 4000
*   **Ammo Cost:** 2 (Primary & Secondary)

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Standard**. High fire rate.
*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Accuracy**: Improved.
*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Recoil**: Reduced.
    *   **Fire Rate**: Maximum.

### 💡 Tips
*   "Bullet hose" weapon. Good for suppression and close-quarters.
*   Can quickly drain an opponent's Block Points.
