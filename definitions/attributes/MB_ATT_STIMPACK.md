# Combat Stimpack

`MB_ATT_STIMPACK`

> Self-buff combat stim consumable.

## What it does

Inventory item that, on use, triggers a short combat-stim effect on the user (typically speed, resistance, or HP burst depending on configuration). Mapped to the `HI_STIMPACK` holdable.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — 1 charge, baseline buff window.
- **Level 2** — 2 charges or stronger effect.
- **Level 3** — 3+ charges, fastest application.

## Notes

- Buff is a temporary timer, not an instant heal.
- Brief animation lockout; safer mid-push than a full medpac.
- Sibling of `HI_STIMPACK` holdable.
