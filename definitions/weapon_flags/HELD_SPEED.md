# 🏃 Fast User

`HELD_SPEED`

Lightweight weapon design enhances user mobility.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Speed:** **1.15x** (15% Faster movement).
*   **Condition:** Active only while holding the weapon.

### 💡 Tips
*   **Replaces:** `CFL_RUNFASTMELEE` (which was melee-only).
*   **Usage:** Knives, Pistols, "Adrenaline" items. Gives players a reason to switch to their sidearm for traversal.
