# Mouse Yaw

`mouseYaw`

> Sensitivity of turning left/right with the mouse.

## What it does

Sensitivity of turning left/right with the mouse.

**Default:** 0.003 (approx)

## Valid values

- **Higher:** Turns faster/snappier.
- **Lower:** Turns slower/heavier.

## Notes

- Crucial for balancing turret turn rates.
