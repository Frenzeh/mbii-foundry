# Concussion Grenade

`WP_CONC_NADE`

> Compact concussion grenade. Smaller blast than a standard frag, but timed for quick area control.

## What it does

A small-radius timed grenade in the "micro grenade" family. Lower damage than a thermal but throws fast and lets you pre-empt routes with minimal commitment. Both fire modes use a `CHGE_TIMED` cook so you can short-throw or long-throw.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Throw (timed cook)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Lobbed throw (timed cook)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_MICRO_GRENADES`.
- Smaller blast than `WP_THERMAL` / `WP_FRAG_NADE` — pick this when you need volume over punch.
