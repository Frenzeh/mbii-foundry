# ❤️ Max Health

`maxhealth`

Sets the character's base maximum HP.

> **Default:** Varies by class (Soldier: 100, Wookiee: 400+, etc.)

### 💡 Tips
*   This is the "naked" HP before any `MB_ATT_HEALTH` or `MB_ATT_WOOKIE_HEALTH` bonuses are applied.
*   For **SBDs** and **Droidekas**, this is their Hull integrity.
