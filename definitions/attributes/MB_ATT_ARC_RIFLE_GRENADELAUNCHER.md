# 💣 ARC Grenade Launcher

`MB_ATT_ARC_RIFLE_GRENADELAUNCHER`

Underslung grenade launcher for the Westar M5.

> **Requirement:** `MB_ATT_WESTARM5`.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Ammo Cost:** Uses shared M5 ammo or separate pool? (Usually separate EMP grenades).
*   **Damage:** Explosive.

### 📊 Levels
*   **Level 1:**
    *   **Capacity**: ~3-6 shots.
    *   **Type**: EMP / Explosive mix.
*   **Level 2:**
    *   **Capacity**: Increased.

### 💡 Tips
*   Right-click to toggle between Rifle and Launcher mode.
*   Conflict: Cannot be equipped with `MB_ATT_ARC_RIFLE_SCOPE`. You must choose Scope OR Launcher.
