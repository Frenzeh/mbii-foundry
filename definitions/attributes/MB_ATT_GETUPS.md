# Getup Speed

`MB_ATT_GETUPS`

> Speed of recovery from knockdowns.

## What it does

Controls how quickly the user gets back up after being knocked down. Lower ranks leave you on the ground vulnerable; higher ranks let you spring up almost instantly and even attack out of recovery.

## Per level

- **Level 0** — standard slow getup.
- **Level 1** — faster getup animation.
- **Level 2** — near-instant recovery.
- **Level 3** — can shoot/melee immediately on getup.

## Notes

- Stacks with `MB_ATT_KNOCKDOWN_ROLL` (rolls) for full knockdown immunity-equivalent.
- Crucial for aggressive classes that take frequent CC.
- Distinct from Strength immunity — this is reaction speed, not damage reduction.
