# Force Absorb

`MB_ATT_FP_ABSORB`

Absorbs hostile Force powers and converts them into Force Points.

> **Default:** 0 FP Drain (Cannot absorb).

### Levels
* **Level 1**: **10 FP** per absorption. Absorbs Push, Pull. Small FP returned.
* **Level 2**: **15 FP** per absorption. Absorbs Grip (Level 1/2), Lightning (Level 1/2). Medium FP returned.
* **Level 3**: **20 FP** per absorption. Absorbs almost all Force powers. High FP returned.

### Tips
* **Anti-Sith:** The hard counter to Force-heavy Sith.
* **Active:** Must be activated to work (unlike Force Block which is passive).
