# Clone Pistol

`MB_ATT_CLONE_PISTOL`

Standard DC-17 Blaster Pistol of the Clone Troopers.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Primary Damage:** **36**
* **Primary Fire Rate:** 500ms
* **Primary Velocity:** 6325
* **Primary Ammo Cost:** 2
* **Secondary Fire Rate:** 900ms (Auto-fire)
* **Secondary Ammo Cost:** 3
* **Clip Size:** 20 (Reloads after 20 shots).

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Standard**. Single-shot primary.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Rapid-fire burst.
* **Level 2**:
 * **Accuracy**: Improved.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Recoil**: Reduced.
 * **Spread**: Tighter spread.

### Tips
* Good for suppressing enemies.
* Can be dual-wielded.
