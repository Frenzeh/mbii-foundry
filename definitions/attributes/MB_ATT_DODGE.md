# 🥷 Dodge

`MB_ATT_DODGE`

Passive chance to completely evade incoming projectile damage.

> **Default:** Disabled.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Rank 1:**
    *   **Chance:** Low.
    *   **Effect:** Evades sniper shots and projectile attacks.
*   **Rank 2:**
    *   **Chance:** Medium.
*   **Rank 3:**
    *   **Chance:** High.
    *   **Effect:** Can dodge blaster bolts from automatic weapons.

### 💡 Tips
*   Used on agile characters like Heroes or Mandalorians.
*   Can be activated manually via `EAS_HI_DODGE` for a guaranteed dodge (at a cost).
