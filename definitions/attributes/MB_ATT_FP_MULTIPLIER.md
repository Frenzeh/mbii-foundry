# Force Pool Multiplier

`MB_ATT_FP_MULTIPLIER`

> Passive multiplier on maximum Force Point pool and regen.

## What it does

Scales the character's maximum FP pool (and indirectly their regen ceiling). Used to build Force-heavy or Force-light characters at the class/FA level. Does not affect cost of individual powers.

## Notes

- Combines with the `forcepool` field to determine total FP.
- Separate from `FP_ATTUNEMENT`, which operates on the player-skill side.
- Every Force-centric class benefits from a higher multiplier.
