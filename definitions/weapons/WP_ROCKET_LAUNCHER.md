# Merr-Sonn Missile System

`WP_ROCKET_LAUNCHER`

> Portable rocket launcher. Dumb-fire primary; homing/guided secondary.

## What it does

The Merr-Sonn is the all-purpose rocket launcher: primary launches a fast unguided rocket at 900ms refire; secondary uses `CHGE_HOMING` for a tracking/guided rocket that locks on after a brief charge. Excellent against vehicles, SBDs, and grouped infantry — dangerous to use in cramped quarters.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Dumb-fire rocket
- **Ammo cost** — 1
- **Fire rate** — 900ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Homing rocket (`CHGE_HOMING`)
- **Ammo cost** — 1
- **Fire rate** — 1200ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_ROCKET_LAUNCHER` (also `MB_ATT_ROCKET` for ammo upgrades).
- Reload speed 250ms but small clip (2-8 across ranks) — manage your shots.
- Anti-vehicle, anti-SBD, and crowd-clearing role.
