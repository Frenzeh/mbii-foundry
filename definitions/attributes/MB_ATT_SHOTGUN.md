# 🔫 Shotgun

`MB_ATT_SHOTGUN`

Close-range scattergun, effective in tight spaces.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Pellet Damage:** **23** (5 pellets = **115 total**)
*   **Splash Damage:** 0 (No splash)
*   **Velocity:** 6325
*   **Ammo Cost:** 5 (Primary) / 12 (Alt-Fire)
*   **Spread:** High at range.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Standard**. Fires a spread of pellets.
*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Damage**: Increased.
*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Spread**: Tighter spread, better range.

### 💡 Tips
*   Devastating at close range.
*   Can be used to clear rooms quickly.
