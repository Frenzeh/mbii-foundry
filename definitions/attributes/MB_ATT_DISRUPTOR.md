# Tenloss Disruptor

`MB_ATT_DISRUPTOR`

Illegal sniper rifle that disintegrates targets.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Primary Damage:** **31**
* **Sniper Damage:** **36** (Base) -> Charges up to **125+**
* **Velocity:** Instant (Hitscan) when scoped? No, extremely fast (Velocity 10000+).
* **Ammo Cost:** 5 per shot.

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Scope:** Usable.
 * **Charge:** Slow charge.

* **Level 2**:
 * **Charge:** Faster charge rate.
 * **Efficiency:** Less ammo per shot?

* **Level 3**:
 * **Master**. Fastest charge.
 * **Penetration:** Can shoot through multiple enemies.

### Tips
* A fully charged headshot dissolves the enemy (no revive).
* Body shots on unarmored targets are often lethal.
* Ignores most armor damage reduction? (Needs verification).
