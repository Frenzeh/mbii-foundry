# Ammo Pack

`MB_ATT_AMMO_PACK`

> Self-use ammo refill consumable.

## What it does

Lets the user refill their own ammo from a personal Ammo Pack inventory item. Distinct from the dispenser/drop family — this is a self-use, not a team-share.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small refill per pack.
- **Level 2** — medium refill per pack.
- **Level 3** — full refill per pack.

## Notes

- Charges are personal; cannot be transferred to teammates.
- Useful on solo-flanker classes that can't rely on a medic for top-ups.
- Sibling of `MB_ATT_MEDI_PACK` (self-heal pack).
