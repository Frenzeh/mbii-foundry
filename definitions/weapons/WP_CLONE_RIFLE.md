# DC-15A Clone Rifle

`WP_CLONE_RIFLE`

> Clone Trooper standard-issue rifle. Fast cadence with an ion/concussion blob secondary.

## What it does

The DC-15A is a high-rate-of-fire rifle that trades raw per-bolt damage for sustained pressure. Refire drops sharply at higher ranks (down to 100ms at level 4). The secondary launches a concussive/ion blob that's effective vs droids and shielded targets.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 24
- **Velocity** — 4600 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 1
- **Fire rate** — 190ms (level 1) → 100ms (level 4)

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Ion/concussion blob (`CHGE_BLOB`)
- **Fire rate** — 850ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_CLONERIFLE`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 9/6 primary; blocking 8/4.
- High clip size (75-125 across ranks) — sustained fire weapon.
- Excellent vs Droideka shields when using the blob alt.
