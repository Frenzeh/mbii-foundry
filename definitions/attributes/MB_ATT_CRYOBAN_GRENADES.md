# Cryoban Grenades

`MB_ATT_CRYOBAN_GRENADES`

> Freezing grenade attribute. Slows or briefly freezes targets in the blast zone.

## What it does

Grants `WP_CRYO_NADE`. Timed-cook grenade that drops a brief AoE that slows or freezes hit targets. Useful for setting up follow-up shots or denying routes when paired with a teammate's heavier weapon.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — One cryo grenade.
- **Level 2** — Two cryo grenades.
- **Level 3** — Three cryo grenades; longer freeze/slow duration.

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_CRYO_NADE`.
- Crowd-control role — chains well with frag/thermal follow-ups.
- Most valuable vs aggressive Force users and runners.
