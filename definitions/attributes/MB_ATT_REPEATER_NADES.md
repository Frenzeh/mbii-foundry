# Repeater Concussion Grenades

`MB_ATT_REPEATER_NADES`

> Concussion grenades fired from the Heavy Repeater alt-fire.

## What it does

Adds the concussion grenade alt-fire ammunition pool to `WP_REPEATER`. Without this, the repeater is primary-only spam; with it, the secondary launcher fires a concussion-grenade-style bolt for area damage.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Two repeater grenades.
- **Level 2** — Three repeater grenades.
- **Level 3** — Four+ grenades; faster reload.

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_REPEATER` (the base weapon).
- Used as the secondary fire mode of the Heavy Repeater.
- Heavy Trooper, Galactic Marine, Imperial Heavy Soldier kits.
