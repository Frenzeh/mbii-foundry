# Binoculars

`MB_ATT_BINOCULARS`

> Configures the personal Electrobinoculars optic.

## What it does

Determines zoom range and FoV for the `HI_BINOCULARS` holdable. Higher ranks unlock higher zoom levels for long-range scouting.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — basic 2x zoom.
- **Level 2** — multi-step zoom (2x/4x).
- **Level 3** — full sniper-grade zoom range.

## Notes

- Sibling of holdable `HI_BINOCULARS`.
- Cannot fire weapons while zoomed.
- Quickscope synergy with Projectile Rifle — readies instantly when exiting binocs view.
