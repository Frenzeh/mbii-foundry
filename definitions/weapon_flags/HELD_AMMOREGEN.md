# Passive Ammo Regen

`HELD_AMMOREGEN`

> Weapon regenerates its own ammo over time.

## What it does

Weapon regenerates its own ammo over time.

## Valid values

- **Regen:** Slowly tick up ammo while active.
- **Conflict:** May not work with `HELD_ALTRELOAD`.

## Notes

- Good for energy weapons or infinite-ammo concepts.
