# Westar M5 Carbine

`WP_M5`

> ARC Trooper modular rifle. Fast spray primary; high-velocity sniper alt.

## What it does

The Westar M5 is the ARC Trooper's signature modular weapon — fast 200ms primary cadence for sustained suppression, and a `ZOOM_PROJ` sniper-style secondary at very high velocity (9825) for picking targets across long lanes. Mode-cycle (`swapMode`) lets you flip primary/secondary to suit the engagement.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 30
- **Velocity** — 4600 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 200ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Sniper / scoped (`ZOOM_PROJ`)
- **Damage** — 69
- **Velocity** — 9825 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 12
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms (650ms at level 3, 350ms at level 4)

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_WESTARM5`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 12/8 primary, 24/18 sniper; blocking 9/6 / 18/12.
- Reload speed 1100ms — slow swap penalty.
- ARC Trooper / Clone Commander / GAR Officer.
