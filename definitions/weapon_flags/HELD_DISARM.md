# Disarms

`HELD_DISARM`

> Target's held weapon is dropped on hit.

## What it does

The currently equipped weapon of the hit target is forcibly dropped (or temporarily removed). Forces the target to switch loadout slot mid-fight.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Pairs with `HELD_NODISARM` for users that should be immune.
- Strong against single-weapon classes; less punishing against full sidearm loadouts.
