# 🏹 Traditional Bowcaster

`WP_TRAD_BOWCASTER`

Solid projectile bowcaster.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Damage:** **40** (Uncharged)
*   **Charge Cap:** **100**
*   **Velocity:** 7000
*   **Ammo Cost:** 1
*   **Force Drain (Close/Far):** **12/9** (Primary), **25/14** (Secondary)
*   **Force Drain Blocking (Close/Far):** **11/7** (Primary), **20/12** (Secondary)

### 💡 Tips
*   Paired with `MB_ATT_TRAD_BOWCASTER`.
*   Does not fire spread shots like the standard Bowcaster.
