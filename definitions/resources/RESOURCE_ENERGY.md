# Energy

`RESOURCE_ENERGY`

> Generic energy pool for abilities.

## What it does

Generic energy pool for abilities.

**Color:** Dark Yellow
**Default Regen:** 1 / 200ms

## Valid values

- **Regen:** Regenerates even while in the air.
- **Usage:** Elite Trooper, Hero, Commander abilities.

## Notes

- Reliable, steady resource for non-Force users.
