# EE-4 Carbine Rifle

`MB_ATT_EE4`

Short-range burst carbine, known for its rapid-fire capabilities.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Primary Damage:** **36**
* **Sniper Damage:** **70**
* **Velocity:** 6325 (Primary) / 9825 (Sniper)
* **Ammo Cost:** Unknown (Wiki says "NOT CONNECTED"). Need to estimate.

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Standard**. 3-shot burst.
* **Level 2**:
 * **Fire Rate**: Faster.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Sniper Mode**: Unlocks secondary fire as a powerful sniper shot.

### Tips
* The Level 3 sniper mode transforms it into a versatile weapon, similar to the E-11.
