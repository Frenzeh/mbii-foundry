# Akimbo Pistol 3

`CFL_AKIMBOPISTOL3`

Upgrades dual-wielded pistols (Clone/Westar) to Level 3 performance.

### Mechanics
* **Damage:** Applies Pistol 3 damage to *both* guns.
* **Ammo:** Drains ammo as if firing two level 3 pistols.

### Usage
* Makes dual pistols viable primary weapons for elite classes.
