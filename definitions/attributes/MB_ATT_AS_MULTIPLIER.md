# ⚔️ Attack Speed Multiplier

`MB_ATT_AS_MULTIPLIER`

Multiplies the base animation speed of saber attacks.

> **Default:** 1.0

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **1.0:** Normal animation speed.
*   **1.1:** +10% Faster animations.
*   **0.9:** -10% Slower animations.

### 💡 Tips
*   Directly affects how quickly your saber swings (animSpeedScale in .sab editing).
*   Can make an otherwise slow style (like Strong) feel much faster.
