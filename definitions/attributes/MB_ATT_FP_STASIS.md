# Force Stasis

`MB_ATT_FP_STASIS`

> Attribute for freezing a target in place.

## What it does

Grants access to `FP_STASIS`. Locks a target into a stasis state, preventing movement and attacks for a short duration. Rank lengthens the lock or hardens it against being broken.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — short lock · *cost 20*
- **Level 2** — medium lock · *cost 20*
- **Level 3** — long lock · *cost 20*

## Notes

- Countered by Absorb; some damage types may break stasis early.
- High-skill setup: freeze, then Destruction / saber hit / teammate shot.
