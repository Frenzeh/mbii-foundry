# Knockdown Roll

`MB_ATT_KNOCKDOWN_ROLL`

> Converts a knockdown into a recovery roll.

## What it does

Replaces the standard "lay on ground" knockdown response with a quick roll that ends in a standing pose. Skips most of the get-up animation, returning the player to combat much faster.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — basic roll on knockdown.
- **Level 2** — directional roll (player can input direction).
- **Level 3** — instant roll — minimal vulnerable window.

## Notes

- Stacks with `MB_ATT_GETUPS` for full knockdown immunity-equivalent.
- Crucial for highly mobile classes that can't afford to be on the floor.
