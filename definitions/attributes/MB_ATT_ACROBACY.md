# Acrobatics (Sense)

`MB_ATT_ACROBACY`

> Mid-tier saberist movement perks — flips, kicks, advanced rolls.

## What it does

Unlocks the Force-Sense-flavor acrobatic toolkit: aerial flips, kick combos when paired with the saber kick keys, and the wider range of dodge rolls. Functions as a Force movement attribute distinct from Dexterity (which is martial).

## Per level

- **Level 1** — basic flips and rolls.
- **Level 2** — kick combos and longer aerial control.
- **Level 3** — full saberist acrobat — flip-stab, wall-kick, every roll variant.

## Notes

- Saberist counterpart to gunner `MB_ATT_DEXTERITY`.
- Pairs with saber-style kick attributes (`MB_ATT_FLIPKICK`).
- Required for Tavion-style flip attacks.
