# Team Heal

`MB_ATT_FP_TEAM_HEAL`

> Attribute for a radial heal pulse centred on the caster.

## What it does

Grants access to `FP_TEAM_HEAL`. Pulses a 25 HP heal to allies within a radius around the user. Does **not** heal the caster themselves.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — radius 256, heals ~25 HP · *cost 50*
- **Level 2** — radius 384 · *cost 33*
- **Level 3** — radius 512 · *cost 25*

## Notes

- Light-side. Defines the "support Jedi" archetype.
- Use `FP_HEAL` for self-sustain; Team Heal only touches teammates.
- Strong pre-push tool — top teammates up before a timing push.
