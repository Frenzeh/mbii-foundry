# Poison

`HELD_POISON`

> Projectiles poison targets.

## What it does

Projectiles poison targets.

## Valid values

- **Effect:** Applies Poison Dart effect (DoT + Blur).
- **Stacking:** Resets timer on hit, does not stack intensity.

## Notes

- Good for "Acid" or "Toxin" weapons.
