# Light's Beacon

`MB_ATT_LIGHTS_BEACON`

> Shares personal Force buffs with nearby allies.

## What it does

Aura-flavor attribute. While active, the user's currently-running Force buffs (Protect, Absorb, Rage, Speed) extend at reduced strength to all friendly players in radius. Turns a single-target Consular into a team buff multiplier.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small radius, partial buff transfer.
- **Level 2** — medium radius, stronger transfer.
- **Level 3** — large radius, full transfer.

## Notes

- Categorized as Advanced.
- Drains the user's Force at a higher rate — sustaining the aura is costly.
- Allies see the buff visual on themselves while in range.
