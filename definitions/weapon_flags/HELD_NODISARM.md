# Disarm Immune

`HELD_NODISARM`

> Weapon cannot be disarmed off the user.

## What it does

Holding this weapon makes the user immune to incoming `HELD_DISARM` effects. The weapon stays in hand regardless of disarm hits.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Use sparingly — counters the new disarm meta but should be a deliberate trade.
