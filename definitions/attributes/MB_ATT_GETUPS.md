# Getup Ranks

`MB_ATT_GETUPS`

> Determines the speed and animation of recovery from knockdowns.

## Per level

- **Level 0** — ** Standard slow getup.
- **Level 1** — ** Faster getup animation.
- **Level 2** — ** Near-instant recovery.
- **Level 3** — ** Allows shooting/melee immediately after recovery.

## Tips

- Crucial for aggressive classes that are often in the thick of combat.
- Combines with `MB_ATT_KNOCKDOWN_ROLL` for maximum recovery speed.
