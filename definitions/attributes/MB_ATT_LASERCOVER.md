# Laser Cover

`MB_ATT_LASERCOVER`

> Bonus deflection chance when blaster bolts strike a defended angle.

## What it does

Improves the user's chance to deflect or partially absorb blaster fire — flavored as a "trooper covering fire" passive. Stacks with armor and is most noticeable on classes that already deflect (Jedi).

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small chance bonus.
- **Level 2** — medium chance bonus.
- **Level 3** — large chance bonus.

## Notes

- Affects projectile damage, not melee or explosives.
- Stacks with `MB_ATT_BLAST_ARMOUR` and `MB_ATT_MAGNETIC_PLATING`.
- Cooperates with `MB_ATT_FP_SABER_DEFENSE` on saberist classes.
