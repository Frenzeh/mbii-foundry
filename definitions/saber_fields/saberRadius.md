# ⭕ Saber Radius

`saberRadius`

The thickness of the lightsaber blade.

> **Default:** 3.0

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Visual:** Makes the core thicker.
*   **Hitbox:** Slightly increases the hit detection cylinder? (Needs verification, often purely visual in JKA mods, but MBII may use it for collision).

### 💡 Tips
*   **Broadsabers:** Use values like **5.0** or **6.0** for a "heavy plasma" look.
*   **Rapiers:** Use **1.5** or **2.0** for a thin, needle-like blade.
