# Dark Rage (Special)

`EAS_FP_DARKRAGE`

> Activates a Dark-side Rage variant.

## What it does

Triggers the Dark Rage force ability — a Dark-aligned variant of `MB_ATT_FP_RAGE` with its own visuals, drain profile, and exit penalty. Typically grants damage / movement boost in exchange for harsher post-effect drain.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Distinct from generic `MB_ATT_FP_RAGE`; Dark Rage is force-aligned and may be incompatible with light-aligned passives.
- Requires the matching attribute on the class for cost, duration, and exit penalty values.
