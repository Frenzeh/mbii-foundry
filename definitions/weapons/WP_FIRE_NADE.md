# Fire Grenade

`WP_FIRE_NADE`

> Incendiary grenade. Drops a burning area-of-denial pool on detonation.

## What it does

The fire grenade trades single-blast damage for a sustained burning hazard — on detonation it leaves a fire patch that ticks burn damage on anyone standing in it. Useful for denying choke routes, smoking out cover, or finishing off low-HP fleeing targets. Standard grenade cook timing.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Throw (timed cook)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Lobbed throw
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_FIRE_GRENADES`.
- Burning patch persists for several seconds — area-denial more than burst kill.
- Effective vs Wookiees and other slow heavy classes.
