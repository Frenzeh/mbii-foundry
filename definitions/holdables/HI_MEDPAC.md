# Medpac

`HI_MEDPAC`

> Standard BioTech bacta canister — slow heal.

## What it does

Restores HP up to the user's max in a controlled animation window. Non-rapid: locks the user out of weapons during use, so it's a "behind cover" tool, not a clutch save.

## Notes

- Charge count and per-use heal scale with `MB_ATT_BACTA`.
- Cannot be cancelled mid-use — commit only when you have cover.
- Use `HI_MEDPAC_BIG` for the larger, slower variant or `HI_STIMPACK` for the combat-stim alternative.
