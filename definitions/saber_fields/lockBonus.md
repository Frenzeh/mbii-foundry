# Lock Bonus

`lockBonus`

Modifies the chance of winning a saber lock (blade bind).

> **Default:** 0

### Mechanics
* **Value:** Integer.
* **Effect:** A positive value increases your chance to push back an opponent in a blade lock.

### Tips
* High strength characters (Vader, Desann) or those with high `MB_ATT_WOOKIE_STRENGTH` often have a bonus here.
