# Forcefield Charges

`MB_ATT_FORCEFIELD`

> Charge count for the deployable energy barrier.

## What it does

Sets how many `HI_SHIELD` deployable barriers the user can drop per round, plus per-shield HP at higher ranks.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — 1 charge, baseline HP.
- **Level 2** — 2 charges, tougher shield.
- **Level 3** — 3+ charges, full HP shields.

## Notes

- Sibling of holdable `HI_SHIELD`.
- Sabers pass through unimpeded; only blocks projectile fire.
- Allies can pass through; enemies cannot.
