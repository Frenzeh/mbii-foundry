# Quickdraw

`MB_ATT_QUICKDRAW`

> Faster weapon-swap animations. Cuts the time between switching weapons.

## What it does

Reduces the weapon-swap time — the animation between holstering one weapon and raising the next. With Quickdraw you can shoot, swap to pistol, finish off, and swap back faster than the default. Critical for combo loadouts (rifle → pistol → grenade).

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Faster swap animation.
- **Level 2** — Significantly faster; near-instant swap.
- **Level 3** — Maximum speed; instant swap on most pairs.

## Notes

- Pairs especially well with sidearm-heavy loadouts.
- Useful when you need to switch from a long-cycle primary (T-21, EE-3 burst) to a fast pistol.
- Bounty Hunter, Mandalorian, Hero loadouts.
