# 💥 Force Repulse

`MB_ATT_FP_REPULSE`

An omni-directional blast of Force energy.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Cost:** **60 FP**.
*   **Radius:** **1024** units.
*   **Cooldown:** 1 second.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Effect:** Instant 360-degree knockback.
*   **Damage:** None (Pure knockback).

### 💡 Tips
*   **Get Off Me:** Best panic button when surrounded.
*   **Breaker:** Breaks grabs and grapples instantly.
