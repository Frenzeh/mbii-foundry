# 📈 Stat Viewer

`CFL_STATVIEWER`

Allows the player to see detailed stats of allies.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **HUD:** Displays Health and Shield bars above teammates' heads.
*   **Wallhack:** Often allows seeing these bars through walls.

### 💡 Tips
*   **Medics:** Mandatory for effective healing.
*   **Officers:** Good for tactical awareness.
