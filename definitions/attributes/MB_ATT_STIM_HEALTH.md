# Health Stim

`MB_ATT_STIM_HEALTH`

> Single-target instant heal — point and use.

## What it does

Press Use while aiming at an ally to instantly restore Health in one transfer. Faster than the dispenser, smaller pool than the drop. Limited charges.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small heal per stim.
- **Level 2** — medium heal per stim.
- **Level 3** — large heal per stim.

## Notes

- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DISP_HEALTH` (channel) and `MB_ATT_DROP_HEALTH` (drop).
- Best for clutch saves — single fast injection rather than sustained ticking.
