# Bounce on Walls

`bounceOnWalls`

> Saber bounces off architecture instead of sliding.

## What it does

Saber bounces off architecture instead of sliding.

## Valid values

- **1 (True):** Hitting a wall stops the swing and plays a bounce animation/sound.
- **0 (False):** Saber slides along the wall (Molten cutting effect).

## Notes

- Recommended for physical weapons (Batons, Staves) that cannot cut through durasteel.
