# Block Points Stim

`MB_ATT_STIM_BLOCK`

> Single-target instant BP restore — point and use.

## What it does

Press Use while aiming at an ally saberist to instantly restore Block Points.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small BP per stim.
- **Level 2** — medium BP per stim.
- **Level 3** — large BP per stim.

## Notes

- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DISP_BLOCK` and `MB_ATT_DROP_BLOCK`.
- Niche — only saberists consume BP.
