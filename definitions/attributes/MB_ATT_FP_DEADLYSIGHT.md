# Deadly Sight

`MB_ATT_FP_DEADLYSIGHT`

> Attribute for damage-over-time via eye contact.

## What it does

Grants access to `FP_DEADLYSIGHT`. Applies a DoT to a target while the caster's gaze stays on them. Rank widens the aim cone and strengthens the tick damage. Drains FP continuously while active.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — tight aim cone, short DoT · *cost 20*
- **Level 2** — wider cone, stronger DoT · *cost 20*
- **Level 3** — widest cone, strongest DoT · *cost 20*

## Notes

- Sith power. Punishing against clustered low-HP infantry.
- Counter by breaking line-of-sight or with Absorb.
- Strong opener when combined with Rage or other damage amps.
