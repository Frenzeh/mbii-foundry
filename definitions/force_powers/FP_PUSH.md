# Force Push

`FP_PUSH`

> Telekinetic shove that launches enemies, deflects projectiles, and wins ledge fights.

## What it does

Fires a cone of kinetic force forward. Pushes enemies back (and over at higher ranks), redirects certain projectiles (rockets, grenades), and can break stances. Rank governs cone width, range, and the strength of knockdown.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — narrow cone, short range, light knockback · *cost 20*
- **Level 2** — wider cone, reliable knockdown on hit · *cost 20*
- **Level 3** — widest cone, longest range, strongest knockdown; can redirect rockets · *cost 20*

## Notes

- Countered by Force Absorb (light side) — absorbed and converted to FP.
- Two pushes clash and cancel when opponents push at each other simultaneously.
- Key ledge/projectile tool — even Rank 1 is useful for grenade control.
