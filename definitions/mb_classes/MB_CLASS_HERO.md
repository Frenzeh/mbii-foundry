# Hero

`MB_CLASS_HERO`

> Rebel dueling gunslinger — high-mobility pistolero with best-in-class armor for infantry.

## Role

The Rebellion's signature ranged skirmisher. 100 HP, the fastest human speed in the game (250), a deep 15/30/45/60 armor pool, Energy resource for Dodge, Dash, and Quickthrow — Hero is built to evade damage while kiting. Han, Lando, and Leia are all Hero variants.

## Default loadout

- **Base health** — 100
- **Base speed** — 250.0 (max infantry speed)
- **Armor pool** — `AP_15_30_45_60_M60`
- **Melee moves** — All
- **Resource** — Energy
- **Abilities (CS1-4)** — `EAS_HI_DODGE`, `EAS_HI_DASH`, `EAS_HI_QUICKTHROW`, none
- **Forcecfg** — `forcecfg/red/hero`
- **Model** — `jan`

## Signature mechanics

- Dodge drains Energy to negate blaster hits; Dash trades Energy for a burst of forward momentum; Quickthrow lets grenades be thrown without the weapon-swap animation.
- Thickest armor pool of the non-droid classes — Hero specs lean less on raw HP and more on stretching armor + Dodge against blaster damage.

## Counters

- Energy-burn classes (Sith Lightning, heavy-fire specialists) deplete Dodge reserves.
- Sabers in the duel — no innate melee advantage.
- Rocket / TD — AoE ignores Dodge timing windows.
