# ChargeEffect

`ChargeEffect effects/foo/charge.efx`

> Effect played while the user is charging the weapon.

## What it does

Looped effect at the muzzle while a chargeable weapon is being charged (e.g. Bowcaster, Charged Disruptor). Stops on release / fire.

## Notes

- `OverchargeEffect` is the additional layer for fully-charged shots.
- Pair with `ChargeSound` for matching audio.
