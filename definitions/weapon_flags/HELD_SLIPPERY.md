# Slippery

`HELD_SLIPPERY`

> Target loses footing on hit.

## What it does

Hit targets slip or slide briefly, reducing their ability to hold a stable firing line.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Soft form of crowd-control; less punishing than `HELD_KNOCKDOWN` but more frequent.
