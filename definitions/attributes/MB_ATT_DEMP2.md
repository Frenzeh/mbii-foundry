# DEMP-2 Pulse Rifle

`MB_ATT_DEMP2`

> Anti-droid ion pulse rifle. Devastating versus SBDs and Droidekas.

## What it does

Grants `WP_DEMP2`. Primary fires a steady ion bolt; secondary holds for a chargeable EMP discharge that can stun and strip droid shields. Underwhelming versus organics — this is a specialist anti-droid pick.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Basic ion bolt + short-charge EMP secondary.
- **Level 2** — Faster charge, larger EMP burst radius.
- **Level 3** — Maximum charge and discharge damage; full anti-droid kit.

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_DEMP2`.
- Devastating vs SBDs, Droidekas, Magna Guards.
- Bring a backup pistol — primary damage vs organics is below average.
- Pairs well with `MB_ATT_PULSE_GRENADES` for full anti-droid loadout.
