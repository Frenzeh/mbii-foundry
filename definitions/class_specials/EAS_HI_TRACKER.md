# Tracking Dart (Quickfire)

`EAS_HI_TRACKER`

> Fires a Tracking Dart from the wrist without switching weapons.

## What it does

Single-shot bind that sends a tracking dart at the cursor target. The dart lights the hit player on the team radar for a duration, exposing flank routes and cloaked retreats. Bound on Bounty Hunter alongside `EAS_HI_POISON`.

## Notes

- Ammo and proc duration scale with `MB_ATT_TRACKING_DART`.
- Single-shot per activation; the bind is not a full-auto weapon.
- Stacks with cloak detection — a darted Spy stays visible to teammates even while cloaked.
