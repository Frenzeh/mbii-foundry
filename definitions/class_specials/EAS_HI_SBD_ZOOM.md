# SBD Zoom

`EAS_HI_SBD_ZOOM`

> Zoom toggle restricted to MB_CLASS_SBD.

## What it does

Toggles a mid-tier scope for the Super Battle Droid. Functions as electrobinoculars without consuming an inventory slot. Always active on SBD as special3.

## Notes

- Hard-gated to `MB_CLASS_SBD` — assigning to other classes does nothing in stock builds.
- Does not affect spread or damage; purely visual zoom.
- Binoculars (`MB_ATT_BINOCULARS`) provide a comparable effect for non-SBD classes.
