# Detonation Pack

`MB_ATT_DET_PACK`

> Remotely detonated explosive charge.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — **Single Pack**.
- **Level 2** — **Two Packs**.

## Stats

- **Damage** — High.
- **Splash Radius** — Large.
- **Deployment** — Can be thrown and detonated manually.

## Tips

- Good for ambushes or destroying objectives.
- Can be tricky to use against moving targets.
