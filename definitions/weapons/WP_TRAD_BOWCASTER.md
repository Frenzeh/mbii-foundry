# Traditional Bowcaster

`WP_TRAD_BOWCASTER`

> Solid-projectile bowcaster. Single-bolt primary, heavy charged secondary.

## What it does

The Traditional Bowcaster is the single-bolt counterpart to the standard `WP_BOWCASTER` — it does NOT fire a spread. Primary delivers a high-damage single bolt at 800ms; secondary uses `CHGE_CLONE` for a charged bolt that scales up to 100 damage. Mode-cycle weapon (`swapMode`, swap unlocks at level 3).

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 40 (uncharged)
- **Velocity** — 7000 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 12
- **Fire rate** — 800ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Charged shot (`CHGE_CLONE`)
- **Damage cap** — 100 at full charge
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 900ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_TRAD_BOWCASTER`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 12/9 primary, 25/14 secondary; blocking 11/7 / 20/12.
- `ZOOM_CASTER` zoom mode on both modes.
- Wookiee Warrior and Bowcaster specialist kits.
