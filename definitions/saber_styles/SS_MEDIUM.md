# Yellow (Medium)

`SS_MEDIUM`

> Balanced one-handed style — middle ground between Fast and Strong.

## What it does

The classic "Yellow" stance. Moderate swing speed, moderate damage, moderate range, moderate Block Point cost. Designed as the do-everything style for the saberist who wants no hard weakness in any one duel matchup.

## Notes

- Damage and BP cost sit between Fast and Strong; counters Fast on attack and trades evenly with Strong.
- Reach is wider than Fast but doesn't punish out-of-range whiffs as hard as Strong.
- Pairs well with `MB_ATT_SS_YELLOWSTYLE` rank investment for combo extension.
