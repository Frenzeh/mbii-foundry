# Bounty Hunter

`MB_CLASS_BOUNTY_HUNTER`

> Imperial mercenary — poison darts, tracker, and the heaviest armor in the infantry roster.

## Role

The Imperial counterpart to Hero, but tuned as a hit-and-run disruptor rather than a duelist. 100 HP, 250 speed, and the premium 10/20/50/80 armor pool give a BH the durability to take exchanges other light infantry can't. Default ability set signals the class identity — crowd control, debuff, and anti-Jedi harassment. Boba, Jango, and Cad Bane are BH variants.

## Default loadout

- **Base health** — 100
- **Base speed** — 250.0
- **Armor pool** — `AP_10_20_50_80_M80`
- **Melee moves** — All
- **Resource** — none
- **Abilities (CS1-4)** — none, `EAS_HI_KICK`, `EAS_HI_KICK`, none
- **Abilities (CS5-8)** — `EAS_HI_POISON`, `EAS_HI_TRACKER`, none, none
- **Forcecfg** — `forcecfg/blue/BH`
- **Model** — `rodian`

## Signature mechanics

- Poison Dart: DoT that ignores armor, drains enemy regen.
- Tracker Dart: reveals a target through walls for the team.
- Kick: the anti-Jedi shutdown move — knockdown-capable against saber-users.

## Counters

- Saber deflection if the BH stops to shoot instead of kiting.
- Wookiees trade well once they close the gap.
- Group focus-fire overwhelms the armor pool.
