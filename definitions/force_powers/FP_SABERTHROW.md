# Saber Throw

`FP_SABERTHROW`

> Hurl your saber like a boomerang — high risk, high reward.

## What it does

Telekinetically throws the active lightsaber as a projectile. The saber flies forward, deals damage on contact, and returns to the user. Rank determines distance, steerability, and whether the throw drains FP continuously mid-flight.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — short throw, flat spin, auto-return · *cost 20*
- **Level 2** — medium range, slight return-path control · *cost 20*
- **Level 3** — long range, steerable mid-flight, continuous FP drain while airborne · *cost 20*

## Notes

- You are **defenseless** while the saber is in the air — no parry, no block.
- Pulled weapons + saber throw is a classic combo.
- Can deal ~30 damage on clean hits; flashy but rarely optimal in serious play.
