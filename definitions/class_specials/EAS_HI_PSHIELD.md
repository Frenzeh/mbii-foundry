# Personal Shield

`EAS_HI_PSHIELD`

> Toggles a personal energy shield on the user.

## What it does

Activates a Droideka-style or wrist-shield style personal energy shield around the user. Drains a dedicated shield resource while up; absorbs incoming projectile damage at the cost of mobility / fire restrictions configured by the owning attribute.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Requires `MB_ATT_PSHIELD` on the class.
- Drain rates, max charge, and ability-while-shielded are governed by the attribute, not the EAS entry.
