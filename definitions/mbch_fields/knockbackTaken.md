# Knockback Taken

`knockbackTaken` / `rankKbTaken`

Multiplier for how far YOU fly when hit by explosives or Push.

> **Default:** 1.0

### Mechanics
* **0.0:** **Immovable Object**.
* **0.5:** "Heavy" feel. Hard to push off ledges.
* **1.5:** "Light" feel. Easily ragdolled.

### Tips
* **Droidekas** and **SBDs** usually have low values (0.5 - 0.8) to simulate weight.
