# 🛡️ Blaster Defense

`MB_ATT_GUN_DEFENSE`

Passive ability to block blaster bolts with a lightsaber.

> **Default:** Rank 0 (Glass Canon).

### 📊 Stats
*   **FP Drain Multiplier:**
    *   **Rank 0:** **2.5x** (Blocks drain massive FP).
    *   **Rank 1:** **1.2x** (20% more drain).
    *   **Rank 2:** **1.0x** (Standard drain).
    *   **Rank 3:** **0.85x** (15% less drain).

### 📊 Levels
*   **Rank 1:** Poor defense. Good for emergencies.
*   **Rank 2:** Standard defense.
*   **Rank 3:** **Master**. Highly efficient blocking. Increased block radius on projectiles (+30%).

### 💡 Tips
*   **Rank 3** is essential for surviving against Heavy Repeaters or Miniguns.
*   Without this, a Jedi melts under fire.
