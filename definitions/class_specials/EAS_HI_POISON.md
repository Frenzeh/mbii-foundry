# Poison Dart (Quickfire)

`EAS_HI_POISON`

> Fires a Poison Dart from the wrist without switching weapons.

## What it does

Dispatches a single poison dart on the special-key bind. Deals minor direct damage and applies the poison DoT + screen-blur debuff to the target. Bound by Bounty Hunter alongside `EAS_HI_TRACKER` for offhand utility shots.

## Notes

- Ammo and rank scaling come from `MB_ATT_POISON_DART` — the binding just routes the fire input.
- Single-shot per activation; cannot rapid-fire from the special key.
- Pairs with `HELD_POISON` weapons to stack DoT timers.
