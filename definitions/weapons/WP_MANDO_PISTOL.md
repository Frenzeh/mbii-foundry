# Westar-34 Pistol

`WP_MANDO_PISTOL`

> Mandalorian sidearm. Accurate single-shot primary; charged Mando-style alt.

## What it does

The Westar-34 is the Mandalorian counterpart to the DL-44 — same 36 damage at 6325 velocity, but with a tighter spread and the dual-wield Mandalorian animation set. Secondary holds for a charged shot via `CHGE_MANDO`, available at level 2+.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 36
- **Velocity** — 6325 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 500ms (250ms at level 4)

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Charged shot (`CHGE_MANDO`) at level 2+
- **Ammo cost** — 4
- **Fire rate** — 800ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_MANDO_PISTOL`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 13/9 primary; blocking 11/6.
- Mandalorian Bounty Hunter signature dual-pistol.
