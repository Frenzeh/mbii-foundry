# 📢 Support Beacon (Spawner)

`HI_SPAWNER`

Spawns an allied NPC reinforcement.

> **Uses:** 1-time use.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Effect:** Spawns an NPC based on `MB_ATT_SPAWNER` rank.
*   **Rank 1:** Weak reinforcement.
*   **Rank 2:** Medium reinforcement.
*   **Rank 3:** Strong reinforcement.

### 💡 Tips
*   Use `MB_ATT_SPAWNER` to configure exactly *what* spawns.
