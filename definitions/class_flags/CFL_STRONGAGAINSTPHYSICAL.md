# Melee Resistance

`CFL_STRONGAGAINSTPHYSICAL`

Reduces damage taken from physical attacks.

### Mechanics
* **Effect:** **50% Damage Reduction** vs Melee (Punches, Kicks, Wookiee Slams).
* **Vulnerability:** Does **not** protect against Lightsabers or Blasters.

### Usage
* Essential for "Brawler" characters who want to trade hits in melee.
* Counters Wookiees (`MB_ATT_WOOKIE_STRENGTH`) effectively.
