# Armor Regen Cap

`MB_ATT_ARMOUR_REGEN_CAP`

> Ceiling above which passive armor regen stops.

## What it does

Caps the armor regen at a configured value. Once armor reaches this amount, regen pauses until damage drops it below.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — low cap.
- **Level 2** — medium cap.
- **Level 3** — full max-armor cap.

## Notes

- 0 means cap to current max armor.
- Sibling of `MB_ATT_ARMOUR_REGEN_AMOUNT` and `MB_ATT_ARMOUR_REGEN_RATE`.
