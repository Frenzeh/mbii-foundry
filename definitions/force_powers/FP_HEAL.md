# Force Heal

`FP_HEAL`

> Instantly restores health to yourself at the cost of Force Points.

## What it does

Channels the Force to heal the user. Consumes FP and awards HP in fixed chunks per activation (25 HP per tick internally), respecting a short cooldown between uses. Cannot be used during certain hand-animation states and is interrupted if you take damage mid-channel.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small heal per activation, slowest recovery · *cost 50*
- **Level 2** — larger heal per activation, faster recovery · *cost 40*
- **Level 3** — largest heal per activation, most FP-efficient · *cost 30*

## Notes

- Light-side power — Jedi only; Sith get Drain instead.
- Higher ranks pay less FP per heal, making Rank 3 by far the most efficient.
- Can be interrupted by damage or stagger; safe only when you have cover.
