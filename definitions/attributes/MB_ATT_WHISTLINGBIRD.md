`MB_ATT_WHISTLINGBIRD`

Miniature seeking missiles launcher.

*   **Count:** 12 (Rank 1) / 6 (Rank 2). (Wait, rank 2 has less? Or is it batches? Wiki says Rank 1 unlocks it).

*   **Force Drain Blocking (Close/Far):** **12/10**
