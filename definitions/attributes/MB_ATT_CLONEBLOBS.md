# Clone Blobs

`MB_ATT_CLONEBLOBS`

Specialized ammunition for the DC-15A Clone Rifle. Grants Pulse (Ion) rounds.

**Class Specific:** Clone Trooper, ARC Trooper.

### Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   3 Pulse/Ion rounds (Right-click to fire in Rifle mode).
    *   High damage to droids and shields.
    *   Causes knockdown on direct hit.

*   **Level 2**:
    *   6 Pulse/Ion rounds.

*   **Level 3**:
    *   9 Pulse/Ion rounds.

### Notes
*   Requires `MB_ATT_CLONERIFLE`.
*   Essential for anti-SBD/Droideka loadouts.
