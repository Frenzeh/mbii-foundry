# E-22 Heavy Blaster

`MB_ATT_E_22`

Heavy Blaster Rifle used by Imperial Death Troopers.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Primary Damage:** **33**
* **Secondary Damage:** **45**
* **Velocity:** 8000 (Primary) / 6000 (Secondary)
* **Ammo Cost:** 6 (Primary) / 8 (Secondary)

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Standard**. High damage.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Slower, more powerful shot.
* **Level 2**:
 * **Fire Rate**: Improved.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Recoil**: Reduced.
 * **Accuracy**: Improved while moving.

### Tips
* Combines heavy damage with a decent fire rate.
* The secondary fire is excellent for dislodging enemies.
