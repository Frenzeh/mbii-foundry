# Hull Strength

`MB_ATT_HULL_STRENGTH`

Armor plating for Super Battle Droids.

**Class Specific:** SBD.

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * Light Plating (~100-200 Armor).

* **Level 2**:
 * Medium Plating (~300-400 Armor).

* **Level 3**:
 * Heavy Plating (Up to 900+ Armor depending on model/config).
 * Makes the SBD a walking tank.

### Notes
* SBDs rely almost entirely on Hull Strength as they have no Shields and cannot dodge effectively.
* Combine with `MB_ATT_CORTOSIS` or `MB_ATT_MAGNETIC_PLATING` for maximum durability.
