# Seeker Drone

`MB_ATT_DRONE`

> Deployable scout / combat seeker drone. Floats with the operator and engages targets.

## What it does

Spawns a Seeker Drone that follows the player and autonomously fires at nearby enemies. Drone fires a low-damage blaster shot but provides constant secondary pressure and scouting. Limited HP — line-of-sight blaster fire will pop it.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Single basic drone, low damage per shot.
- **Level 2** — Faster fire rate, slightly tougher drone.
- **Level 3** — Improved damage; persistent until destroyed.

## Notes

- Drone is an entity, not a `WP_*` weapon.
- Useful as an extra "second gun" while the player handles primary fire.
- Common on Imperial Officer / Mandalorian utility loadouts.
