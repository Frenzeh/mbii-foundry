# Wrist Laser

`MB_ATT_WRISTLASER`

> Rapid-fire wrist mounted blaster.

## Stats

- **Damage** — **65** per shot (High).
- **Ammo** — 4 Shots max (`MAX_WRIST_LASER_SHOTS`).
- **Cooldown** — **6 seconds** after depletion.
- **Force Drain (Close/Far)** — **19/15**
- **Force Drain Blocking (Close/Far)** — **15/12**

## Tips

- Good for suppressing Jedi (drains block points) or finishing low HP targets.
- Can be used while flying.
