# Force Focus

`MB_ATT_FORCEFOCUS`

> Increases the maximum size of the Force Pool.

## What it does

Grows the user's max FP — the resource powering all Force powers. Higher pool = more chains of casts before bottoming out, even at the same regen rate.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — 90 FP cap.
- **Level 2** — 100 FP cap.
- **Level 3** — 120 FP cap.

## Notes

- Vital for Force-heavy builds (Consular/Sorcerer flavor).
- Pairs with `MB_ATT_FORCEATTUNE` (cost discount) for sustainable casting.
- Doesn't affect regen rate — pair with `MB_ATT_RESOURCE_REGEN_*` for full caster tuning.
