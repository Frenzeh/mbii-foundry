# 🛡️ Blast Armor

`MB_ATT_BLAST_ARMOUR`

Reinforced padding/plating designed to absorb kinetic shockwaves and shrapnel.

> **Default:** None.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Rank 1:**
    *   **Explosives:** High resistance (~50%?) to Grenades, Rockets, Thermal Detonators.
    *   **Snipers:** High resistance to Disruptor/Projectile Rifle shots (Wiki: "% resistance to Sniper Rifles").
    *   **Melee:** Slight resistance to Melee (Wiki: "% resistance to Melee").
    *   **Knockdown:** Increases threshold to be knocked down by explosions.

### 💡 Usage
*   **Heavy Troopers:** Essential for "Tank" classes to survive grenade spam.
*   **SBD:** Adds the "Blast Armor" property to their hull.
