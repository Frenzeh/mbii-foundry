# Base Seeker

`MB_ATT_BASESEEKER`

> Charge count for `HI_SEEKER` drones.

## What it does

Sets how many Seeker Drone charges the user has and the per-drone HP/dwell-time. Each rank either grows the pool or hardens the drone.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — 1 charge, fragile drone.
- **Level 2** — 2 charges, tougher drone.
- **Level 3** — 3+ charges, full HP and longer dwell.

## Notes

- Sibling of the holdable `HI_SEEKER` and special `EAS_HI_SEEKER`.
- Drone HP is intentionally low — one rocket kills it.
- Effective against Jedi who reflexively swat at projectiles.
