# Damage Given Multiplier

`damageGiven` / `rankDmgGiven`

> Global damage output multiplier for this character.

## What it does

Global damage output multiplier for this character.

**Default:** 1.0 (100%)

## Valid values

- **1.0:** Normal Damage.
- **0.8:** 80% Damage (20% reduction).
- **1.2:** 120% Damage (20% bonus).

## Notes

- **0.8** is common for classes with high fire rate or spam potential.
- **1.2+** is used for "Glass Cannons" or Bosses.
