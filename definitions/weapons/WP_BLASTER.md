# E-11 Blaster Rifle

`WP_BLASTER`

> Standard Imperial issue rifle. Bread-and-butter trooper weapon, versatile at all ranges.

## What it does

The E-11 is the most balanced rifle in the game: solid damage, dependable fire rate, and a faster spray secondary. Available to a wide array of Imperial classes and used as the baseline against which other rifles are tuned. At higher ranks the alt-fire becomes accurate enough to make the E-11 a serious sustained-fire option.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 36
- **Velocity** — 6325 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 350ms

## Secondary fire

- **Damage** — 31
- **Velocity** — 4600 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 3
- **Fire rate** — 200ms (rapid spray)

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_BLASTER`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 13/9 primary, 9/7 secondary; blocking 11/7 / 7/5.
- A staple Stormtrooper loadout — workhorse weapon, reliable on every map.
