# Armor Dispenser

`MB_ATT_DISP_ARMOR`

> Hold-to-armor allies — passive armor transfer.

## What it does

Hold the Use key while looking at an ally to restore their Armor every 250 ms. Lets the user repair shielded teammates (Droid units) without committing to a stationary spawn-room visit.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small armor per tick.
- **Level 2** — medium armor per tick.
- **Level 3** — large armor per tick.

## Notes

- Tick interval is fixed at 250 ms.
- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DROP_ARMOR` and `MB_ATT_STIM_ARMOR`.
- Useful on engineer-type kits supporting SBDs and Droideka.
