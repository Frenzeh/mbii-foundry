# Barge

`MB_ATT_BARGE`

> Wookiee bull-rush charge attack.

## What it does

Enables the Wookiee Barge — a forward charge that bowls over enemies and deals impact damage. Routed through `Skill_Barge`. Originally gated to Wookiee Fury; this attribute lets non-Fury classes equip Barge as a standalone special.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — short charge distance, low damage.
- **Level 2** — longer charge, higher damage.
- **Level 3** — full charge with knockdown on contact.

## Notes

- Bound to `EAS_HI_BARGE`.
- Knocks down unblocking targets; blocked targets are pushed and staggered.
- Avoids the Resource Rage assignment that vanilla Wookiee Fury imposes — usable on classes without Rage as a resource.
