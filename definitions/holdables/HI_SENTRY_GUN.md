# 🔫 Assault Sentry

`HI_SENTRY_GUN`

Deployable automated turret.

> **Uses:** 1-time deployment.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Deploy:** Fixes to the ground/surface.
*   **Combat:** Fires medium-fast bolts at enemies. Has collision.
*   **Duration:** Destroys itself after X seconds, X damage taken, or X shots fired.
*   **Death:** Explodes when destroyed.

### 💡 Tips
*   Place around corners to ambush enemies or cover flanks.
