# Commander

`MB_CLASS_COMMANDER`

> Imperial officer — faster, tankier, enables team-play attributes.

## Role

The Imperial team's elite rifle-infantry / force-multiplier. Commanders trade Soldier's point efficiency for better baseline stats (80 HP, 225 speed, 10/20/30/40 armor) and gate attributes like `MB_ATT_RALLY`, `MB_ATT_ASSEMBLE`, and `MB_ATT_DODGE` that buff nearby teammates or grant personal survivability. The shot-caller of a standard Imperial comp.

## Default loadout

- **Base health** — 80
- **Base speed** — 225.0 (~17% faster than Soldier)
- **Armor pool** — `AP_10_20_30_40_M40`
- **Melee moves** — All (`MM_ALL`) — punch, kick, kata
- **Resource** — Energy (drives Dodge)
- **Abilities (CS1-4)** — `EAS_HI_DODGE`, `EAS_HI_SPAWNER`, none, none
- **Forcecfg** — `forcecfg/blue/commander`
- **Model** — `imperial`

## Signature mechanics

- Energy resource pool — fuels Dodge and other ability-consuming attributes.
- Often the Rally-anchor enabling friendly Soldiers to Assemble (spawn on) them.

## Counters

- Sabers still win the duel at close range.
- Concussion Rifle / Rocket Launcher two-shots through the armor pool.
- If the Commander dies, any teammates relying on Rally / Assemble lose their buff.
