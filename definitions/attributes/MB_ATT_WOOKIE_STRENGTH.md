# Wookiee Strength

`MB_ATT_WOOKIE_STRENGTH`

Increases physical strength, melee damage, and resistance to crowd control.

> **Default:** Normal (Human strength).

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Damage**: Increased Melee damage.
 * **Stability**: Slight knockback resistance.

* **Level 2**:
 * **Damage**: Heavy Melee damage.
 * **Speed**: Faster movement while holding Melee.
 * ** Immunity**: Cannot be knocked down by normal explosions or kicks.

* **Level 3**:
 * **Damage**: **Lethal**. 1-hit kill potential on weak enemies.
 * **Speed**: Agile Brawler.
 * ** Immunity**: Immune to **Force Grip** (except Crush), **Lightning Stun**, and **Force Push/Pull** knockback.
 * **Shove**: Running into enemies pushes them aside.

### Tips
* Level 3 makes a Wookiee a terrifying anti-Jedi juggernaut.
* Combine with **Wookiee Health** for maximum tankiness.
