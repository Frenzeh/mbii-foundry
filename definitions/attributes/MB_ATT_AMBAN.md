# 🔫 Amban Sniper Rifle

`MB_ATT_AMBAN`

Mandalorian phase-pulse blaster, capable of disintegrating targets.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Primary Damage:** **20** (Pulse) / **50** (Level 3 Pulse)
*   **Secondary Damage:** **80** (Sniper) -> **143** (Max Charge)
*   **Velocity:** 9825 (Sniper)
*   **Charge Time:** 1000ms (1 second).
*   **Ammo Cost:** 1 (Primary) / 1 (Secondary).

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Primary**: Pulse fire.
    *   **Secondary**: Charged sniper shot.
*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Charge**: Faster charge rate for secondary.
*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Damage**: Primary pulse damage **+50%**.
    *   **Effect**: Secondary shot instantly disintegrates targets on kill.

### 💡 Tips
*   **Disintegration**: The secondary fire is an instant-kill on most unarmored targets.
*   **Charge**: Requires careful timing to maximize secondary damage.
