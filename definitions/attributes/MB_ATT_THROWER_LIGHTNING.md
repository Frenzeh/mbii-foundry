# Lightning Thrower

`MB_ATT_THROWER_LIGHTNING`

> Lightning element variant for the Thrower stream weapon.

## What it does

Configures the Thrower (`WP_THROWER`) to deal lightning/ion damage type — strong against droids and shielded targets, with a brief stun chance on tick. Selected as part of the Thrower's element loadout alongside `MB_ATT_THROWER`.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Lightning element active; minor stun chance per tick.
- **Level 2** — Higher stun chance; better droid damage scaling.
- **Level 3** — Max stun and droid bonus.

## Notes

- Requires `MB_ATT_THROWER` for the base weapon.
- Element-swap variants: `_ICE`, `_POISON`, `_PLASMA`, `_FLAME`.
- Strong vs SBDs, Droidekas, and Magna Guards.
