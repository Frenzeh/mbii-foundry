# Cortosis

`MB_ATT_CORTOSIS`

A rare, conductive material that shorts out lightsabers upon contact.

> **Default:** None.

### Mechanics
* **Rank 1:**
 * **Resistance:** Armor stops lightsabers. Damage is subtracted from Armor pool first.
 * **Bleed:** Some damage may still bleed through to HP depending on saber damage vs armor value.
* **Rank 2:**
 * **Stagger:** Causes the attacker's saber to recoil/stagger briefly on hit. (Wiki mentions "Causes a brief stagger").

### Usage
* **Anti-Jedi:** Mandatory for any class expected to survive melee combat (Mandalorians, MagnaGuards, Cortosis Droids).
* **Weakness:** Does nothing against blasters.
