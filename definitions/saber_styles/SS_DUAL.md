# Dual

`SS_DUAL`

> Two single sabers — fast offense, throw one keep one.

## What it does

Dual-wielded single sabers (Anakin/Asajj/Starkiller flavor). Fast attack speed with the second blade adding hits to combos. Smaller block radius than Staff, but excellent against blasters since both blades deflect. Can throw one saber while keeping the other — Dual's signature trick.

## Notes

- Saber throw uses the off-hand blade; primary stays in hand for melee.
- Block box is tighter than Staff; fewer "free saves" against angled attacks.
- Pairs with `MB_ATT_SS_DUALSTYLE` rank for combo extension and chain count.
