# 🔫 Pistol

`MB_ATT_PISTOL`

The standard **DL-44 Blaster Pistol**. Reliable sidearm with decent damage and accuracy.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Damage:** **34** (Standard), **50** (Level 3/Charged?)
*   **Charge:** Can charge up to **62** damage.
*   **Velocity:** 6325 units/sec.
*   **Ammo Cost:** 1 per shot.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Basic**. Standard accuracy and fire rate.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: Charged shot.

*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Improved**. Increased fire rate. Better accuracy.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: Charge time reduced.

*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Elite**. Maximum fire rate.
    *   **Damage:** Base damage increased to **50**.
    *   **Perfect Accuracy** even while moving.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: Charged shot is extremely powerful and charges quickly.

### 💡 Tips
*   Level 3 Pistol is a viable primary weapon for aggressive "Run & Gun" styles (Hero/Bounty Hunter).
*   Often overlooked, but has higher DPS than many rifles at close range.
