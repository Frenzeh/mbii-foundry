# 🛡️ Shield Projector

`MB_ATT_SHIELD_PROJ`

Determines the coverage and integrity of the Droideka's projected shield.

> **Class Specific:** Droideka.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Rank 1:** Basic coverage.
*   **Rank 2:** Improved integrity.
*   **Rank 3:** Maximum projection stability.

### 💡 Tips
*   Separate from `MB_ATT_DEKA_SHIELD` (which is raw HP). This affects the *quality* or specific mechanics of the projection? (Needs verification, often used in tandem).
