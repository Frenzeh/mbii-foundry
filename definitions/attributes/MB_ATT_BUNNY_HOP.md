# Bunny Hop

`MB_ATT_BUNNY_HOP`

Alters the physics of jumping to allow momentum conservation.

### Levels

* **Rank 1:**
 * **Style:** Siege/Base JKA.
 * **Effect:** No movement penalty when landing. No added acceleration on jump.
* **Rank 2:**
 * **Style:** FFA/Speed.
 * **Effect:** **Added acceleration** on jump + No landing penalty. Allows gaining speed by hopping.

### Usage
* **Speedsters:** Use Rank 2 for characters meant to race around the map (e.g. Sonic, Scout).
* **Normal:** Regular MBII physics apply a 45% speed penalty on landing. This attribute removes that.
