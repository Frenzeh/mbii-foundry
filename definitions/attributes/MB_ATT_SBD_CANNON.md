# SBD Arm Cannon

`MB_ATT_SBD_CANNON`

Integrated arm blaster for Super Battle Droids.

> **Class Specific:** SBD.

### Stats
* **Damage:** **28** (Direct Hit)
* **Splash Damage:** **30**
* **Splash Radius:** **100** units
* **Velocity:** 3500
* **Charge Time:** 600ms (Max)

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Basic blaster fire**.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Low power slap.
* **Level 2**:
 * **Increased fire rate** and accuracy.
 * **Alt-Fire**: "Knockdown" slap (slower).
* **Level 3**:
 * **High fire rate**.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Powerful cannon blast (consumes battery).

### Tips
* SBDs cannot pick up other weapons. This is their primary tool.
* The Alt-Fire cannon blast is powerful for suppressing groups.
