# Clone Rifle

`MB_ATT_CLONERIFLE`

DC-15A Blaster Rifle. The backbone of the Grand Army of the Republic.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Damage:** **24** (Low per hit)
* **Rate of Fire:** Very Fast (900 RPM?)
* **Velocity:** 4600
* **Ammo Cost:** 1

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Standard**. High rate of fire. Low damage.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Fires loaded special ammo (Blobs).

* **Level 2**:
 * **Improved**. Better accuracy and cooling.

* **Level 3**:
 * **Elite**. Maximum fire rate. No recoil?

### Tips
* Rely on volume of fire to suppress enemies.
* The real power comes from **Clone Blobs** (`MB_ATT_CLONEBLOBS`) or **Concussion Blobs** (`MB_ATT_STRONGBLOBS`).
