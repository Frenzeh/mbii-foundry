# Z-6 Minigun

`WP_MINIGUN`

> Rotary blaster cannon. Spin-up windup; sustained high-volume fire.

## What it does

The Minigun is a heavy automatic weapon that requires a brief spin-up before reaching full cadence. Once spinning, it pours bolts at 70-120ms cadence depending on rank. Heavy ammo drain, slow movement while firing, but devastating at sustained engagements.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Sustained automatic fire (spin-up required)
- **Fire rate** — 120ms (level 1) → 70ms (level 4)

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Same fire profile (no separate alt)
- **Fire rate** — 120ms (level 1) → 70ms (level 4)

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_MINIGUN`.
- Movement is slower while firing (heavy weapon penalty).
- Wookiee Heavy / Heavy Trooper signature pick.
