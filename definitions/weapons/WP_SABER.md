# Lightsaber

`WP_SABER`

The elegant weapon of a more civilized age.

### Damage Potential (Base)
* **Fast (Blue):** ~120
* **Medium (Yellow):** ~240
* **Strong (Red):** ~520 (Massive)
* **Staff/Duals:** ~260
* **Cyan:** ~120
* **Purple (Desann):** ~400

### Tips
* **Blue/Cyan:** Fast swings, low damage. Use for defense or whittling down opponents.
* **Red:** Slow, telegraphed swings. One hit kills almost anything.
* **Purple:** Heavy, wide swings. Good for crowd control.
* **Staff:** Excellent defense, rapid hits.
