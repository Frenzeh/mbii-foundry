# Assemble

`MB_ATT_ASSEMBLE`

Allows the soldier to respawn on a teammate who has the `Rally` attribute (e.g. Commander).

**Class Specific:** Soldier, Clone Trooper, Rebel Soldier.

### Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   When dead, click fire to cycle spawn points.
    *   Can spawn on any living Rally unit in a safe location.

### Notes
*   Vital for reinforcing the front lines without walking from spawn.
