# Melee

`WP_MELEE`

Hand-to-hand combat.

### Characteristics
*   **Punch**: Standard attack.
*   **Kick**: Alt-fire (if class allows).
*   **Wookiee**: With `MB_ATT_WOOKIE_STRENGTH`, this becomes a lethal weapon.

### Use Cases
*   Last resort for most classes.
*   Primary weapon for Wookiees and SBDs.
