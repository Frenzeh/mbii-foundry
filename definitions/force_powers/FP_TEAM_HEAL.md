# Team Heal

`FP_TEAM_HEAL`

> AoE radiant pulse that heals every nearby ally — team medic for Jedi.

## What it does

Emits a Force pulse that restores ~25 HP to allies within a radius around the user. Radius and efficiency scale with rank. Does **not** heal the caster — pair with `FP_HEAL` for self-sustain.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — radius 256, heals ~25 HP · *cost 50*
- **Level 2** — radius 384 · *cost 33*
- **Level 3** — radius 512 · *cost 25*

## Notes

- Light-side. Critical for any "support Jedi" loadout.
- Synergises with Team Energize for a full sustain bubble during pushes.
- Cooldown prevents spam — time it around team pushes or defends.
