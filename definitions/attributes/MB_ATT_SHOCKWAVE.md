# Shockwave

`MB_ATT_SHOCKWAVE`

> Defensive radial energy discharge.

## What it does

Activates `EAS_HI_SHOCKWAVE` — a panic-button radial knockback that pushes all enemies (and ground grenades) away from the caster. SBD-flavored anti-saberist tool. Costs 50 Battery, 1750 ms cooldown.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — short range.
- **Level 2** — medium range, faster recovery.
- **Level 3** — long range, biggest knockback.

## Notes

- Cost: 50 of class resource per use.
- Use against incoming saberists or with grenades at your feet — pushes both.
- Also disrupts cloaked enemies caught in the radius.
