# Stamina

`MB_ATT_STAMINA`

Determines the size of the "Stamina Bar" used for sprinting (`Shift`), wall-running, and acrobatics.

> **Default:** 0 (Base movement only).

### Levels

* **Level 1**: **Low**. Short sprints. Good for repositioning.
* **Level 2**: **Medium**. Extended sprints. Capable of tactical wall-runs.
* **Level 3**: **High**. Athletic marathon runner. Can sprint across large map sections.

### Tips
* **Heavy Armor** drains stamina faster.
* Required for **ARC Troopers** (`MB_ATT_DEXTERITY`) to perform advanced rolls and flips.
* If you have **Dexterity 3** but **Stamina 0**, you can perform moves but will tire out instantly.
