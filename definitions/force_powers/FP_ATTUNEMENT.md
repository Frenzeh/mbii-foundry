# Force Attunement

`FP_ATTUNEMENT`

> Resizes your Force Pool — more FP to spend, more power to channel.

## What it does

Passive multiplier on the user's maximum Force Point pool. Higher ranks give you more FP to burn before you run dry; lower ranks constrain how often you can cast big-cost powers like Destruction or Grip.

## Per level

- **Level 0** — 80 FP total pool
- **Level 1** — 100 FP (baseline)
- **Level 2** — 120 FP
- **Level 3** — 140 FP

## Notes

- Does not change regen rate — that's `MB_ATT_FP_MULTIPLIER` territory.
- Every Force-heavy build wants this at 2 or 3.
- Costs are set at the class/FA level, not per-use.
