# DL-44 Blaster Pistol

`WP_BRYAR_PISTOL`

> Standard sidearm for most classes. Reliable, accurate, with a chargeable alt-fire.

## What it does

The DL-44 is the default pistol most loadouts get a copy of. Primary is a steady single-shot tap; secondary holds a charge and releases a heavier round whose damage scales with `MB_ATT_PISTOL` rank. The pistol is intentionally accurate and forgiving — a fall-back when your primary runs dry.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 34 (level 1) up to 50 (level 3)
- **Velocity** — 6325 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 1
- **Fire rate** — 500ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Charged shot (`CHGE_PISTOL`)
- **Damage** — up to 62 (level 1) / 75 (level 3) at full charge
- **Fire rate** — 800ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_PISTOL` (rank determines damage cap and unlocked features).
- Force drain (Close/Far): 13/9 primary and secondary; blocking 11/6.
- Backup weapon by design — almost every kit has it.
