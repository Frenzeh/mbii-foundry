# 🔫 A280 Rifle

`MB_ATT_A280`

Heavy Blaster Rifle used by the Rebel Alliance. Hard-hitting but slower firing.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Damage:** **35**
*   **Velocity:** 8075 (Fast)
*   **Ammo Cost:** 2

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Standard**. Good damage per shot.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: Burst fire (3 shots).

*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Improved**. Faster refire rate.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: Tighter burst spread.

*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Elite**. High accuracy.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: 🎯 **Sniper Mode**. High power, long range shots.

### 💡 Tips
*   Similar to the E-11 but trades fire rate for raw stopping power per bolt.
*   Velocity is significantly higher than E-11 (8075 vs 6325), making it easier to hit moving targets.
