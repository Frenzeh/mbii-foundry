# Droideka Power

`MB_ATT_DEKA_POWER`

Main reactor output, affecting firepower and shield recharge.

> **Class Specific:** Droideka.

### Stats
* **Blaster Damage:** **25** (per shot)
* **Primary Velocity:** 4025
* **Shield Recharge Rate:** **21** per tick.
* **Discharge Cost:** **200** (initial) / **50** (per rank reduction).

### Levels

* **Level 1**: **Standard**. Normal fire rate. Normal shield recharge.
* **Level 2**: **High Output**. Increased fire rate. Faster shield recharge.
* **Level 3**: **Overcharge**. **Rapid-fire** blasters. **Fastest** shield recharge. Increased damage per bolt.

### Tips
* Level 3 is essential for "DPS Deka" builds that want to melt enemies before their shields drop.
* The Discharge cost of the `MB_ATT_SHOCKWAVE` is also reduced by this attribute.
