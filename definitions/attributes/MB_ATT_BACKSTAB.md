# Backstab

`MB_ATT_BACKSTAB`

> Bonus damage when striking an unaware target from behind.

## What it does

Multiplies damage when the user lands a hit on an enemy's back ‚Äî typically saber or knife strikes. The bonus only applies when the target's facing puts them within the rear-arc cone.

## Per level

- **Level 1** ‚Äî small bonus multiplier.
- **Level 2** ‚Äî larger bonus.
- **Level 3** ‚Äî full assassin damage; can one-shot unaware targets.

## Notes

- Requires being in the rear arc; the angle is checked at impact, not at swing start.
- Pairs with `MB_ATT_STEALTH` and `MB_ATT_CLOAK` for ambush builds.
- Saber `lungeAtkMove` and Tavion-style `LS_A_BACK` benefit most.
