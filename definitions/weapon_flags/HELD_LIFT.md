# Lifts

`HELD_LIFT`

> Adds an upward knockback ("lift") on hit.

## What it does

Knocks the target into the air on a successful hit. Functions like a vertical-component knockback, useful for designing weapons that punish ground-bound targets.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Pairs naturally with weapons that already have knockdown / disrupt patterns.
- Distinct from `HELD_KNOCKBACK` (horizontal push) — this is the vertical lift.
