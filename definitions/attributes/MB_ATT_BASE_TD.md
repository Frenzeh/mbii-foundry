# Base Thermal Detonator

`MB_ATT_BASE_TD`

> Standard thermal detonator grenade attribute. The basic-issue TD.

## What it does

Grants the base `WP_THERMAL` thermal detonator — a 3-second-fuse timed grenade with hard splash damage. The "basic" version of the TD attribute, distinct from `MB_ATT_THERMALS` which represents the Real TD (canon-accurate nuke variant).

## Per level

- **Level 1** — One thermal detonator.
- **Level 2** — Two TDs.
- **Level 3** — Three TDs.

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_THERMAL`.
- Distinct from `MB_ATT_THERMALS` (which maps to `WP_REAL_TD`).
- Cook the fuse by holding fire to shorten detonation time.
