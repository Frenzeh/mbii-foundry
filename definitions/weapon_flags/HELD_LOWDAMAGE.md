# Low Damage

`HELD_LOWDAMAGE`

> Reduces weapon output, often for training or non-lethal variants.

## What it does

Reduces weapon output, often for training or non-lethal variants.

## Valid values

- **Multiplier:** **0.5x** (50% Less Damage).
- **Scope:** Applies to base damage and Force Drain.

## Notes

- Combine with `HELD_POISON` or `HELD_FLAME` to create "Status Effect" weapons that debuff rather than kill directly.
- Used on the "Shock Baton" (Soldier Melee).
