# 💥 Shockwave

`MB_ATT_SHOCKWAVE`

A defensive discharge of energy.

> **Class Specific:** Droideka.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Trigger:** Special Class Ability.
*   **Effect:** Releases a 360-degree shockwave.
*   **Result:** Knocks back enemies, deflects grenades/rockets.
*   **Cost:** Drains Shield/Battery.

### 💡 Tips
*   **Panic Button:** Use when a Jedi gets too close or grenades land under you.
