# Trip Mine

`WP_TRIP_MINE`

> Laser-actuated wall-stick mine. Place, beam-trip, detonate.

## What it does

Trip mines stick to walls and surfaces and emit an invisible laser tripwire. Anyone who breaks the beam triggers the detonation. Primary throws/places the mine; secondary lobs it further. Long arming delay before the laser is live, then the mine sits as area denial until tripped or the placer dies.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Place mine (`CHGE_GRENADE`)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Lob mine
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_TRIP_MINES`.
- Sticks to walls and ceilings — set traps in alleys and around objectives.
- Engineer, Saboteur, and Demolition kits.
