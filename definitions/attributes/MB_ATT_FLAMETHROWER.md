# Flamethrower

`MB_ATT_FLAMETHROWER`

> Heavy flamethrower attribute. Sustained fire stream for area denial.

## What it does

Grants a heavy flamethrower weapon — sustained fire stream that delivers high tick damage at short range. Distinct from the wrist-mounted variant (`MB_ATT_WPFLAMETHROWER`); this is a full main-weapon flamethrower used by certain Heavy Mercenary and Imperial Pyro kits.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Standard flame stream; short range.
- **Level 2** — Longer effective range; higher tick damage.
- **Level 3** — Maximum range and damage; widest cone.

## Notes

- Distinct from `MB_ATT_WPFLAMETHROWER` (wrist-mounted Mandalorian variant).
- Pairs conceptually with `MB_ATT_THROWER_FLAME` (element-swap on the broader Thrower).
- Devastating vs Jedi/Sith who close to melee.
- Heavy fuel/ammo drain — sustain isn't free.
