# Force Repulse

`EAS_FP_REPULSE`

> Activates Force Repulse — a 360-degree push.

## What it does

Triggers a radial Push pulse centered on the caster, hitting every enemy in range simultaneously. Unlike normal Push (cone), Repulse is omnidirectional — useful when surrounded or for breaking up melee scrums.

## Notes

- Force cost and knockback live on `MB_ATT_FP_REPULSE`.
- Bound on the FP_PUSH skill tree — players need both the attribute and the special bind.
- Pairs well with crowded chokepoints; wasted in 1v1.
