# Quick Throw

`MB_ATT_QUICKTHROW`

> Throw grenades instantly without switching weapons.

## What it does

Adds the ability to throw the currently-selected grenade type without holstering your primary weapon. Press Special Class Ability 1 (or bound key) to lob a grenade mid-firefight. Critical for combos: shoot → throw → shoot.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Quickthrow enabled. Standard throw velocity.
- **Level 2** — Higher throw velocity and range; shorter animation.

## Notes

- Requires grenades in inventory (`MB_ATT_FRAGS`, `MB_ATT_THERMALS`, etc.).
- Critical for combo gameplay — chains gunfire with explosives.
- Soldier, Hero, Mercenary loadouts; especially valuable on Mandalorian.
