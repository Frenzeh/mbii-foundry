# Flipkick

`MB_ATT_FLIPKICK`

A powerful acrobatic kick that knocks down opponents.

> **Default:** Disabled.

### Levels

* **Rank 1:**
 * **Damage:** 9.
 * **Effect:** Can knock down opponents (based on opponent's strength).
* **Rank 2:**
 * **Damage:** 12.
 * **Effect:** Higher chance of knockdown.
* **Rank 3:**
 * **Damage:** 15.
 * **Effect:** Guaranteed knockdown against most targets.

### Tips
* **Mechanism:** Double jump off an opponent.
* **Counter:** Blocked by high strength (`MB_ATT_WOOKIE_STRENGTH`) or Force Block.
