# Grapple Hook

`MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOOK`

> Equips the character with a wrist grappling hook.

## What it does

Unlocks the grapple line — a fast vertical-traversal tool. Hits the hook target and reels the player in. Higher ranks add range, reel speed, and at top tier the ability to pull enemies toward you instead.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — basic hook, short range.
- **Level 2** — improved hook, medium range, faster reel-in.
- **Level 3** — long range; can pull enemies into melee.

## Notes

- Bound to `EAS_HI_GRAPPLEHOOK`.
- Pairs with `MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOP` for chained grapples without ground contact.
- Stock kit requires Melee weapon active to swing the line.
