# Banking Speed

`bankingSpeed`

> How quickly the vehicle rolls/tilts into turns.

## What it does

How quickly the vehicle rolls/tilts into turns.

## Valid values

- **Visual:** Controls the leaning animation.
- **Feel:** High banking makes the vehicle feel agile/light. Low feels rigid/heavy.

## Notes

- Critical for the "Swoop Bike" feel.
