# Block Regen Amount

`MB_ATT_BLOCK_REGEN_AMOUNT`

> Per-tick Block Points restored.

## What it does

Sets how many BP are restored each regen tick. Lets duelist classes recover from sustained pressure faster than the default. Important for Strong-style users who burn BP fast on their own swings.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small amount per tick.
- **Level 2** — medium amount per tick.
- **Level 3** — large amount per tick.

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_BLOCK_REGEN_RATE` and `MB_ATT_BLOCK_REGEN_CAP`.
- Stacks with `MB_ATT_FP_SABER_DEFENSE` rank-based BP pool.
