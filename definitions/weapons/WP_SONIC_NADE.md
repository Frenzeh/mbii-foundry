# Sonic Detonator

`WP_SONIC_NADE`

> Sonic stun grenade. Disorients targets in the blast zone instead of dealing damage.

## What it does

A timed-cook grenade that detonates with a sonic shockwave — disorients hit targets (screen blur, control disruption) rather than killing them. Specialized control tool — pair with frag/thermal follow-ups for the actual kill.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Throw (`CHGE_GRENADE`)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Lobbed throw
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_SONIC_DETONATOR`.
- Stun/disorient instead of kill — best as a setup tool.
- Useful versus Force users to break their concentration.
