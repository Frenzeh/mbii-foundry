# DLT-20A

`WP_DLT20A`

> Long-range Rebel marksman rifle. High velocity, accurate single shots.

## What it does

The DLT-20A is the Rebellion's long-range workhorse — a slower-cycling rifle with very high projectile velocity (8075) that lets it reach across maps with minimal lead. Mode-cycle weapon: secondary swaps to a scoped marksman cadence at level 2+.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 31
- **Velocity** — 8075 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 300ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Scoped marksman shot (`ZOOM_PROJ`)
- **Ammo cost** — 20
- **Fire rate** — 1250ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_DLT20A`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 16/11 primary, 11/8 secondary; blocking 13/7 / 10/6.
- Excellent range and velocity — best Rebel mid-long lane option.
