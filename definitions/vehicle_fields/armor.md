# Armor (Vehicle)

`armor`

> Hull integrity of the vehicle.

## What it does

Hull integrity of the vehicle.

## Valid values

- **Health:** When this hits 0, the vehicle explodes.
- **Resistance:** Vehicles generally resist small arms fire better than players.

## Notes

- **Speeder Bike:** ~200-400.
- **Tank:** ~2000+.
