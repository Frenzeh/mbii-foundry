# Trip Mines

`MB_ATT_TRIP_MINES`

> Laser-actuated wall-stick mines. Place, beam-trip, detonate.

## What it does

Grants `WP_TRIP_MINE`. Mines stick to walls and surfaces and emit an invisible laser tripwire — anyone who breaks the beam triggers detonation. Long arming delay before the laser is live, then the mine sits as area denial until tripped, shot, or the placer dies.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — One trip mine.
- **Level 2** — Two trip mines.
- **Level 3** — Three trip mines.

## Stats

- **Damage** — 100+ (lethal in radius)
- **Trigger radius** — Small (laser-line)
- **Adhesion** — Sticks to walls, ceilings, floors

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_TRIP_MINE`.
- Excellent for securing flanks and objective points.
- Can be shot by enemies to clear safely (but they take splash).
- Engineer, Saboteur, Demolition kits.
