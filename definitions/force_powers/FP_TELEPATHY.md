# Mind Trick

`FP_TELEPATHY`

> Cloud the minds of those around you — go invisible until you draw steel.

## What it does

Wraps the user in mind-trick invisibility. You become translucent or entirely unseen depending on rank. The effect breaks the instant you ignite your saber, fire a weapon, or take damage. Also hides you from the radar.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — translucent silhouette, visible up close · *cost 50*
- **Level 2** — near-invisible at range, still visible under scrutiny · *cost 30*
- **Level 3** — fully invisible; strongest against Sense · *cost 30*

## Notes

- Counter: Force Sense (`FP_SEE`) pierces Mind Trick at matching or higher rank.
- Breaks on saber ignition, firing, or taking damage — travel carefully.
- Best used for flanks, objective grabs, and escapes.
