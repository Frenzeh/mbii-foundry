# 💥 Frag Grenade

`MB_ATT_FRAGS`

Standard fragmentation grenade.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Direct Hit Damage:** **100**
*   **Splash Damage:** **100**
*   **Splash Radius:** **256** units
*   **Fuse Time:** ~3 seconds.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Single Grenade**. Standard blast.
*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Two Grenades**.
*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Three Grenades**.

### 💡 Tips
*   Good all-purpose explosive.
*   Can be used with `MB_ATT_QUICKTHROW` for rapid deployment.
