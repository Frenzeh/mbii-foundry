# Strong Style

`SS_STRONG`

A lightsaber style focused on raw power and breaking defenses.

### Characteristics
* **Speed**: Slow, telegraphed swings.
* **Damage**: High damage (can 1-hit kill).
* **Range**: Long range.
* **Defense**: High block drain (inefficient blocking).
* **Special**: Breaks through weak blocks.

### Use Cases
* Aggressive duelists.
* Crushing turtle players.
