# Thrower (Element Stream)

`MB_ATT_THROWER`

> CR-24 stream weapon. Continuous-beam family with elemental variants.

## What it does

Grants `WP_THROWER` — a continuous-stream weapon firing at 50ms cadence (effectively a constant beam). Damage type varies by which thrower-element attribute is also bought (`MB_ATT_THROWER_LIGHTNING/ICE/POISON/PLASMA/FLAME`). Drains battery while firing.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Basic stream; short range and low tick damage.
- **Level 2** — Improved range and tick rate.
- **Level 3** — Max range and damage; full continuous beam.

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_THROWER`.
- Pair with one or more `MB_ATT_THROWER_*` element attributes for damage type.
- See also `MB_ATT_FLAMETHROWER` and `MB_ATT_WPFLAMETHROWER`.
- Uses `THROWER_ANIM_SET`. Heavy Mercenary / Sith Cultist kits.
