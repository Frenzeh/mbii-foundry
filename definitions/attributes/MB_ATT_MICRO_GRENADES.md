# Micro Grenades

`MB_ATT_MICRO_GRENADES`

> Compact concussion grenade attribute. Smaller blast, faster throw.

## What it does

Grants `WP_CONC_NADE` — the small-radius timed grenade in the "micro grenade" family. Lower damage than a thermal but throws fast and lets you pre-empt routes with minimal commitment. Both fire modes use a `CHGE_TIMED` cook.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Two micro grenades.
- **Level 2** — Three micro grenades.
- **Level 3** — Four micro grenades; tightest cook timing.

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_CONC_NADE`.
- Smaller blast than `WP_THERMAL` / `WP_FRAG_NADE`.
- Pick this when you need volume over punch.
