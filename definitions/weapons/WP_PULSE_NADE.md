# Pulse Grenade

`WP_PULSE_NADE`

> EMP grenade. Disables droids, drains battery, scrambles electronics.

## What it does

A timed-cook grenade that, instead of damage, releases an EMP burst on detonation — disables droids, drains SBD battery, and disrupts shielded targets. Limited damage versus organics; specialized utility tool.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Throw (`CHGE_GRENADE`)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Lobbed throw (`CHGE_GRENADE`)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_PULSE_GRENADES`.
- Strong against SBDs, Droidekas, Magna Guards, and shielded targets.
- Pair with DEMP-2 for full anti-droid loadout.
