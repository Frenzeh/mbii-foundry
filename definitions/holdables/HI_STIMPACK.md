# 💉 Stimpak

`HI_STIMPACK`

Combat stimulant injector.

> **Uses:** 1-time use.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Health:** Grants **+30 HP** (Overheal possible).
*   **Speed:** Increases movement speed by **1.2x**.
*   **Duration:** Effects last **30 seconds**.
*   **Fade:** Extra health is lost after duration expires.
*   **Override:** Overrides existing Rally buffs (does not stack).

### 💡 Tips
*   Use before a push or to escape a bad situation.
*   Locked in animation briefly upon use.
