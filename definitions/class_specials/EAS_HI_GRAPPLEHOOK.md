# 🧗 Grapple Hook

`EAS_HI_GRAPPLEHOOK`

Fires the grappling hook.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Trigger:** Press Special Key (hold to reel in).
*   **Effect:** Fires a hook. If it hits a surface, pulls the player towards it.
*   **Combat:** Hitting an enemy pulls them to you ("Get over here!").
*   **Requirement:** `MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOOK`.

### 💡 Tips
*   Must have Melee weapon active to use.
