# DEMP-2 Pulse Rifle

`WP_DEMP2`

> Anti-droid pulse rifle. Standard primary; chargeable EMP burst secondary.

## What it does

The DEMP-2 trades raw damage versus organics for devastating effect against droids — SBDs and Droidekas in particular. Primary fires a steady ion shot; secondary holds for a chargeable EMP discharge that can stun and strip droid shields.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Single ion bolt
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 500ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Charged EMP (`CHGE_CLONE`)
- **Ammo cost** — 3
- **Fire rate** — 900ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_DEMP2`.
- Devastating against SBDs and Droidekas — staple anti-droid pick.
- Damage vs organics is below average; bring a backup pistol.
