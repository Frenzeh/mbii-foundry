# Heavy Pistol

`MB_ATT_HEAVY_PISTOL`

Powerful, high-caliber blaster pistol.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Damage:** **55** (Primary & Secondary)
* **Primary Fire Rate:** 500ms
* **Primary Velocity:** 4000
* **Secondary Fire Rate:** 900ms
* **Clip Size:** 15.

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Standard**. High damage per shot.
* **Level 2**:
 * **Accuracy**: Improved.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Fire Rate**: Faster.

### Tips
* A hard-hitting sidearm, capable of quickly dispatching enemies.
* Good for a "Sheriff" or "Lawman" type character.
