# Concussion Rifle

`WP_CONCUSSION`

> Trandoshan heavy rifle. Hard-hitting concussive shots; energy-beam secondary.

## What it does

The Concussion Rifle fires a slow, splash-damaging concussive bolt as primary — heavy single-shot impact. The secondary unleashes a hitscan beam dealing rapid sustained damage in a straight line. Big drains, slow cadence, very lethal in close lanes.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Concussive bolt (splash)
- **Ammo cost** — 10
- **Fire rate** — 800ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Sustained beam
- **Ammo cost** — 20
- **Fire rate** — 1200ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_CONCUSSION`.
- Splash damage means it punishes grouped targets and can catch you in your own blast at point-blank.
- Bossk's signature weapon; also available on heavy Mercenary loadouts.
