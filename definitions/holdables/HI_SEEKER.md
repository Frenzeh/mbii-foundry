# 🛸 Seeker Drone

`HI_SEEKER`

Automated defense drone.

> **Uses:** 1-time deployment.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Behavior:** Orbits the player.
*   **Combat:** Slowly fires at enemies. Has physical collision (can block shots).
*   **Duration:** Destroys itself after X seconds, X damage taken, or X shots fired.
*   **Death:** Explodes when destroyed, dealing damage to nearby enemies.

### 💡 Tips
*   Good for distraction and minor DPS.
