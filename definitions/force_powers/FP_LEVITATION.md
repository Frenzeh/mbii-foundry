# Force Jump

`FP_LEVITATION`

> Jump higher than any mortal and take zero fall damage from the drop.

## What it does

Enhances vertical leap, horizontal trajectory and mid-air control. Each jump drains a small amount of FP; the height and air control available scale with rank. Also reduces fall damage on landing.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — basic boosted jump, short hops · *cost 10*
- **Level 2** — higher leap, noticeably better air control · *cost 10*
- **Level 3** — maximum height, full air control, wall runs · *cost 10*

## Notes

- Almost universal for Jedi/Sith — without it, you're grounded.
- Interacts with saber specials (e.g. Red DFA, Yellow lunge) that require a minimum jump rank.
- Pairs with acrobatics for roll distance and kick variants.
