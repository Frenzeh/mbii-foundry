# Saber Offense

`FP_SABER_OFFENSE`

> Raw lightsaber skill — unlocks styles, governs attack speed.

## What it does

Passive. Determines which saber styles the character can wield and scales their baseline attack speed. Without at least Rank 1, a Jedi cannot meaningfully fight with a saber.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Medium (Yellow) style, baseline attack speed · *cost 0*
- **Level 2** — Fast (Blue) style unlocked, slightly faster swings · *cost 0*
- **Level 3** — Strong (Red) style unlocked; Dual/Staff/Desann/Tavion available where granted · *cost 0*

## Notes

- Does not consume FP — it is a passive skill gate.
- Style availability also depends on per-style attribute grants (`MB_ATT_SS_*`).
- Rank 3 is standard for any duelist build.
