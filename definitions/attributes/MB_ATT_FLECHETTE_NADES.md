# Flechette Mines

`MB_ATT_FLECHETTE_NADES`

> Bouncing explosive mines fired from the Flechette Launcher alt-fire.

## What it does

Adds the bouncing-mine alt-fire ammunition pool to `WP_FLECHETTE`. The mines bounce until they come to rest, then arm and detonate on proximity or after a short fuse. Without this, the flechette is shotgun-only.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Two flechette mines.
- **Level 2** — Three flechette mines.
- **Level 3** — Four flechette mines; longer arming distance.

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_FLECHETTE` (the base weapon).
- Mines bounce — useful for indirect placements around corners.
- Mercenary, Mandalorian, Bounty Hunter kits.
