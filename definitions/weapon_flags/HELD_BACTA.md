# Bacta Heal

`HELD_BACTA`

> Hit allies (or self) receive bacta-style heal-over-time.

## What it does

Hits trigger bacta-like heal-over-time on the target. On allies this functions as a healing weapon; on self-shots / area weapons it functions as personal sustain.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Pairs with medic / support archetypes.
- Behavior with hostile targets depends on damage flags — most `HELD_BACTA` weapons are coded to be friendly-only.
