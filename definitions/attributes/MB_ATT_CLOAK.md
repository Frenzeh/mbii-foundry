# Cloak

`MB_ATT_CLOAK`

> Pool size and effectiveness of the personal cloaking generator.

## What it does

Configures the cloak resource pool and recharge for the `HI_CLOAK` holdable. Higher ranks give longer cloak duration and faster recharge between activations.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — short cloak window.
- **Level 2** — medium duration with faster recharge.
- **Level 3** — long duration, near-passive recharge.

## Notes

- Sibling of the holdable `HI_CLOAK`.
- Firing or taking damage breaks cloak immediately.
- Tracking Darts (`MB_ATT_TRACKING_DART`) reveal cloaked players to the team — counterplay for Bounty Hunters.
