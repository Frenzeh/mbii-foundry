# 🕵️ Spy Disguise

`MB_ATT_SPY_DISGUISE`

Allows the Spy to impersonate enemy units.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**: **Grunt**. Can disguise as basic enemy soldiers. Weapon is hidden (appears to hold enemy weapon).
*   **Level 2**: **Elite**. Can disguise as elite units (e.g. Commander, ARC). Harder to detect visually.
*   **Level 3**: **Infiltrator**. Can disguise as **Jedi/Sith**. Mimics saber sounds and blocking animations. Only detected by touch or attacking.

### 💡 Tips
*   **Attacking breaks the disguise** instantly.
*   Jedi/Sith with **Force Sense** (`MB_ATT_FP_SEE`) can see through disguises (red aura).
