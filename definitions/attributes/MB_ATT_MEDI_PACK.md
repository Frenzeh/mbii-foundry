# Medi Pack

`MB_ATT_MEDI_PACK`

> Self-use HP refill consumable.

## What it does

Lets the user heal themselves from a personal Medi Pack inventory. Self-targeting only.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small heal per pack.
- **Level 2** — medium heal per pack.
- **Level 3** — large heal per pack.

## Notes

- Charges are personal; cannot be transferred to teammates.
- Sibling of `MB_ATT_AMMO_PACK` (self-ammo) and `MB_ATT_BACTA` (medpac holdable).
- Useful for self-sufficient flanker builds.
