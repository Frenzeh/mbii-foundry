# Sith

`MB_CLASS_SITH`

> Imperial Force user — saber + dark-side powers. 10% universal damage reduction.

## Role

Imperial mirror of Jedi. Identical chassis (100 HP, 225 speed, no armor pool) but defaults weight dark-side powers — Grip, Lightning, Rage, Drain, Deadly Sight. Functions as the Imperial team's melee anchor and force-supremacy piece.

## Default loadout

- **Base health** — 100
- **Base speed** — 225.0
- **Armor pool** — `NO_ARMOR_POOL`
- **Melee moves** — All
- **Force-sensitive** — yes
- **Resource** — Force
- **Abilities (CS1-4)** — `EAS_FP_SABERTHROW`, `EAS_MELEE`, `EAS_FP_GRIP`, `EAS_FP_LIGHTNING`
- **Abilities (CS5-8)** — `EAS_FP_REPULSE`, none, none, none
- **Forcecfg** — `forcecfg/blue/sith`
- **Model** — `reborn` (skin `forceuser`)

## Signature mechanics

- 10% universal damage reduction (same as Jedi).
- Dark-side offensive kit — Grip immobilizes, Lightning drains force pools and stuns, Drain steals HP/FP.
- Shares BP and Force pool mechanics with Jedi.

## Counters

- Jedi in the mirror.
- Wookiee Strength 3 ignores Grip (except Crush) and resists knockback.
- Sustained rifle fire to crack BP.
