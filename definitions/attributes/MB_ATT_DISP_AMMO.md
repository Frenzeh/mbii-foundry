# Ammo Dispenser

`MB_ATT_DISP_AMMO`

> Hold-to-resupply allies — passive ammo transfer.

## What it does

Hold the Use key while looking at an ally to restore their ammo every 250 ms. Used for keeping gunner teammates fed through a long firefight.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small ammo per tick.
- **Level 2** — medium ammo per tick.
- **Level 3** — large ammo per tick.

## Notes

- Tick interval is fixed at 250 ms.
- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DROP_AMMO` (drop a pickup) and `MB_ATT_STIM_AMMO` (single-target instant).
- Restores all weapon ammo proportionally on the recipient.
