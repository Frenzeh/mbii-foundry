# Force Protect

`FP_PROTECT`

> Wrap yourself in a damage-absorbing bubble — tank rockets, shrug off blasters.

## What it does

Activates a defensive aura that reduces incoming damage while ticking FP per second. Does not stop saber hits from draining BP (you still parry normally) but heavily mitigates blaster, explosive, and other ranged damage.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small damage reduction, highest drain · *cost 50*
- **Level 2** — moderate reduction, lower drain · *cost 25*
- **Level 3** — best reduction, most efficient · *cost 10*

## Notes

- Light-side. Pairs with Heal to make a Jedi very hard to kill.
- Does not block direct-damage one-shots from things like cortosis/sniper headshots.
- Breaks on activating/swinging lightsaber in some modes.
