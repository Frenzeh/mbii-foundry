# ⬆ High Damage

`HELD_HIGHDAMAGE`

Significantly amplifies the weapon's energy output.

### Mechanics
* **Multiplier:** **2.0x** (100% More Damage).
* **Scope:** Applies to base damage.
* **Force:** Also doubles Force Drain effectiveness (on Drain-based weapons).

### Tips
* Use with caution. Can turn a fast-firing rifle into an instant-kill machine.
* Good for "Sniper" variants of standard rifles.
