# 💪 Damage Taken Multiplier

`MB_ATT_DMG_TAKEN_MULTIPLIER`

Multiplies all incoming damage.

> **Default:** 1.0

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **1.0:** Normal damage taken.
*   **0.9:** 10% Less damage taken.
*   **1.1:** 10% More damage taken.

### 💡 Tips
*   Jedi typically have a hidden 0.9 (10% damage reduction) in their base class.
*   Use this to fine-tune the durability of a class.
