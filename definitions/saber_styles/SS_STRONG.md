# Red (Strong)

`SS_STRONG`

> Power style — slow, telegraphed, devastating.

## What it does

The Djem So / Red / "Strong" stance. Slowest startup of any style but the highest damage per swing — landed cleanly, a Red swing can one-shot an unblocking target. Long reach makes it the king of poke trades when used correctly.

## Notes

- High BP cost on the wielder's *own* block — if the opponent counters mid-swing, you bleed BP fast.
- Breaks weak blocks and poorly-timed parries; punishes BP-starved opponents.
- Counters Medium on poke trades; vulnerable to Fast on interrupt and Staff on close-in scrambles.
