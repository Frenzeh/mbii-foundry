# Pulse

`HELD_PULSE`

> Projectiles electrify targets.

## What it does

Projectiles electrify targets.

## Valid values

- **Effect:** Applies Pulse Grenade effect.
- **Drain:** Drains half the magazine of enemy weapons (vs full drain of grenade).

## Notes

- Anti-Droid/Anti-Gunner disruption.
