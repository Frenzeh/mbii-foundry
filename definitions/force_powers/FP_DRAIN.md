# Force Drain

`FP_DRAIN`

> Vampire the life and Force out of your enemies.

## What it does

Siphons HP (and at higher ranks, FP and stamina) from a target, healing the user for a portion of what is drained. Target must be in line of sight and within range. Costs a small amount of FP per tick; net effect is a strong sustain if your target has HP to give.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — single-target HP drain · *cost 10*
- **Level 2** — adds FP drain · *cost 10*
- **Level 3** — cone/multi-target; drains HP, FP, and stamina · *cost 10*

## Notes

- Sith counterpart to Heal — vampiric instead of self-cast.
- Countered by Absorb (Jedi absorbs the drain).
- Anti-Jedi: stripping their FP shuts down their kit.
