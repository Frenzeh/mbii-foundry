# Force Stasis

`FP_STASIS`

> Freeze a target in place — a snapshot prison they can't move out of.

## What it does

Locks a target in a frozen stasis state, preventing movement and attacks for a short duration. Cost is paid on activation and the effect respects a cooldown. Rank raises duration and/or resistance to being broken.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — short lock · *cost 20*
- **Level 2** — medium lock · *cost 20*
- **Level 3** — long lock · *cost 20*

## Notes

- Countered by Absorb; some damage types may break stasis early.
- High-skill setup tool — freeze, then land a Destruction, saber hit, or teammate shot.
