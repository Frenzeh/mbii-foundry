# Force Absorb

`MB_ATT_FP_ABSORB`

> Attribute for absorbing hostile Force powers and converting them to FP.

## What it does

Grants access to `FP_ABSORB`. Activates a shield that intercepts incoming Force powers and returns a portion of their energy as FP. Rank governs which powers it can absorb and how much FP it returns.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — 10 FP per absorption, catches Push and Pull · *cost 50*
- **Level 2** — 15 FP per absorption, catches Grip and Lightning (ranks 1/2) · *cost 25*
- **Level 3** — 20 FP per absorption, catches almost all Force powers · *cost 10*

## Notes

- Light-side. The hard counter to Force-heavy Sith loadouts.
- Must be **active** (toggled on) — unlike passive Force Block.
- Does nothing against sabers, blasters, or explosives.
