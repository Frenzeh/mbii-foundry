# T-21 Heavy Blaster

`MB_ATT_T21`

A devastating repeating blaster with high damage and splash.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Primary Damage:** **78**
* **Secondary Damage:** **34**
* **Velocity:** 5600
* **Ammo Cost:** 8 per shot.

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Heavy**. Slow movement while firing.
 * **Damage**. High per-shot damage.

* **Level 2**:
 * **Efficiency**. Better ammo consumption.

* **Level 3**:
 * **Master**. Faster movement? (Check `HELD_SLOW` flag).
 * **Secondary**: Rapid fire suppression mode.

### Tips
* Primary fire deals massive damage (78!), capable of 2-shotting standard soldiers.
* Very high ammo cost.
