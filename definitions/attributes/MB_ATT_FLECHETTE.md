# Flechette Launcher

`MB_ATT_FLECHETTE`

Fires a burst of shrapnel or a bouncing explosive ball.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Primary Damage:** **12** (per pellet, 5 pellets = **60 total**)
* **Alt-Fire Damage:** **20-75** (Explosive ball)
* **Velocity:** 6325
* **Ammo Cost:** 1 (Primary) / 15 (Alt-Fire)

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Primary**: Short-range shotgun blast.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Bouncing explosive ball.
* **Level 2**:
 * **Damage**: Increased.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Spread**: Tighter primary spread.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Stronger explosion.

### Tips
* Versatile weapon for close to medium range.
* Alt-Fire can clear rooms or hit enemies around corners.
