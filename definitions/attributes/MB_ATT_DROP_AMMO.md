# Ammo Drop

`MB_ATT_DROP_AMMO`

> Drops a one-shot ammo pickup on the ground.

## What it does

Spawns an Ammo pickup at the user's feet that any ally can grab. Fire-and-forget version of the dispenser.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small ammo per pickup.
- **Level 2** — medium ammo per pickup.
- **Level 3** — large ammo per pickup.

## Notes

- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DISP_AMMO` and `MB_ATT_STIM_AMMO`.
- Charge count tied to `MB_ATT_SUPPLYDROP`.
- Useful for "drop and reposition" plays mid-firefight.
