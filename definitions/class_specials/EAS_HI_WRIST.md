# Wrist Laser

`EAS_HI_WRIST`

> Fires the precision wrist-mounted laser.

## What it does

Beam-fire alternative to the wrist flamethrower. Tight, low-spread bolt for picking off targets at range without committing to a primary weapon swap. Mandalorian gauntlet toggle (`SMBF_GAUNTLET_MODE`) swaps between this and `EAS_HI_FLAME` on the same key.

## Notes

- 500 ms cooldown — the fastest wrist option.
- Damage and ammo from `MB_ATT_WRISTLASER`.
- Mandalorian-flavored; toggles between flame and laser via the gauntlet command.
