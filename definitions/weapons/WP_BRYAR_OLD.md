# Bryar Pistol (Old)

`WP_BRYAR_OLD`

> Classic-style Bryar pistol. Single-shot reliable sidearm with a charged alt.

## What it does

The "old" Bryar is the long-running Jedi Knight series sidearm in MBII. It functions like the standard pistol but uses a different feel and ammo lane (Westar pool). Reliable mid-damage single shots; the charged secondary delivers a heavier round at higher rank.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — ~34 (standard pistol-tier)
- **Velocity** — 6325 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 500ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Charged Mando-style shot (`CHGE_MANDO`) at level 2+
- **Fire rate** — 800ms
- **Notes** — Higher damage on hold-to-charge

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_BRYAR_OLD`.
- Shares the Westar ammo pool (`AMMO_WESTAR`).
- Used on classic-era characters and select Hero loadouts.
