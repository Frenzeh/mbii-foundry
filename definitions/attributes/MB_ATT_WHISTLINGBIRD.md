# Whistling Birds

`MB_ATT_WHISTLINGBIRD`

> Miniature seeking-missile launcher. Mandalorian wrist gauntlet ability.

## What it does

Mandalorian-specific attribute. Launches a swarm of small heat-seeking missiles from the gauntlet. Each "bird" deals modest damage but the swarm seeks targets, making this excellent for chasing down elusive enemies (Jedi mid-roll, jetpack runners) or applying pressure across a chokepoint.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Whistling birds unlocked; standard tracking and bird count.
- **Level 2** — Improved tracking, higher per-bird damage.
- **Level 3** — Maximum bird count and damage; tightest tracking.

## Stats

- **Per-bird damage** — ~33
- **Velocity** — 600 (slow tracking)
- **Force drain (Close/Far)** — 16/14; blocking 12/10

## Notes

- Class-specific: Mandalorian (Beskar/Mandalorian Bounty Hunter kits).
- Ideal for crowd control and hitting elusive targets like Jedi.
- Pairs with `MB_ATT_BESKAR` on the full Mandalorian loadout.
