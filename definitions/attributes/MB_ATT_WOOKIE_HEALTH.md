# ❤️ Wookiee Health

`MB_ATT_WOOKIE_HEALTH`

Increases base HP for Wookiee classes to simulate their natural hardiness.

> **Default:** ~100-150 depending on class config.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**: **+100 HP** (Total ~200-250).
*   **Level 2**: **+250 HP** (Total ~350-400).
*   **Level 3**: **+400 HP** (Total ~500+).

### 💡 Tips
*   Wookiees cannot wear armor (`MB_ATT_ARMOUR` does nothing for them), so **Health is their only defense**.
*   A Level 3 Wookiee has more HP than a Droideka has Shields+Hull combined.
