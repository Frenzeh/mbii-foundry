# Dodge (Manual)

`EAS_HI_DODGE`

> Forces the Dodge state on demand.

## What it does

Manually triggers the Dodge ability rather than relying on the passive proc. Used for scripted evasions or for Dodge-builds that want a guaranteed activation, typically at higher Force/Stamina cost than the passive roll.

## Notes

- The passive chance is governed by `MB_ATT_DODGE`. This binding only fires the active variant.
- Stamina/Energy is consumed even if no projectile is incoming — there is no "free" miss.
- Bounty Hunter binds this to special1 in the stock kit.
