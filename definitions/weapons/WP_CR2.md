# CR-2 Repeating Pistol

`WP_CR2`

> Repeating blaster pistol. Extremely high rate of fire; ADS secondary.

## What it does

The CR-2 sacrifices accuracy and per-shot damage for raw cadence — both primary and secondary cycle at 175ms (150ms at level 4), making it a close-range shredder. Aim-down-sights secondary tightens the spread for medium-range engagements. Generous clip size (28-44) keeps you firing.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 25
- **Velocity** — 4000 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 175ms (150ms at level 4)

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Aim-down-sights (ADS) — same cadence, tighter spread
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 175ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_CR2`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 8/6 primary; blocking 7/5 — relatively low FP impact for the volume.
- Pairs well with light scout/recon loadouts.
