# ✋ Extended Interaction

`MB_ATT_USE_DISTANCE`

Increases the range at which the character can interact with objects.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Base Range:** 64 Units.
*   **Effect:** Adds **+32 Units** per Rank.
*   **Rank 1:** 96 Units.
*   **Rank 2:** 128 Units.

### 💡 Tips
*   Also increases the range of **Dispensing** abilities (giving ammo/health to allies).
