# Stasis (Special)

`EAS_FP_STASIS`

> Freezes a target in place using the Force.

## What it does

Locks the targeted player into a brief stasis — they cannot move, attack, or use abilities for the duration. Distinct from Grip (which lifts and damages) and from Push/Pull (which displaces). Stasis is pure crowd-control with no built-in damage.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Requires the corresponding stasis attribute on the class for FP cost, duration, and break-conditions.
- Often configured to break early on damage taken; check the attribute's level table.
