# Force Blind

`MB_ATT_FP_BLIND`

> Attribute for aimed blinding flash via the Force.

## What it does

Grants access to `FP_BLIND`. A direct-aim power that whites out the target's vision for a few seconds. Respects a cooldown and costs a flat FP amount per cast.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — ~2 second blind · *cost 20*
- **Level 2** — ~3 second blind · *cost 20*
- **Level 3** — ~4 second blind · *cost 20*

## Notes

- Countered by Force Block/Absorb — if they have it up, the cast is wasted.
- Excellent setup for Destruction, a saber engage, or a teammate's shot.
- Traces up to 2048u forward; must have line-of-sight at cast time.
