# Shock Field

`MB_ATT_SHOCKFIELD`

> Charge count for the `HI_SHOCKFIELD` deployable.

## What it does

Sets how many Shock Field deployables the user has. The field is a stationary AoE that periodically shocks anything in range — used for area-denial play.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — 1 charge.
- **Level 2** — 2 charges.
- **Level 3** — 3+ charges with longer dwell.

## Notes

- Sibling of `EAS_HI_SHOCKFIELD` / `HI_SHOCKFIELD`.
- Friendly-fire safe.
- Effective against droids — DEMP-flavor disruption.
