# Frag Grenade

`WP_FRAG_NADE`

> Standard fragmentation grenade. Timed cook with a heavy splash blast.

## What it does

The frag is the basic timed grenade in MBII: 3-second fuse, hard splash damage, predictable arc. Primary is a standard throw; secondary is a heavier lob. The throw can cook (hold to shorten the fuse) using the `CHGE_TIMED` mechanic.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Throw (`CHGE_GRENADE`)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Lobbed throw (`CHGE_TIMED`)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_FRAGS`.
- Heavier blast than `WP_THERMAL` and `WP_CONC_NADE`.
- Common on Soldier, Hero, and Trooper kits.
