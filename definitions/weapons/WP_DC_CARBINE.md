# DC-15S Side-arm Carbine

`WP_DC_CARBINE`

> Compact Clone carbine. Fast primary, slow heavy secondary.

## What it does

The DC-15S (DC Carbine) is the shorter, lighter counterpart to the DC-15A rifle. Fast 200ms primary with the same blaster ammo pool, and a slow but high-energy secondary that drops a heavier shot. Reload anim shared with the repeater family.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — Standard rifle tier
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 200ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Heavy shot
- **Ammo cost** — 20
- **Fire rate** — 400ms (level 2+ unlock)

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_DC_CARBINE`.
- Uses `AMMO_BLASTER` shared with the E-11 — generous reserves.
- Common on Clone Trooper / Sergeant kits.
