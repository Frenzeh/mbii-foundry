# 💥 Explosive Rounds

`HELD_EXPLOSIVE`

Projectiles carry a payload that detonates on impact.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **AoE:** Deals damage in a radius around the impact point.
*   **Damage:** Fixed explosive damage (configurable?).
*   **Bouncing:** If used on bouncing projectiles (e.g. Flechette Alt), they deal damage *continuously* on contact with surfaces/players.

### 📝 Note
*   **Visuals:** You *must* assign an explosion effect (e.g. `missileEffect`) or players won't know why they are taking damage near walls.

### 💡 Tips
*   Turns a standard blaster into a "Bolter" or "Grenade Launcher".
