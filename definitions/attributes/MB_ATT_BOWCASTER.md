# Bowcaster

`MB_ATT_BOWCASTER`

Wookiee traditional weapon. Fires explosive quarrels.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Damage:** **39** (Uncharged)
* **Max Charge:** **100+**
* **Velocity:** 7625
* **Ammo Cost:** 2

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Standard**. Can charge shots for higher damage and spread (3 bolts).
 * **Alt-Fire**: Bouncing bolt.

* **Level 2**:
 * **Improved**. Faster charge time.
 * **Spread**: 5 bolts when fully charged.

* **Level 3**:
 * **Master**.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Scope? Or just tighter spread.

### Tips
* Fully charged shots can one-hit kill if all bolts connect.
* Bolts bounce off walls, allowing for trick shots.
