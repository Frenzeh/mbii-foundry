# Force Grip

`FP_GRIP`

> Choke a target in the air — drain their FP, damage them, and pull them closer.

## What it does

Locks onto a single target after a brief acquisition window, then drains HP and FP per tick while the grip holds. Higher ranks let the user walk while gripping and apply stronger damage and movement effects. Breaks on line-of-sight loss, target knockback, or the grip owner being interrupted.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — stationary grip, slow damage, short lock · *cost 50*
- **Level 2** — slow-walk grip, medium damage, harder to escape · *cost 65*
- **Level 3** — full-walk grip, heavy damage, crush effect · *cost 70*

## Notes

- Costs scale UP with rank (unusual — most powers go down).
- Counters: Push/Pull can break grip; Absorb converts the drain into FP.
- Target can still shoot while gripped at lower ranks — not free damage.
