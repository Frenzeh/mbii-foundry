# Sonic Detonator

`MB_ATT_SONIC_DETONATOR`

> Sonic stun grenade attribute. Disorients targets in the blast zone.

## What it does

Grants `WP_SONIC_NADE`. A timed-cook grenade that detonates with a sonic shockwave — disorients hit targets (screen blur, control disruption) rather than killing them. Specialized control tool — pair with frag/thermal follow-ups for the actual kill.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — One sonic detonator.
- **Level 2** — Two sonic detonators.
- **Level 3** — Three sonic detonators; longer disorient duration.

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_SONIC_NADE`.
- Stun/disorient instead of kill — best as a setup tool.
- Useful versus Force users to break their concentration.
