# Melee Rate of Fire Multiplier

`MB_ATT_ROF_MELEE_MULTIPLIER`

> Multiplier for melee weapon swing speed.

## What it does

Like `MB_ATT_ROF_MULTIPLIER` but isolated to melee weapons (Stun Baton, Knife, Sword). Lets you tune a fast knife class without affecting blasters and vice versa.

## Notes

- Lower = faster swings.
- Sibling of `MB_ATT_ROF_MULTIPLIER` (ranged).
- Stacks with `HELD_SPEED` flag on the melee weapon for max mobility builds.
