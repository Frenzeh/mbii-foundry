# UGL Burst

`MB_ATT_UGL_BURST`

> Burst-fire variant of the UGL. Fires multiple grenades in rapid succession.

## What it does

Modifies `WP_UGL` to fire grenades in a burst pattern — one trigger pull launches a short volley of contact/timed grenades for area saturation rather than precise placement. Higher ammo cost per pull but devastating against grouped targets.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — 2-grenade burst.
- **Level 2** — 3-grenade burst, tighter spread.
- **Level 3** — Maximum burst, fastest follow-on cadence.

## Notes

- Requires `MB_ATT_UGL` for the base launcher.
- Higher ammo cost per trigger — manage your supply.
- Variants: `MB_ATT_UGL_IMPACT`, `MB_ATT_UGL_BURST_MIXED`.
