# Lifesteal

`HELD_LIFESTEAL`

> Portion of damage dealt heals the firer.

## What it does

A fraction of damage inflicted is converted into HP for the user — vampiric weapon behavior.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Strong on sustained-damage / high-DPS weapons; weaker on burst.
- Prefer pairing with utility flags (slow / stagger) so the user can stay in range to actually leech.
