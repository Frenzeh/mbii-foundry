# Battery Recharge

`MB_ATT_RECHARGE`

Determines how fast energy-based weapons or droid batteries regenerate.

> **Default:** Normal (Slow/Steady).

### Levels

* **Level 1**: Faster recharge delay (starts recharging sooner).
* **Level 2**: Increased recharge rate.
* **Level 3**: **Rapid Recharge**. Essential for SBDs to sustain fire.

### Tips
* Vital for **Super Battle Droids** (`MB_CLASS_SBD`) who rely on battery for both weapons and ammo.
* Also affects **Droideka** shield regeneration if `MB_ATT_DEKA_POWER` is not sufficient.
