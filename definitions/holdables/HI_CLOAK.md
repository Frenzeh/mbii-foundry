# 👻 Cloaking Device

`HI_CLOAK`

Personal cloaking generator.

> **Uses:** Unlimited (Requires Resource).

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Activation:** Turns user semi-invisible.
*   **Combat:** Can use all weapons and abilities while cloaked.
*   **Drain:** Drains resource faster while moving.
*   **Counter:** Smoke Grenades and Force Sense can help reveal cloaked players.

### 💡 Tips
*   Effective for ambushes and escaping.
*   Not true invisibility; shimmering is visible.
