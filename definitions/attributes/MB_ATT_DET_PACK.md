# Detonation Pack

`MB_ATT_DET_PACK`

> Remote-detonated explosive charges. Place-then-trigger area denial.

## What it does

Grants `WP_DET_PACK`. Primary places the charge on a surface; secondary remote-detonates all placed charges. Massive damage in the blast radius. Excellent for breaching, area denial, and ambushing chokepoints. You can place multiple charges before triggering.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — One pack at a time.
- **Level 2** — Two packs simultaneously.
- **Level 3** — Three packs; faster placement / detonation.

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_DET_PACK`.
- Place several at a chokepoint, fall back, detonate when enemies enter.
- Tricky vs moving targets — better as area denial than direct kill tool.
- Engineer, Demolition, and Saboteur loadouts.
