# DC-17 Hand Blaster

`WP_CLONE_PISTOL`

> Clone Trooper sidearm. Standard pistol shot primary; rapid-fire secondary.

## What it does

The DC-17 is the Clone-era counterpart to the DL-44. Primary is a familiar single-shot, but the secondary fires a tighter, faster cadence than the pistol's normal charged-shot — making it surprisingly potent at close range when dual-wielded.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 36
- **Velocity** — 6325 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 500ms (250ms at level 4)

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Rapid-fire / charge (`CHGE_CLONE`) at level 2+
- **Ammo cost** — 3
- **Fire rate** — 900ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_CLONE_PISTOL`.
- Dual-wield animation set (`CDUAL_ANIM_SET`) for ARC and Clone Commander.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 13/9 primary; blocking 11/6.
- Reload speed is fast (20 base) compared to other pistols.
