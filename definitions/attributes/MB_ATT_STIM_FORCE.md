# Resource Stim

`MB_ATT_STIM_FORCE`

> Single-target instant resource restore — point and use.

## What it does

Press Use while aiming at an ally to instantly restore their class resource (Force, Energy, Stamina, Battery, Fuel).

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small resource per stim.
- **Level 2** — medium resource per stim.
- **Level 3** — large resource per stim.

## Notes

- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DISP_FORCE` and `MB_ATT_DROP_FORCE`.
- Pool restored depends on the recipient class.
