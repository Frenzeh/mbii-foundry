# Seeker Drone

`HI_SEEKER`

> Disposable orbital combat drone.

## What it does

Spawns a friendly Seeker drone that orbits the player and shoots at enemies in line of sight. Provides minor damage and useful distraction — enemies often shoot the drone instead of the player, especially Jedi who reflexively swat at projectiles.

## Notes

- Drone HP is fixed; one stray rocket destroys it.
- Charge count comes from `MB_ATT_BASESEEKER`.
- Good against Jedi (forces them to break combos) and gunner stack-ups.
