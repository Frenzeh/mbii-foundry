# Czerka CP-50 Shotgun

`WP_SHOTGUN`

> Close-quarters scattergun. 5 pellets per shot; massive close-range burst.

## What it does

The Shotgun is the dedicated CQB weapon — five pellets per shot at 23 damage each (115 total at point-blank). Slow refire (1000ms) but devastating in tight corridors. Heavy energy cost on both modes; secondary uses `1350ms` cycle for a heavier discharge.

## Primary fire

- **Pellet damage** — 23 × 5 (~115 max)
- **Velocity** — 6325 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 12
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Heavy single discharge
- **Ammo cost** — 22
- **Fire rate** — 1350ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_SHOTGUN`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 32/22; blocking 15/22.
- `isShotgun` flag changes the crosshair to the spread reticle.
- Mercenary, Stormtrooper, and Imperial Shock Trooper kits.
