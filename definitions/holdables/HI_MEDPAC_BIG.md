# Big Bacta

`HI_MEDPAC_BIG`

> Rapid-injection bacta — high-volume heal.

## What it does

Larger heal version of the standard medpac. Restores significantly more HP per use but consumes a heavier charge. Designed as a clutch-survive tool — the heal lands fast enough to outpace incoming damage in many fights.

## Notes

- Charge count and per-use heal scale with `MB_ATT_BACTA_BIG`.
- Same animation lockout as `HI_MEDPAC` — commit when you have a half-second of breathing room.
- Sith Drain ignores Big Bacta in flight — beware mid-heal interrupts.
