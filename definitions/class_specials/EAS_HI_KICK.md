# Kick

`EAS_HI_KICK`

> Performs a melee kick that can knock down unblocking enemies.

## What it does

Fires the kick action regardless of weapon — useful for gunners who would otherwise have to swap to melee. Can stagger or knock down humanoid targets and applies short stun on hit. SBDs and Wookiees have stronger variants tied to their melee mode (`MM_KICK`, `MM_PK_KATA`).

## Notes

- Knocks down enemies who are not actively blocking; blocked kicks just stagger.
- Cooldown shared with class kick mode — chain-kicking is not possible.
- Pairs with `MB_ATT_FLIPKICK` for the airborne variant on Jedi/Sith builds.
