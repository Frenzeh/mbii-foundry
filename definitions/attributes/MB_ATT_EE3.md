# EE-3 Carbine Rifle

`MB_ATT_EE3`

Boba Fett's iconic carbine. Fires in short, powerful bursts.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Damage:** **33** (Primary & Secondary)
* **Velocity:** 6750 (Primary) / 7000 (Secondary)
* **Primary Fire Rate:** 1100ms (Base)
* **Burst Count:** 3 shots.
* **Ammo Cost:** 8 (Primary) / 2 (Secondary)

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Standard**. 3-shot burst.
 * **Fire Rate**: Slow.
* **Level 2**:
 * **Fire Rate**: Improved.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Fire Rate**: Fastest.
 * **Spread**: Reduced spread while moving.

### Tips
* Known for its high burst damage.
* The secondary fire is a single shot, good for accuracy.
