# Projectile Rifle

`WP_PROJ`

> Slugthrower sniper rifle. Massive single-shot damage; bypasses Force shielding.

## What it does

The Projectile Rifle (sometimes called the Felucian Slugthrower) is a kinetic ballistic sniper — primary fires a relatively quick aimed slug, secondary uses `ZOOM_SNIPER` for a long-cycle precision shot. Distinct from energy-based rifles, kinetic rounds bypass parts of Force-related defense, making it a hard-counter pick versus Jedi/Sith and shielded opponents.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 160
- **Velocity** — 10500 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 1
- **Fire rate** — 500ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Sniper scope (`ZOOM_SNIPER`)
- **Ammo cost** — 1
- **Fire rate** — 2000ms
- **Zoom delay** — 600ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_PROJECTILE_RIFLE`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 50/40 primary; blocking 40/30 — extreme block penalty.
- Bypasses Force-shielding partially — anti-Jedi specialty.
- Reload 3000ms; tiny clip (1) — every shot is a commitment.
- Bounty Hunter, Mandalorian, Imperial Sniper.
