# Saber Defense

`FP_SABER_DEFENSE`

> How well you parry — governs BP regen and incoming-saber-damage scaling.

## What it does

Passive. Sets the user's parry quality: how much BP regenerates per tick, how much damage their saber blocks take, and the cost of special techniques like jumps while parrying.

## Per level

- **Rank 0** — 1.6x incoming BP damage, no passive regen · *cost N/A*
- **Level 1** — 1.4x incoming, low regen · *cost 2*
- **Level 2** — 1.0x incoming, normal regen · *cost 1*
- **Level 3** — 1.0x incoming, normal regen, halved special costs, reduced jump drain · *cost 0*

## Notes

- Passive — costs shown are the point-buy cost in the skill system, not FP drain.
- Rank 3 is mandatory for duelists; Rank 1 is "Padawan" and loses stamina wars fast.
- Does not help against gunner damage — this is saber-vs-saber only.
