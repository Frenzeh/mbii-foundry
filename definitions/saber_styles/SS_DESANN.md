# Desann

`SS_DESANN`

> Heavy brutish style — wide hitbox swings.

## What it does

Specialized strong-flavor stance modeled after the JK2 boss Desann. Moderate-slow swings with very wide hitboxes — designed for crushing larger characters and "tank" Sith builds. Often used as a custom-Sith style on heavy bosses.

## Notes

- Animation set has wider arcs than vanilla Strong; better for AoE pressure in scrums.
- Damage scaling tracks the Strong stance baseline, not Medium.
- Used on Desann-replica characters and custom heavy Sith roles in FA.
