# Beskar Armor

`MB_ATT_BESKAR`

Legendary Mandalorian iron plating.

**Class Specific:** Mandalorian.

### Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   Light Beskar plating. Slight resistance to lightsabers.

*   **Level 2**:
    *   Medium Beskar plating. Can withstand glancing saber hits.

*   **Level 3**:
    *   Heavy Beskar plating.
    *   High resistance to lightsabers (takes reduced damage).
    *   High resistance to blasters.

### Notes
*   Does not make you invincible, but significantly increases survival against lightsabers compared to normal armor.
