# 🛡️ Force Block

`MB_ATT_FORCEBLOCK`

Passive mental defense against hostile Force powers.

> **Default:** 0 (Vulnerable to everything).

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**: **Resistant**. Resists Level 1 powers (Push/Pull/Grip). Reduces duration of effects.
*   **Level 2**: **Strong**. Resists Level 2 powers. Immune to Level 1 Grip/Lightning stun.
*   **Level 3**: **Immune**. Resists almost all Force powers. Push/Pull only nudge you slightly. Immune to Grip lift (still take damage). Immune to Lightning stun.

### 💡 Tips
*   Does **not** block "Force Destruction" or "Deadly Sight" damage, only CC effects.
*   **Vital** for dueling other Force users. Without it, you will be ragdolled.
