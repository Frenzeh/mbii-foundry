# Radar

`MB_ATT_RADAR`

> Sensor array that detects nearby enemies through walls.

## What it does

Activates a minimap radar in the corner of the HUD showing enemy positions as red dots within a fixed radius. Sees through walls — invaluable for sentry/defensive play and SBD anti-flank coverage.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — radar enabled at fixed range.
- **Level 2** — extended detection range.
- **Level 3** — long range and faster ping rate.

## Notes

- Drains the user's class resource (Battery / Energy) while active.
- Sees through walls but does not break cloak — `MB_ATT_TRACKING_BEACON` is the cloak-breaker.
- Most valuable on classes that watch fixed angles (SBD, sentry-positions).
