# EE-4 Carbine

`WP_EE4`

> Short-range burst blaster with a sniper toggle at level 3.

## What it does

The EE-4 is a versatile carbine: primary spits at 375ms (250ms at level 3+) for steady close-to-mid pressure, and the secondary toggles into a `ZOOM_PROJ` sniper-style heavy shot at level 3, swinging the weapon between bullhorn pressure tool and precision rifle.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 36
- **Velocity** — 6325 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 375ms (250ms at level 3+)

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Sniper / scoped (`ZOOM_PROJ`) at level 3+
- **Damage** — 70
- **Velocity** — 9825 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 26
- **Fire rate** — 750ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_EE4`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 13/8 primary, 43/29 sniper; blocking 12/6 / 36/24.
- Mode-cycle weapon — sniper unlocks at level 3.
- Common on Imperial Officer and Mandalorian kits.
