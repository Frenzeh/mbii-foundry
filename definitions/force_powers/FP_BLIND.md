# Force Blind

`FP_BLIND`

> Flashbang the target's brain — aimed blind that disables vision.

## What it does

Requires direct aim on a target. Inflicts a temporary blinding effect, whiting out the victim's screen for several seconds. Duration scales with rank. Consumes FP on cast and respects a cooldown.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — ~2 second blind · *cost 20*
- **Level 2** — ~3 second blind · *cost 20*
- **Level 3** — ~4 second blind · *cost 20*

## Notes

- Countered by Force Block/Absorb — if they have it up, the blind is wasted.
- Great setup for Destruction or a saber engage.
- Requires line-of-sight at cast time; effect persists through walls.
