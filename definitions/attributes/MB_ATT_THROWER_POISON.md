# Poison Thrower

`MB_ATT_THROWER_POISON`

> Poison-gas element variant for the Thrower stream weapon.

## What it does

Configures the Thrower (`WP_THROWER`) to deal poison damage-over-time — initial hit is light, but the poison continues ticking after contact. Strong vs lightly-armored organics; less effective vs droids.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Brief poison DoT on contact.
- **Level 2** — Longer DoT duration; higher tick damage.
- **Level 3** — Max duration and damage; severe DoT.

## Notes

- Requires `MB_ATT_THROWER` for the base weapon.
- Element-swap variants: `_LIGHTNING`, `_ICE`, `_PLASMA`, `_FLAME`.
- No effect on droids — pair with another loadout for mixed teams.
