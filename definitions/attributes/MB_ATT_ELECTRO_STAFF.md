# Electro Staff

`MB_ATT_ELECTRO_STAFF`

> Magna Guard staff weapon. Long-reach electrified melee that can parry lightsabers.

## What it does

Grants the electrostaff — a two-handed melee polearm that delivers heavy strikes and can parry lightsaber attacks. Slower than a knife or sword but with significantly more reach and a brief stun on hit. The Magna Guard's signature weapon.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Basic staff; standard melee damage with reach.
- **Level 2** — Improved swing speed and parry window vs sabers.
- **Level 3** — Max damage and best parry window; brief stun-on-hit.

## Notes

- Long reach — outranges most other melee weapons.
- Can briefly parry / interrupt lightsaber strikes.
- Magna Guard signature; rarely available outside that class.
