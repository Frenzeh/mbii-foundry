# Armor Drop

`MB_ATT_DROP_ARMOR`

> Drops a one-shot armor pickup on the ground.

## What it does

Spawns an Armor pickup at the user's feet that any ally can grab.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small armor per pickup.
- **Level 2** — medium armor per pickup.
- **Level 3** — large armor per pickup.

## Notes

- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DISP_ARMOR` and `MB_ATT_STIM_ARMOR`.
- Charge count tied to `MB_ATT_SUPPLYDROP`.
