# 💨 Knockdown Roll

`MB_ATT_KNOCKDOWN_ROLL`

Converts a standard knockdown into a quick roll.

> **Default:** Disabled.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Effect:** Instead of being stunned and falling, the character performs a fast recovery roll.
*   **Resource:** Does not cost any resource.
*   **Benefit:** Reduces vulnerability to follow-up attacks.

### 💡 Tips
*   Excellent for highly mobile classes that need to recover quickly from crowd control effects.
