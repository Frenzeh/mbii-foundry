# Projectile Rifle

`MB_ATT_PROJECTILE_RIFLE`

> Slugthrower sniper rifle attribute. Massive damage; bypasses parts of Force shielding.

## What it does

Grants `WP_PROJ` — the Felucian-style ballistic sniper rifle. Kinetic rounds bypass parts of Force defense, making this a hard-counter pick versus Jedi/Sith. Each shot is a commitment: 500ms primary cycle, 2000ms scoped secondary, single-round clip with 3000ms reload.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Standard slug; high damage (160) at 10500 velocity.
- **Level 2** — Improved scope handling and reload speed.
- **Level 3** — Maximum scope speed; full anti-Jedi suite.

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_PROJ`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 50/40 primary; blocking 40/30.
- `ZOOM_SNIPER` scope; reload 3000ms — every shot must count.
- Bounty Hunter, Mandalorian, Imperial Sniper.
