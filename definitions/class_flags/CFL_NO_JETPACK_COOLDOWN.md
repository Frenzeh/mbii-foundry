# No Jetpack Cooldown

`CFL_NO_JETPACK_COOLDOWN`

Removes the delay between jetpack bursts.

### Effects
* **Mobility**: Allows for rapid tapping of the jetpack for "skating" or unpredictable movement.
* **Flight**: Easier to maintain altitude.

### Use Cases
* Elite Mandalorians (e.g. Jango/Boba Fett) for superior aerial agility.
