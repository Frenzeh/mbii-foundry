# Class-A Thermal Detonator

`WP_REAL_TD`

> Canon-accurate "real" thermal detonator. Massive blast radius, often instakill.

## What it does

The Real TD is the canon-accurate, massively-overpowered version of a thermal detonator — this is the Boushh-from-Jedi-style nuke. Detonation radius is enormous and damage is effectively instakill within the lethal zone. Used as a special class ability (often Bounty Hunter) rather than a standard grenade.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Throw (`CHGE_GRENADE`)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Lobbed throw
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_THERMALS`.
- Massive blast — lethal to the thrower if mishandled.
- Boushh-style canon weapon; used for area denial and last-resort wipes.
