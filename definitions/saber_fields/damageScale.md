# Damage Scale

`damageScale`

> Multiplier for saber damage.

## What it does

Multiplier for saber damage.

**Default:** 1.0

## Valid values

- **1.0:** Standard MBII saber damage.
- **0.1:** Training saber (negligible damage).
- **2.0:** One-hit kill on almost anything.

## Notes

- Use **0.0** or extremely low values for "Knockback Sticks" or utility weapons that shouldn't kill.
