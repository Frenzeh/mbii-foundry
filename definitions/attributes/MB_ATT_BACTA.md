# Bacta Charges

`MB_ATT_BACTA`

> Standard medpac inventory.

## What it does

Determines how many `HI_MEDPAC` charges the user starts with and the per-charge heal amount. Each rank either grows the pool, increases per-use heal, or both.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small starting charges, low heal-per-use.
- **Level 2** — medium pool / heal.
- **Level 3** — full pool, fastest animation.

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_BACTA_BIG` for the larger-injection variant.
- Heal animation locks the user out of weapons — commit only with cover.
- Sibling of the holdable `HI_MEDPAC`.
