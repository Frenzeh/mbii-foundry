# Team Heal

`MB_ATT_FP_TEAM_HEAL`

Channels Force energy to heal nearby allies.

> **Class Specific:** Jedi (Light Side).

### Levels

* **Rank 1:**
 * **Target:** Single ally (aimed).
 * **Heal:** ~30 HP.
* **Rank 2:**
 * **Target:** Radius (small).
 * **Heal:** ~50 HP.
* **Rank 3:**
 * **Target:** Radius (large).
 * **Heal:** ~50-75 HP to everyone nearby.

### Tips
* **Medic Jedi:** Keeps the team alive during a push.
* **Self:** Does *not* heal the user (use `FP_HEAL` for that).
