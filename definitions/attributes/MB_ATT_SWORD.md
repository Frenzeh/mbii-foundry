# Sword (Vibroblade)

`MB_ATT_SWORD`

> Melee bladed weapon. Vibroblade / vibrosword close-quarters slot.

## What it does

Grants a melee blade weapon — a vibroblade or sword used for close-quarters fighting. Faster than a stun baton and capable of parrying lightsaber strikes briefly. Used by select Mandalorian and Imperial Officer kits as a sidearm-tier melee option.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Vibroblade equipped. Standard slash damage.
- **Level 2** — Improved swing speed and parry window.
- **Level 3** — Best damage; reliable saber-parry on timing.

## Notes

- Maps to a vibroblade item rather than a `WP_*` enum directly.
- Pairs conceptually with `MB_ATT_KNIFE` for the broader bladed-melee family.
- Mandalorian / Bounty Hunter / Imperial Officer specialty.
