# Sentry Gun Charges

`MB_ATT_SENTRY`

> Charge count and tier of the deployable Assault Sentry.

## What it does

Determines how many `HI_SENTRY_GUN` charges the user starts with, plus per-Sentry HP/damage at higher ranks.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — 1 charge, baseline turret.
- **Level 2** — 2 charges, tougher turret.
- **Level 3** — 3 charges, full HP and damage.

## Notes

- Sibling of the holdable `HI_SENTRY_GUN`.
- Sentries auto-target enemies in LoS; friendly-fire safe.
- Destructible — place around corners or near cover.
