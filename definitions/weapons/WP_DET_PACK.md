# Detonation Pack

`WP_DET_PACK`

> Remote-detonated explosive charge. Stick, run, then trigger.

## What it does

Det packs are placed by primary fire — the charge sticks to the surface where it's thrown — and detonated remotely with secondary fire. Massive damage in the blast radius; classic for breaching, area denial, and ambushing chokepoints. You can place multiple charges before triggering.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Throw / place charge
- **Fire rate** — 800ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Remote detonate (all placed packs)
- **Fire rate** — 400ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_DET_PACK`.
- Place several at a chokepoint, fall back, detonate when enemies enter.
- Common on Engineer and Demolition loadouts.
