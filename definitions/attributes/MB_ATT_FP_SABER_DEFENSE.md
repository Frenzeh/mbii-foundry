# Saber Defense (Parry)

`MB_ATT_FP_SABER_DEFENSE`

The ability to block incoming attacks with a lightsaber.

### Stats
* **BP Regen Rate:** 400ms (0.4s).
* **BP Regen Amount:**
 * **Rank 1:** **3 BP** per tick.
 * **Rank 2/3:** **4 BP** per tick.

### Levels
* **Rank 0:**
 * **Damage Taken:** **1.6x** (60% more damage from sabers).
 * **Regen:** **0** (No passive regen).
* **Rank 1:**
 * **Damage Taken:** **1.4x** (40% more damage).
 * **Regen:** Low.
* **Rank 2:**
 * **Damage Taken:** **1.0x** (Normal).
 * **Regen:** Normal.
* **Rank 3:**
 * **Damage Taken:** **1.0x**.
 * **Perks:** BP cost halved for specials. Jumping drain reduced.

### Tips
* **Rank 3** is standard for any duelist.
* **Rank 1** is "Padawan" level—you will lose a stamina battle quickly.
