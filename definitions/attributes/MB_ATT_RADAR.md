# Radar

`MB_ATT_RADAR`

> Advanced sensor array that detects nearby enemies.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — **Active**. Enables a minimap radar in the top right. Shows enemy positions (red dots) within a certain radius, even through walls.

## Tips

- **Consumes Battery/Energy** while active.
- Invaluable for SBDs to prevent flanking.
