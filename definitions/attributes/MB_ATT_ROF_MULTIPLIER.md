# 🔫 Rate of Fire Multiplier

`MB_ATT_ROF_MULTIPLIER`

Multiplies the weapon's base rate of fire.

> **Default:** 1.0

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **1.0:** Normal ROF.
*   **1.1:** +10% Faster ROF.
*   **0.9:** -10% Slower ROF.

### 💡 Tips
*   Often used to balance a weapon with very high damage or custom ammo.
*   Affects all weapons.
