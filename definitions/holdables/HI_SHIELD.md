# 🛡️ Forcefield

`HI_SHIELD`

Deployable energy barrier.

> **Uses:** 1-time deployment.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Effect:** Creates a large shield wall.
*   **Blocking:** Prevents enemies and weapon fire from passing.
*   **Permeable:** Owner and allies can walk/shoot through it.
*   **Duration:** Deactivates permanently after X seconds or X damage.

### 💡 Tips
*   Critical for creating cover in open areas or blocking choke points.
