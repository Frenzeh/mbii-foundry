# Cloaking Device

`HI_CLOAK`

> Personal cloaking generator — partial invisibility on demand.

## What it does

Engages a stealth-field that makes the player visually translucent. Not full invisibility — heavy movement or firing reveals shimmer. Designed for ambushes and disengages, not extended stealth play.

## Notes

- Drains the cloak resource (`MB_ATT_CLOAK` rank determines pool/regen).
- Firing or taking damage breaks cloak immediately.
- Tracking Darts (`MB_ATT_TRACKING_DART`) reveal cloaked players to the entire team — Bounty Hunter counterplay.
