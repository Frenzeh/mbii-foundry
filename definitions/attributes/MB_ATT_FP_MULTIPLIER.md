# 馃攱 Force Pool Multiplier

`MB_ATT_FP_MULTIPLIER`

Multiplies the total Force Point (FP) pool.

> **Default:** 1.0

### 鈿欙笍 Mechanics
*   **1.0:** Normal FP.
*   **1.1:** +10% FP.
*   **0.9:** -10% FP.

### 馃挕 Tips
*   Use to create Force-heavy or Force-light characters.
*   Combines with the `forcepool` field to determine total FP.
