# Force Lightning

`FP_LIGHTNING`

> Stream electric death from your fingertips — suppress gunners, melt Jedi.

## What it does

Channels a continuous lightning stream from the user's hand. Damages the target over time and at Rank 3 suppresses gunner fire by stunning them. Drains FP rapidly while active. Rank 3 can chain between targets.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — single-target sparks, low damage · *cost 2*
- **Level 2** — sustained bolt, medium damage · *cost 2*
- **Level 3** — chain lightning, strong damage, stuns gunners · *cost 2*

## Notes

- Listed cost is per-tick; real drain is high (Rank 1/2 ~60 FP/sec, Rank 3 ~50 FP/sec).
- Countered by Absorb (light) — Jedi absorbs it outright.
- Sabers can block lightning partially at the cost of BP.
