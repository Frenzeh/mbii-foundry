# UGL Impact

`MB_ATT_UGL_IMPACT`

> Impact-fuse variant of the UGL. Grenades detonate on contact instead of after a timer.

## What it does

Modifies `WP_UGL` so grenades use an impact (contact) fuse — they detonate immediately on hitting a surface or target rather than rolling and waiting on a timer. Trades the ability to bounce shots around corners for guaranteed-on-target detonations.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Impact fuse on standard rounds.
- **Level 2** — Improved blast radius on impact detonation.
- **Level 3** — Maximum damage; tightest contact-detonation profile.

## Notes

- Requires `MB_ATT_UGL` for the base launcher.
- Variants: `MB_ATT_UGL_BURST`, `MB_ATT_UGL_BURST_MIXED`.
- Best vs vehicles and SBDs where you want guaranteed detonation.
