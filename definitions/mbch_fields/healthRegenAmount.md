# ❤️ Health Regen

`healthRegenAmount` / `healthRegenRate`

Passive HP regeneration.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Amount:** How much HP to restore per tick (e.g., `1`, `5`).
*   **Rate:** How often to tick in milliseconds (e.g., `1000` = 1 second).

### 💡 Tips
*   **Bosses:** Give them `1` HP every `1000` ms for slow, steady recovery.
*   **Trolls:** Give them `100` HP every `500` ms for near-invincibility (not recommended).
