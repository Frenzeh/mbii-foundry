# Disintegrate

`HELD_DISRUPTIFY`

Weapon output is tuned to destabilize molecular bonds.

### Mechanics
* **Visual:** Enemies killed by this weapon vanish in an orange/red disintegration effect (Disruptor style).
* **Gameplay:**
 * **No Revive:** Bodies cannot be revived by medics.
 * **No Assemble:** Enemies cannot spawn on the disintegrated ally.
 * **No Corpse:** Clears visual clutter.

### Tips
* Great for "High Tech" or "Illegal" weaponry.
* Strategic counter to `MB_ATT_ASSEMBLE`.
