# Gun Bash

`MB_ATT_GUNBASH`

> Melee strike with a held firearm — interrupt and stagger.

## What it does

Lets gunner classes deliver a melee bash with their primary weapon. Damage is light, but the hit applies a brief stagger (`HELD_STUN` flavor) and can interrupt animations like reloads, hacks, or charged shots.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — slow bash, minor damage.
- **Level 2** — faster animation, more reliable stagger.
- **Level 3** — full speed, can knock unblocking targets off-balance.

## Notes

- Bound to `EAS_MELEE` for the universal slap action.
- Strength 3 characters (Wookiees) resist the stagger effect.
- Useful counter to a sabe-rusher closing into your face.
