# View-Based Drain

`MB_ATT_VIEWBASEDDRAIN`

> Force Drain hits whoever you are looking at.

## What it does

Modifies the targeting mode of Force Drain so the cone is replaced with a strict crosshair-target check — drain only ticks on the entity directly under the user's view. Higher-precision drain at the cost of multi-target capability.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — narrow drain cone.
- **Level 2** — strict crosshair-only drain.
- **Level 3** — drain ticks faster on the focused target.

## Notes

- Categorized as Advanced — Sith-tuning attribute, not a generic pick.
- Only affects Drain; other Force powers retain their normal targeting.
- Useful for "executioner" Sith builds that want focused drain over AoE.
