# Armor Stim

`MB_ATT_STIM_ARMOR`

> Single-target instant armor restore — point and use.

## What it does

Press Use while aiming at an ally to instantly restore armor in one transfer.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small armor per stim.
- **Level 2** — medium armor per stim.
- **Level 3** — large armor per stim.

## Notes

- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DISP_ARMOR` and `MB_ATT_DROP_ARMOR`.
