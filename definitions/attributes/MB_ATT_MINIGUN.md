# 🔫 Minigun

`MB_ATT_MINIGUN`

Portable rotary blaster cannon with an extremely high rate of fire.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Damage:** **20** (per shot)
*   **Fire Rate:** 120ms (Spin-up delay) -> 80ms (Sustained fire)
*   **Velocity:** 6325
*   **Ammo Cost:** 1
*   **Clip Size:** 150

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Standard**. Requires spin-up.
*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Spin-up**: Faster.
*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Accuracy**: Improved.
    *   **Sustain**: Longer sustained fire without overheating.

### 💡 Tips
*   Excellent for suppressing large groups and area denial.
*   Highly effective against vehicles and droids.
*   User is slowed (`HELD_SLOW`) while firing.
