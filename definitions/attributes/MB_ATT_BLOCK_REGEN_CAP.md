# Block Regen Cap

`MB_ATT_BLOCK_REGEN_CAP`

> Ceiling above which BP regen stops.

## What it does

Caps the regen at a configured BP value. Useful for "fragile defender" builds — duelist can recover to a stable mid-pool but never to full BP without combat lulls.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — low cap.
- **Level 2** — medium cap.
- **Level 3** — full BP cap.

## Notes

- 0 means cap to current max BP.
- Sibling of `MB_ATT_BLOCK_REGEN_AMOUNT` and `MB_ATT_BLOCK_REGEN_RATE`.
