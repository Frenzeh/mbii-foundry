# Saber Deflect

`MB_ATT_DEFLECT`

Chance to passively deflect blaster bolts while holding a lightsaber and blocking.

**Class Specific:** Jedi, Sith.

### Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   Low chance to deflect bolts back at shooter.
    *   Most bolts are just blocked (negligible FP drain).

*   **Level 2**:
    *   Medium chance to deflect.
    *   Improved accuracy of deflected bolts.

*   **Level 3**:
    *   High chance to deflect.
    *   Deflected bolts are very accurate (auto-aim at shooter).
    *   Essential for fighting gunners at range.

### Notes
*   Requires `MB_ATT_FP_SABER_DEFENSE` to be effective (you must be blocking).
*   Does not work if Force Pool is depleted.
