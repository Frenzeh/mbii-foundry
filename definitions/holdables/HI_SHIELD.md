# Forcefield

`HI_SHIELD`

> Deployable energy barrier.

## What it does

Drops a wall-shaped energy barrier that blocks projectiles in both directions. Allies can pass through it — enemies cannot, and their shots are absorbed. Crucial for creating cover on open ground or sealing chokes.

## Notes

- HP and dwell time scale with `MB_ATT_FORCEFIELD`.
- Sabers can pass through unimpeded — the field stops projectile fire only.
- Multiple shields can stack to reinforce a point.
