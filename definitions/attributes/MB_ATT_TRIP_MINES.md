# 🪚 Trip Mine

`MB_ATT_TRIP_MINES`

Deployable laser trip mines.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Damage:** 100+ (Lethal)
*   **Trigger Radius:** Small.
*   **Deployment:** Sticks to surfaces.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Single Mine**.
*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Two Mines**.

### 💡 Tips
*   Excellent for securing flanks or objective points.
*   Can be shot by enemies to clear them safely.
