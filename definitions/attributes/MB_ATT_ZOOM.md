# Zoom

`MB_ATT_ZOOM`

> Generic zoom optic — works without a scoped weapon.

## What it does

Grants the user the ability to zoom in via the secondary-fire / zoom key without needing a scoped weapon equipped. Equivalent functionality to `HI_BINOCULARS` but as a passive trait.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — basic 2x zoom.
- **Level 2** — variable zoom (multiple steps).
- **Level 3** — full sniper-class zoom range.

## Notes

- Cannot fire while zoomed unless the equipped weapon supports scoped fire.
- Sibling of `MB_ATT_BINOCULARS` (item-based zoom).
