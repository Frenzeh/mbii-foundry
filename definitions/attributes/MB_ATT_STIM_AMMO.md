# Ammo Stim

`MB_ATT_STIM_AMMO`

> Single-target instant ammo refill — point and use.

## What it does

Press Use while aiming at an ally to instantly refill ammo in one transfer.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small ammo per stim.
- **Level 2** — medium ammo per stim.
- **Level 3** — large ammo per stim.

## Notes

- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DISP_AMMO` and `MB_ATT_DROP_AMMO`.
