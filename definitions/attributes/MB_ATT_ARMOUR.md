# 🛡️ Armor

`MB_ATT_ARMOUR`

Provides damage reduction. Armor takes damage before Health in most cases.

> **Default:** 0 (Unarmored)

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**: **Light Armor**. Good for mobility classes.
*   **Level 2**: **Medium Armor**. Standard trooper protection.
*   **Level 3**: **Heavy Armor**. High protection but may incur stamina penalties on some classes.

### 💡 Tips
*   **Cortosis** (`MB_ATT_CORTOSIS`) is required to make Armor effective against Lightsabers. Without it, sabers cut right through.
*   **Blast Armor** (`MB_ATT_BLAST_ARMOUR`) increases effectiveness against explosions.
*   **Magnetic Plating** (`MB_ATT_MAGNETIC_PLATING`) increases effectiveness against blasters.
