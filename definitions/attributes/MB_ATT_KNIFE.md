# Knife (Combat Knife)

`MB_ATT_KNIFE`

> Combat knife / vibroknife. Compact bladed melee with backstab potential.

## What it does

Grants a small melee blade — a combat knife or vibroknife. Lower damage than a sword but faster swing and a backstab bonus when hitting targets from behind. Ideal for stealthy or assassin-style Bounty Hunter and Mandalorian kits.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Basic knife; minor backstab multiplier.
- **Level 2** — Improved swing speed; better backstab multiplier.
- **Level 3** — Maximum backstab damage; deflect window.

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_BACKSTAB` for the multiplier mechanic.
- Faster than a stun baton, less reach than a sword.
- Bounty Hunter / Mandalorian assassin kits.
