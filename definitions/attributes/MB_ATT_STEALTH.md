# Stealth

`MB_ATT_STEALTH`

> Reduces the user's footstep and breathing audio profile.

## What it does

Quiets the player's footsteps, weapon-handling sounds, and breathing — making them harder to hear at close range. Doesn't affect vision; this is purely an audio mask.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — softer footsteps.
- **Level 2** — near-silent walking.
- **Level 3** — silent on all surfaces, no breath audio.

## Notes

- Stacks with `MB_ATT_CLOAK` for full sneak builds.
- Not visual — does not affect what enemies see, only what they hear.
- Sprinting still produces audio; walk/crouch to fully exploit Stealth.
