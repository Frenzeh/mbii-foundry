# MGL Burst

`MB_ATT_MGL_BURST`

> Burst-fire variant of the MGL. Fires multiple sticky bombs in a short volley.

## What it does

Modifies `WP_MGL` to fire sticky bombs in a burst pattern — one trigger pull launches a short volley of stickies for fast area-cover. Higher ammo cost but devastating for mining a chokepoint quickly.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — 2-sticky burst.
- **Level 2** — 3-sticky burst, tighter pattern.
- **Level 3** — Maximum burst; fastest follow-on cycle.

## Notes

- Requires `MB_ATT_MGL` for the base launcher.
- Variants: `MB_ATT_MGL_IMPACT`.
- Best for rapid mining of chokepoints.
