# Aerial Force Regen

`MB_ATT_INAIR_FORCE_REGEN`

> Force Points regenerate while airborne.

## What it does

Removes the standard "no regen while airborne" rule for `RESOURCE_FORCE`. Lets Force users sustain mid-air casts — Push, Pull, Lightning at altitude — without bottoming out.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — partial in-air regen.
- **Level 2** — full ground-rate regen.
- **Level 3** — bonus regen rate.

## Notes

- Categorized as Advanced.
- Doesn't affect Stamina, Energy, Battery, Fuel, or Rage — only Force.
- Critical for aerial-jedi builds and pure caster Sith.
