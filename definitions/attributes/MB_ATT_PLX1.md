# 🚀 PLX-1 Portable Missile Launcher

`MB_ATT_PLX1`

A compact rocket launcher, often equipped with guided missiles.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Direct Hit Damage:** **250**
*   **Splash Damage:** **200**
*   **Splash Radius:** **200** units
*   **Velocity:** 1610 (Slow)
*   **Ammo Cost:** 1
*   **Clip Size:** 1 (Single Shot)
*   **Reload Time:** ~2.5 seconds.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Standard**. Unguided rocket.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: Guided mode (requires manual steering).
*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Guided**: Improved guidance system.
*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Fast Reload**: Significantly reduced reload time.

### 💡 Tips
*   Functionally very similar to `MB_ATT_ROCKET_LAUNCHER`, but potentially has different cosmetic overrides or specific class access.
*   Often seen with ARC Troopers.
