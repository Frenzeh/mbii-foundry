# WESTAR-34 Pistol

`MB_ATT_MANDO_PISTOL`

Mandalorian sidearm. Often dual-wielded.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Damage:** **36** (Base) -> **62** (Charged)
* **Velocity:** 6325
* **Primary Fire Rate:** 500ms
* **Primary Ammo Cost:** 2
* **Secondary Fire Rate:** 800ms (Charged shot)
* **Secondary Ammo Cost:** 4
* **Clip Size:** 20.

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Standard**. Single-shot primary.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Charged shot.
* **Level 2**:
 * **Accuracy**: Improved.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Damage**: Charged shot deals significantly more damage.

### Tips
* With `CFL_AKIMBOPISTOL3`, becomes very powerful.
* Excellent for bounty hunters and Mandalorians.
