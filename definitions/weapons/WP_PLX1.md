# PLX-1 Missile Launcher

`WP_PLX1`

> Heavy anti-vehicle launcher. Dumb-fire primary; mode-swap targeting alt.

## What it does

The PLX-1 is a single-shot rocket launcher — high-damage explosive primary at 900ms refire, with a long 2000ms reload after each shot. Designed for anti-vehicle and anti-tank work (SBDs, walkers). Mode-cycle weapon (`swapMode`, swap unlocked at level 0).

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Dumb-fire rocket
- **Ammo cost** — 1
- **Fire rate** — 900ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Alternate launch profile (mode-swap)
- **Ammo cost** — 2
- **Fire rate** — 1200ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_PLX1`.
- Reload speed 2000ms — long commitment per shot.
- Anti-SBD, anti-vehicle, anti-emplaced gun specialty.
