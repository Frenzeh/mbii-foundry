# Damage Taken Multiplier

`damageTaken` / `rankDmgTaken`

> Global damage resistance multiplier.

## What it does

Global damage resistance multiplier.

**Default:** 1.0 (100% / No resistance)

## Valid values

- **1.0:** Takes normal damage.
- **0.5:** Takes 50% damage (Half damage).
- **0.9:** Takes 90% damage (10% resistance - Standard Jedi trait).

## Notes

- **Jedi/Sith** usually have **0.9** (10% innate reduction).
- **Wookiees** often use `0.85` or `0.9` to simulate toughness.
