# Thermal Detonator

`MB_ATT_THERMALS`

A powerful explosive device.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Direct Hit Damage:** **100** (Estimate from `FRAG_GRENADE_DAMAGE`)
* **Splash Damage:** **100** (Estimate)
* **Splash Radius:** **256** units (Estimate)
* **Fuse Time:** ~3 seconds.

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Single Grenade**. Standard blast.
* **Level 2**:
 * **Two Grenades**.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Three Grenades**.

### Tips
* A fully charged thermal can clear a room or one-shot most unarmored targets.
* Use `CFL_REALTD` for an ultra-powerful, cinematic version.
