# Imperial Heavy Repeater

`WP_REPEATER`

> Sustained-fire heavy weapon. Very fast cadence; concussion-blob secondary.

## What it does

The Heavy Repeater is the workhorse Imperial sustained-fire weapon — primary fires at 100ms cadence (very high volume) for room-clearing, while the secondary launches a concussion grenade-style bolt for area damage. Primary alone burns through ammo fast; the alt is for picking off groups or breaking up choke pushes.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 26
- **Ammo cost** — 1
- **Fire rate** — 100ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Concussion grenade launcher
- **Fire rate** — 850ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_REPEATER`.
- Very fast — burns clip (150) quickly.
- Heavy Trooper, Galactic Marine, and Imperial Heavy Soldier kits.
