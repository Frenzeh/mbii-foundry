# No Dlight (Dynamic Light)

`noDlight`

> Prevents the saber from casting light on the environment.

## What it does

Prevents the saber from casting light on the environment.

## Valid values

- **1 (True):** Blade does not glow/illuminate surroundings.
- **0 (False):** Blade casts light matching its color.

## Notes

- Essential for metallic weapons (Swords, Axes, Clubs) so they don't look like glowsticks.
