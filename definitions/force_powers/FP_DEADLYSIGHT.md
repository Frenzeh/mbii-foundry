# Deadly Sight

`FP_DEADLYSIGHT`

> Kill with a glare — the target bleeds while you look at them.

## What it does

Applies a damage-over-time effect on the target while the user keeps their gaze on them. Rank 1 requires a direct line-of-sight trace; higher ranks soften that requirement. Consumes FP while active.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — requires direct aim, short DoT · *cost 20*
- **Level 2** — wider aim cone, stronger DoT · *cost 20*
- **Level 3** — widest cone, strongest DoT · *cost 20*

## Notes

- Sith power. Especially effective on clustered, low-HP infantry.
- Countered by Absorb and by breaking line-of-sight.
- Good opener if you have Rage or another damage amp queued.
