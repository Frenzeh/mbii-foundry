# Ice Thrower

`MB_ATT_THROWER_ICE`

> Cryo element variant for the Thrower stream weapon.

## What it does

Configures the Thrower (`WP_THROWER`) to deal cryo damage — slows targets caught in the stream and can briefly freeze them at higher rank. Setup tool more than damage tool.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Slow effect on stream contact.
- **Level 2** — Stronger slow; brief freeze chance.
- **Level 3** — Reliable freeze on sustained contact.

## Notes

- Requires `MB_ATT_THROWER` for the base weapon.
- Element-swap variants: `_LIGHTNING`, `_POISON`, `_PLASMA`, `_FLAME`.
- Excellent for setting up teammate follow-ups.
