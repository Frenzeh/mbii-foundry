# Thermal Rockets

`CFL_THERMALROCKETS`

Upgrades the Rocket Launcher to fire Thermal Detonators.

### Mechanics
* **Weapon:** `WP_PLX1` / `MB_ATT_PLX1`.
* **Projectile:** Fires a Thermal Detonator instead of a standard rocket.
* **Damage:** **Massive** (Thermal Detonator damage scaling).
* **Arc:** Projectile may have a slight arc compared to straight rocket?

### Tips
* **Siege Breaker:** One of the most destructive weapons in the game.
