# 🪝 Grapple Hook

`MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOOK`

Equips the character with a grappling hook.

> **Default:** Disabled.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Rank 1:**
    *   **Basic Hook:** Short range.
*   **Rank 2:**
    *   **Improved Hook:** Medium range. Faster reel-in.
*   **Rank 3:**
    *   **Advanced Hook:** Long range. Can pull enemies closer.

### 💡 Tips
*   Use `EAS_HI_GRAPPLEHOOK` to bind to a special ability key.
*   Allows for unique movement and positioning.
*   Can pull enemies into melee range.
