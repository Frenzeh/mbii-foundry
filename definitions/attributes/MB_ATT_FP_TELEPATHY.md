# Mind Trick

`MB_ATT_FP_TELEPATHY`

> Attribute for Force-induced invisibility.

## What it does

Grants access to `FP_TELEPATHY`. Makes the user translucent or invisible and hides them from radar while the power is active. Breaks instantly on firing, saber ignition, or taking damage.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — translucent silhouette, off radar · *cost 50*
- **Level 2** — stronger concealment · *cost 30*
- **Level 3** — near-total invisibility · *cost 30*

## Notes

- Countered by `FP_SEE` at matching or higher rank.
- Ideal for flanking, objective grabs, and escaping bad fights.
- Sustain drain is ~20-40 FP/sec depending on rank.
