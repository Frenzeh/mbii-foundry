# Tavion

`SS_TAVION`

> Acrobatic light style — flips and backstabs.

## What it does

Specialized fast-flavor stance modeled after Tavion (JK2). Quick, mobile swings with unique flip-stab and backstab animations. Used on assassin-flavored Sith builds where the player wants Fast's speed plus a high-damage backstab option.

## Notes

- Animation set adds flip-stab specials not present in vanilla Fast.
- Damage tracks Fast baseline; the special moves are where the value lives.
- Used on Tavion-replica characters and stealth-Sith roles in FA.
