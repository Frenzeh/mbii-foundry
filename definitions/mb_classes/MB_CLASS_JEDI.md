# Jedi

`MB_CLASS_JEDI`

> Rebel Force user — saber + light-side powers. 10% universal damage reduction.

## Role

The anchor melee / anti-infantry class on the Rebel side. Jedi carry the universal Force-user kit — saber stance selection, block points, Force powers — with light-side leanings (Heal, Speed, Telepathy, Protect, Absorb). 100 base HP and the classwide 10% damage mitigation let them duel multiple blaster-infantry if played correctly.

## Default loadout

- **Base health** — 100
- **Base speed** — 225.0
- **Armor pool** — `NO_ARMOR_POOL` (force-users don't carry armor)
- **Melee moves** — All
- **Force-sensitive** — yes (`isFS = qtrue`)
- **Resource** — Force
- **Abilities (CS1-4)** — `EAS_FP_SABERTHROW`, `EAS_MELEE`, `EAS_FP_SPEED`, `EAS_FP_TELEPATHY`
- **Abilities (CS5-8)** — `EAS_FP_REPULSE`, none, none, none
- **Forcecfg** — `forcecfg/red/jedi`
- **Model** — `jedi`

## Signature mechanics

- 10% universal damage reduction baked into the class (applied in the damage pipeline).
- Block Points (BP) resource — drained by blocking blaster fire and deflecting sabers.
- Force pool is spent on both active powers and saber defense.

## Counters

- Sith in 1v1 — even mirror matchup.
- Overwhelming ranged fire — BP drain eventually breaks defense.
- Wookiees / Droidekas — ignore pushes or simply tank through.
