# Concussion Rifle

`MB_ATT_CONCUSSION`

A heavy rifle firing concussive blasts.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Primary Damage:** **40** (Pellet damage, multiple pellets)
* **Alt-Fire Damage:** **70** (Alt-Fire Shot)
* **Velocity:** 3500 (Primary) / 1100 (Alt-Fire)
* **Splash Damage:** 80
* **Splash Radius:** 180
* **Ammo Cost:** 10 (Primary) / 20 (Alt-Fire)

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Standard**. Fires a spread of concussive pellets.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Single concussive shot.
* **Level 2**:
 * **Damage**: Increased.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Splash**: Larger splash radius.

### Tips
* Excellent for crowd control and area denial.
* Can knock down multiple enemies.
