# Stun Baton

`WP_STUN_BATON`

A melee weapon that delivers an electric shock.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Damage:** **25** (Standard)
* **Effect:** Mini-stun on hit.
* **Reach:** Short.

### Levels
* **Level 1:**
 * **Damage**: 25.
 * **Pulse**: No shock pulse.
* **Level 2/3:**
 * **Damage**: 25.
 * **Pulse**: Emits a shock pulse effect on hit.

### Tips
* Often used as a "Prison Guard" or "Riot Trooper" weapon.
