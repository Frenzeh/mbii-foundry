# Toxic Taunt

`MB_ATT_ROSHTAUNT`

> Weaponized arrogance — interrupts heals.

## What it does

Special taunt ability that emits a brief debuff aura interrupting nearby enemy heals. Joke/meme attribute named after Rosh Penin's reputation — but mechanically functional as an anti-medic disruptor.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — short-range taunt.
- **Level 2** — medium-range, slightly longer interrupt.
- **Level 3** — long-range; can interrupt big-bacta heals mid-channel.

## Notes

- Categorized as Advanced — meme attribute, sparingly used.
- Pairs surprisingly well with kill-secure plays where the enemy is mid-medpac.
- Cosmetic taunt animation also plays.
