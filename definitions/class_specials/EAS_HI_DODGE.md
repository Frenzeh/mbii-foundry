# 🛡️ Dodge (Special)

`EAS_HI_DODGE`

Activates the Dodge ability manually.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Trigger:** Press Special Key.
*   **Effect:** Drains ammo/stamina to evade all incoming projectile fire for a short duration.
*   **Requirement:** `MB_ATT_DODGE`.

### 💡 Tips
*   Unlike passive Dodge, this forces the state to "Active" immediately.
