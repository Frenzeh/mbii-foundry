# Plasma Thrower

`MB_ATT_THROWER_PLASMA`

> Plasma element variant for the Thrower stream weapon.

## What it does

Configures the Thrower (`WP_THROWER`) to deal plasma damage — high tick damage, no DoT, balanced versus most target types. The "raw damage" element pick.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Standard plasma damage per tick.
- **Level 2** — Higher tick damage; longer effective range.
- **Level 3** — Max plasma damage; widest cone.

## Notes

- Requires `MB_ATT_THROWER` for the base weapon.
- Element-swap variants: `_LIGHTNING`, `_ICE`, `_POISON`, `_FLAME`.
- Best general-purpose element pick.
