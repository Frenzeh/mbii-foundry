# T-21 Heavy Blaster

`WP_T21`

> Heavy repeating blaster. Massive single-shot primary; lighter secondary spam.

## What it does

The T-21 is a heavy support weapon — primary delivers a single 78-damage hard-hitting bolt at 1250ms cadence (1000ms at level 4), while secondary fires a faster 200ms cadence at 34 damage. The user moves slowly while firing, so positioning matters as much as aim. Mode-cycle weapon with high force-drain values.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 78
- **Velocity** — 5600 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 8
- **Fire rate** — 1250ms (1000ms at level 4)

## Secondary fire

- **Damage** — 34
- **Velocity** — 5100 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 3
- **Fire rate** — 200ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_T21`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 26/18 primary, 11/8 secondary; blocking 18/14 / 9/6.
- Slows movement while firing — a true heavy weapon.
- Heavy Trooper, Gonk Heavy, and Imperial Heavy Soldier kits.
