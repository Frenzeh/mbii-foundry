# 🔫 E-11 Blaster

`MB_ATT_BLASTER`

The standard **Imperial E-11 Blaster Rifle**. Versatile and effective at medium range.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Primary Damage:** **36**
*   **Secondary Damage:** **31**
*   **Velocity:** 6325 (Primary) / 4600 (Secondary)
*   **Ammo Cost:** 2 (Primary) / 3 (Secondary)

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Standard**. Decent rate of fire.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: Rapid-fire spray (inaccurate).

*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Improved**. Tighter spread. Higher rate of fire.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: More controlled rapid-fire.

*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Elite**. High accuracy while moving.
    *   **Alt-Fire**: 🎯 **Sniper Mode**. Zooms in for high-precision shots.
        *   **Velocity**: 9825 (Sniper).
        *   **Damage**: High.

### 💡 Tips
*   The Level 3 Sniper Mode makes this weapon very deadly at long range.
*   A staple for Stormtrooper Commanders.
