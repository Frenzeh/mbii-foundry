# Environmental Protection

`MB_ATT_ENV_PROT`

> Resists environmental and elemental damage not covered by other armors.

## What it does

Reduces damage from environmental sources — drowning, lava, vacuum, acid pools — and elemental DoTs (fire, poison, electric) that aren't already mitigated by `MB_ATT_BLAST_ARMOUR`, `MB_ATT_MAGNETIC_PLATING`, or `MB_ATT_CORTOSIS`.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — partial resistance to elemental DoTs.
- **Level 2** — strong resistance to most environment damage.
- **Level 3** — near-immunity to lava, drowning, and elemental sources.

## Notes

- Distinct from blast/magnetic/cortosis — those handle their specific damage flags. This is the "everything else" resistance.
- Useful on map-specific classes (vacuum spacewalkers, lava troopers).
- Does not affect direct weapon damage.
