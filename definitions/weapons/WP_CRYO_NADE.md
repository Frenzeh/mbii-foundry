# Cryoban Grenade

`WP_CRYO_NADE`

> Freezing grenade. Slows or briefly freezes targets in the blast zone.

## What it does

A timed-cook grenade that, instead of high damage, drops a brief area-of-effect that slows or freezes hit targets. Useful for setting up follow-up shots or denying routes when paired with a teammate's heavier weapon.

## Primary fire

- **Mode** — Throw (timed cook)
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Lobbed throw
- **Fire rate** — 1000ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_CRYOBAN_GRENADES`.
- Crowd-control role — chains well with frag/thermal follow-ups.
