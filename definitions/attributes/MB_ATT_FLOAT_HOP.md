# Float Hop

`MB_ATT_FLOAT_HOP`

> Slow descent — anti-gravity stabilizers.

## What it does

Reduces fall speed by adding upward thrust during descent. Simulates hovering or jet-pack stabilization without committing to full flight. Lets the user "float" off ledges and across gaps.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — slight slow-fall.
- **Level 2** — half-speed descent.
- **Level 3** — near-glide; can cross long gaps from a single jump.

## Notes

- Categorized as Advanced — movement-tech.
- Pairs with `MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOP` and `MB_ATT_BUNNY_HOP`.
- Distinct from `MB_ATT_JETPACK` (full flight); this is a passive descent modifier.
