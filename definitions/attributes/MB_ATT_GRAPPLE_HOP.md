# Grapple Hop

`MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOP`

> Chain grapple-jumps without ground contact.

## What it does

Lets the user fire the grapple hook (`MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOOK`) again while still mid-air, swinging Spider-Man style without needing to land between shots.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — one mid-air re-grapple.
- **Level 2** — multiple chained grapples.
- **Level 3** — unlimited chain (only fuel-limited).

## Notes

- Categorized as Advanced — movement-tech, rarely picked directly.
- Requires the underlying Grapple Hook attribute to function.
- Pairs with `MB_ATT_FLOAT_HOP` and `MB_ATT_BUNNY_HOP` for high-mobility builds.
