# Cripples

`HELD_CRIPPLE`

> Brief slow-and-stagger debuff on hit.

## What it does

Combines reduced movement speed with a stagger — target both moves slower and has their action timing disrupted for a short window.

## Notes

- Added in R22.0.00.
- Stronger than `HELD_SLOW` alone; weaker than full `HELD_STUN`.
- Effective vs. melee/saber pursuers.
