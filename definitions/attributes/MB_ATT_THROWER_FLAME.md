# Flame Thrower (Element)

`MB_ATT_THROWER_FLAME`

> Flame element variant for the Thrower stream weapon.

## What it does

Configures the Thrower (`WP_THROWER`) to deal fire damage — initial hit plus burning damage-over-time on the target. Leaves brief burning patches at high rank.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Standard fire damage; brief DoT.
- **Level 2** — Longer burn duration; small ground patch on heavy hits.
- **Level 3** — Max burn and patch persistence.

## Notes

- Requires `MB_ATT_THROWER` for the base weapon.
- Element-swap variants: `_LIGHTNING`, `_ICE`, `_POISON`, `_PLASMA`.
- See also `MB_ATT_FLAMETHROWER` (separate broader flame attribute).
