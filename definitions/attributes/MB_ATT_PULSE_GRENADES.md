# ⚡ Pulse Grenade

`MB_ATT_PULSE_GRENADES`

EMP grenade, designed to disable electronics and drain energy.

> **Default:** None.

### 📊 Stats
*   **Direct Hit Damage:** **75** (to organics, estimate)
*   **Splash Damage:** **60** (estimate)
*   **Splash Radius:** **300** units
*   **Fuse Time:** ~3 seconds.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Level 1**:
    *   **Single Grenade**. Drains droid battery / energy shields.
*   **Level 2**:
    *   **Two Grenades**.
*   **Level 3**:
    *   **Three Grenades**.

### 💡 Tips
*   Highly effective against droids and Jedi (drains FP).
*   Can cause targets to drop weapons if their energy is drained.
