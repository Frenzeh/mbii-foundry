# Fuel Regen

`MB_ATT_FUELREGEN`

> Rate at which jetpack/flamethrower fuel refills.

## What it does

Controls how fast the `RESOURCE_FUEL` pool regenerates. Critical for Mandalorian and Jet Trooper play — better Fuel Regen means less downtime between flights.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — slow refill (long downtime between bursts).
- **Level 2** — medium refill.
- **Level 3** — fast refill (near-continuous flight on small bursts).

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_FUEL` (pool size).
- Regen happens when grounded; airborne regen depends on class config.
- Stacks with `MB_ATT_RESOURCE_REGEN_*` family.
