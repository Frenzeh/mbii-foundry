# Icethrower

`CFL_ICETHROWER`

Converts the Flamethrower into a Cryo-projector.

### Mechanics
* **Weapon:** `MB_ATT_FLAMETHROWER` / `WP_THROWER`.
* **Visual:** Blue "flame".
* **Effect:** **Freezes** enemies on contact (Stun/Slow) instead of burning.

### Usage
* Carbonite Troopers or specialized Droids.
