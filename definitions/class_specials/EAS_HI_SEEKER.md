# Seeker Drone

`EAS_HI_SEEKER`

> Deploys a Seeker Drone without using the inventory slot.

## What it does

Spawns a friendly Seeker drone that orbits the player and shoots nearby enemies for a short window. Same drone as the holdable, but accessible from a special-key bind so the player doesn't have to scroll inventory.

## Notes

- Inventory-shared: each activation costs one Seeker charge from `HI_SEEKER`.
- Drone HP and dwell time come from the holdable definition, not this binding.
- Drone is destroyable by enemy fire; do not rely on it as primary DPS.
