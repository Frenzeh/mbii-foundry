# VelocityMod

`VelocityMod 0.85`

> Multiplier on projectile speed.

## What it does

Scales the velocity of fired projectiles. `0.85` = projectiles travel at 85% speed; `1.5` = projectiles 50% faster.

## Valid values

Float, typical range 0.25..2.0.

## Notes

- Has no effect on hitscan weapons (lasers, hitscan rifles).
- Slower projectiles are easier to dodge / Push; faster projectiles harder to predict.
- Useful balance lever for over-tuned weapons that don't need a damage cut.
