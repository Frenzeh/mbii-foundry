# Assault Sentry

`HI_SENTRY_GUN`

> Deployable automated turret.

## What it does

Drops a tripod-mounted turret at the user's feet. The Sentry auto-targets enemies in LoS within range and continues firing until destroyed or its ammo runs out. Friendly-fire safe.

## Notes

- HP, damage, and ammo budget come from `MB_ATT_SENTRY` rank.
- Place around corners and chokes — the Sentry has no awareness of cover.
- Destructible by any weapon; treats explosives as critical damage.
