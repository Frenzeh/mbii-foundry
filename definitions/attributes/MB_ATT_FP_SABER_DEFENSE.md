# Saber Defense

`MB_ATT_FP_SABER_DEFENSE`

> Attribute gating parry quality and BP regen.

## What it does

Passive. Sets how much BP regenerates per tick, how much damage the user's saber blocks take, and the cost of jump/special techniques during parry.

## Per level

- **Rank 0** — 1.6x incoming BP damage, no regen
- **Level 1** — 1.4x incoming, low regen (3 BP per tick) · *cost 2*
- **Level 2** — 1.0x incoming, normal regen (4 BP per tick) · *cost 1*
- **Level 3** — 1.0x incoming, normal regen, halved special costs, reduced jump drain · *cost 0*

## Notes

- BP regen ticks every 400ms.
- Rank 3 is standard for any duelist; Rank 1 is "Padawan" and will lose stamina wars fast.
- Does not affect damage taken from gunners — saber-vs-saber only.
