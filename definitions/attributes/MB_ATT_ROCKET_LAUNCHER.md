# Rocket Launcher

`MB_ATT_ROCKET_LAUNCHER`

Merr-Sonn Missile System. Fires unguided rockets.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Direct Hit Damage:** **250**
* **Splash Damage:** **200**
* **Splash Radius:** **200** units
* **Velocity:** 1610 (Slow)
* **Ammo Cost:** 1
* **Clip Size:** 1 (Single Shot)
* **Reload Time:** ~2.5 seconds (inferred from PLX1)

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Standard**. Unguided rocket.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Guided mode (requires manual steering).
* **Level 2**:
 * **Blast Radius**: Increased.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Damage**: Increased.

### Tips
* **Anti-Vehicle:** Excellent against vehicles and droids.
* **Area Denial:** Effective for flushing enemies out of cover.
* **Danger:** High risk of self-damage in close quarters.
