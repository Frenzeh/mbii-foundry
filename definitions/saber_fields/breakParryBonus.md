# Break Parry Bonus

`breakParryBonus`

> Increases the chance to smash through an opponent's parry/block.

## What it does

Increases the chance to smash through an opponent's parry/block.

**Default:** 0

## Valid values

- **Value:** Integer.
- **Effect:** Increases BP damage dealt to a blocking opponent.

## Notes

- Essential for "Guard Breaker" builds.
