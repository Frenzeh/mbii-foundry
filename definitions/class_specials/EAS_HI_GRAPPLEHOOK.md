# Grapple Hook

`EAS_HI_GRAPPLEHOOK`

> Fires the wrist grapple hook.

## What it does

Launches the grapple line. On impact, reels the player toward the hook anchor for fast vertical traversal. Movement-tech only — does no direct damage.

## Notes

- Stock kit requires Melee weapon active to swing — the line attaches to the off-hand wrist.
- Range and reel speed are configured by the underlying `MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOOK` attribute.
- Pairs with `MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOP` for chained grapple-jumps without ground contact.
