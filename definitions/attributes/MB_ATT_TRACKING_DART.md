# Tracking Dart

`MB_ATT_TRACKING_DART`

Launcher for tracking beacons that tag enemies for the entire team.

> **Default:** 0.

### Levels

* **Level 1**: **3 Darts**. Tag lasts **10s**. Visible through walls.
* **Level 2**: **6 Darts**. Tag lasts **20s**.
* **Level 3**: **9 Darts**. Tag lasts **30s**. Splash radius can tag Jedi even if they block the dart itself.

### Tips
* Tagged enemies have a **green triangle** over their head.
* Wall-hacks for the whole team! Invaluable for coordination.
