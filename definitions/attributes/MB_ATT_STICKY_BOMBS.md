# Sticky Bombs (Ammo)

`MB_ATT_STICKY_BOMBS`

> Sticky-bomb ammunition pool for the MGL family.

## What it does

Increases the sticky-bomb ammo capacity used by `WP_MGL` and its variants. Base capacity is 15 (`AMMO_STICKY_BOMBS`); higher ranks raise the pool and reload speed.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Standard capacity (15).
- **Level 2** — Expanded capacity; faster reload.
- **Level 3** — Maximum capacity and reload speed.

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_MGL` (and `MGL_IMPACT` / `MGL_BURST` variants).
- Drives the `AMMO_STICKY_BOMBS` pool.
- FRENZY_STICKY feature line.
