# Heavy Pistol

`WP_HEAVY_PISTOL`

> High-caliber Imperial sidearm. Hits hard but eats energy fast.

## What it does

The Heavy Pistol is a high-damage sidearm built around hard-hitting, slow-cycle shots. Primary delivers 55 damage at 600ms, secondary an even slower 900ms heavier round. Energy use is significant (5 primary / 30 secondary) — you reload often relative to the standard pistol.

## Primary fire

- **Damage** — 55
- **Velocity** — 4000 units/sec
- **Ammo cost** — 5
- **Fire rate** — 600ms

## Secondary fire

- **Mode** — Heavy aimed shot
- **Ammo cost** — 30
- **Fire rate** — 900ms

## Notes

- Pairs with `MB_ATT_HEAVY_PISTOL`.
- Force drain (Close/Far): 18/13 both modes; blocking 14/10.
- Imperial Officer / Sith Cultist signature sidearm.
