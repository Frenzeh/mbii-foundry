# UGL Burst (Mixed)

`MB_ATT_UGL_BURST_MIXED`

> Mixed-mode burst variant of the UGL. Combines burst-fire with mixed grenade fuses.

## What it does

Modifies `WP_UGL` to fire a mixed-fuse burst — combining timed and impact rounds in the same volley for layered area control. The most complex UGL variant; useful for unpredictable area-denial patterns.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Mixed 2-grenade burst (1 timed, 1 impact).
- **Level 2** — 3-grenade mixed burst.
- **Level 3** — Maximum burst with optimized mixed-fuse pattern.

## Notes

- Requires `MB_ATT_UGL` for the base launcher.
- Variants: `MB_ATT_UGL_BURST` (pure burst), `MB_ATT_UGL_IMPACT` (pure impact).
- Best vs mixed groups in unpredictable layouts.
