# EE-4 Carbine

`WP_EE4`

> Short range burst blaster.

## Stats

- **Damage** — **36** (Primary) / **70** (Sniper)
- **Velocity** — 6325 (Primary) / 9825 (Sniper)
- **Ammo Cost** — 26 (Sniper)
- **Force Drain (Close/Far)** — **13/8** (Primary), **43/29** (Sniper)
- **Force Drain Blocking (Close/Far)** — **12/6** (Primary), **36/24** (Sniper)

## Tips

- Paired with `MB_ATT_EE4`.
- Can be toggled to Sniper mode at Level 3.
