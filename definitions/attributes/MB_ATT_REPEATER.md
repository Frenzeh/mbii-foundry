# Imperial Heavy Repeater

`MB_ATT_REPEATER`

The standard heavy repeating blaster rifle for Imperial forces. Effective for suppression.

> **Default:** None.

### Stats
* **Damage:** **26** (Primary)
* **Alt-Fire Damage:** **40** (Explosive Blob)
* **Alt-Fire Splash Damage:** **26**
* **Alt-Fire Splash Radius:** **128** units.
* **Primary Rate of Fire:** 100ms per shot (Extremely fast).
* **Velocity:** 2600
* **Ammo Cost:** 1 (Primary) / 10 (Alt-Fire)
* **Clip Size:** 150 (Primary) / 10 (Alt-Fire).

### Levels

* **Level 1**:
 * **Fire Rate**: High.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Concussion Grenade Launcher (slow fire).
* **Level 2**:
 * **Accuracy**: Improved.
* **Level 3**:
 * **Recoil**: Reduced.
 * **Alt-Fire**: Faster reload/fire rate for concussion rounds.

### Tips
* Excellent for **suppression** and crowd control.
* The Alt-Fire is good for dislodging enemies from cover.
