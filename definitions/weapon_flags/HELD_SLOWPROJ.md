# Slow Projectile

`HELD_SLOWPROJ`

Projectiles move at a fraction of their normal speed.

### Mechanics
* **Speed:** **0.25x** (25% of normal speed).
* **Dodgeable:** Makes shots easy to side-step at range.

### Tips
* Use for "Energy Ball" or "Plasma Bolt" weapons that hit hard (`HELD_HIGHDAMAGE`) but are slow moving (Skill shots).
