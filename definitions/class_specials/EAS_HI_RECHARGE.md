# Recharge

`EAS_HI_RECHARGE`

> Boosts battery/ammo regen for a short window.

## What it does

SBD-flavored special that briefly accelerates the user's resource regen — primarily Battery for Super Battle Droids. Lets the SBD top off after a sustained burst without retreating to a dispenser.

## Notes

- 2000 ms cooldown per activation (no resource cost in the EAS table — the cost is opportunity time spent in the animation).
- Effect duration and regen multiplier scale with the underlying `MB_ATT_RECHARGE` attribute.
- SBD binds this to special1 in the stock kit; not exposed to other classes.
