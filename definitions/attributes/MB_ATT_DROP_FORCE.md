# Resource Drop

`MB_ATT_DROP_FORCE`

> Drops a one-shot resource pickup on the ground.

## What it does

Spawns a Force/Resource pickup at the user's feet. Restores whichever class resource the recipient uses.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — small resource per pickup.
- **Level 2** — medium resource per pickup.
- **Level 3** — large resource per pickup.

## Notes

- Sibling of `MB_ATT_DISP_FORCE` and `MB_ATT_STIM_FORCE`.
- Charge count tied to `MB_ATT_SUPPLYDROP`.
