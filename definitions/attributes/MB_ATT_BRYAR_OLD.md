# Bryar Pistol (Classic)

`MB_ATT_BRYAR_OLD`

> Classic-style Bryar pistol attribute. Single-shot reliable sidearm.

## What it does

Grants `WP_BRYAR_OLD` — the classic Jedi Knight series Bryar pistol. Functions like the standard pistol but uses the Westar ammo pool. Reliable mid-damage single shots; charged secondary delivers heavier rounds at level 2+.

## Per level

- **Level 1** — Basic single-shot pistol; standard pistol-tier damage.
- **Level 2** — Charged secondary unlocked; mid-tier damage on hold.
- **Level 3** — Maximum damage and charge speed.

## Notes

- Unlocks `WP_BRYAR_OLD`.
- Shares the Westar ammo pool (`AMMO_WESTAR`).
- Used on classic-era characters and select Hero loadouts.
