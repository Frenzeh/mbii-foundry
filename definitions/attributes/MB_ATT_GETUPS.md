# 🤸 Getup Ranks

`MB_ATT_GETUPS`

Determines the speed and animation of recovery from knockdowns.

> **Default:** Varies by class.

### 📊 Levels

*   **Rank 0:** Standard slow getup.
*   **Rank 1:** Faster getup animation.
*   **Rank 2:** Near-instant recovery.
*   **Rank 3:** Allows shooting/melee immediately after recovery.

### 💡 Tips
*   Crucial for aggressive classes that are often in the thick of combat.
*   Combines with `MB_ATT_KNOCKDOWN_ROLL` for maximum recovery speed.
