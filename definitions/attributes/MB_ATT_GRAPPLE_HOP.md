# 🤸 Grapple Hop

`MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOP`

Momentum boost for grappling hooks.

### ⚙️ Mechanics
*   **Effect:** The first jump after detaching a grappling hook retains momentum (Bunny Hop).
*   **Requirement:** Must have `MB_ATT_GRAPPLE_HOOK` and `EAS_HI_GRAPPLEHOOK`.

### 💡 Tips
*   Allows for "Spiderman" swinging maneuvers.
